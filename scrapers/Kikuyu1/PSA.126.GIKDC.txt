Kũhooya Ũhonokio
1Rĩrĩa MWATHANI aatũcookirie Jerusalemu,
tuonaga ta arĩ kũroota twarootaga!
2Hĩndĩ ĩyo tũgĩtheka
na tũkĩina nyĩmbo tũkeneete,
nacio ndũũrĩrĩ ikĩĩrana atĩrĩ,
“MWATHANI nĩekĩĩte andũ aake maũndũ manene!”
3MWATHANI nĩatwĩkĩire maũndũ manene;
kaĩ nĩtũkeneete-ĩ!
4Tũma tũgaacĩre o rĩngĩ, MWATHANI,
o ta ũrĩa mbura ĩtũmaga njũũĩ iria ihũĩte ciyũre rĩngĩ.
5Arĩa mahandaga makĩrĩraga,
marogetha makĩrũũhagia nĩgũkena.
6Arĩa moimagaraga makĩrĩraga makuuĩĩte mbegũ cia kũhanda,
makaahũndũka makĩrũũhagia nĩgũkena,
makuuĩĩte magetha maao.

1
Aso eoŋ loarai ekimat aupakus ka ekajenan da loka aisiriebio naka Kristo, ekamoran da loka aibuses naekoto apukorio, ecori imakio kotomakus: 2
kicokienenete esipan loka Edeke lokotomakus, mere kanuka aitiŋaikinio, dimarai kanuka alosikinit, mere kanuka ameda nakileic, dimarai koipud, 3
mere kwape lueyaito kagogoŋ nukibeito, konye koraut aanyuneta nuka esipan. 4
Kosodi naetakanunia Ekecokon lokapolon, idumunete aboko nakaibuses namam erinyori. 5
Yes da ikatumunak kwape kwaŋin, kononokikis imakio. Konapito yesi kere aitasonikino bonik ka bonik, naarai “itepegi Edeke luiwoŋoko, konye einakini luitasonos asianut.”
6
Aso kitasonikinos kokwap kakan nailerit naka Edeke, tetere kitopoloori yes kedol apak. 7
Kowoikis ŋes aiyalononokus kere, naarai epod ŋesi yes. 8
Kinyeis amer, kokwenyenenete. Lokasurupikus Satan irimirimi kwape eŋatuny loegat, emoot yenenyamari. 9
Kitepegata ŋes, kicasito kaiyuun, kojenete ebe itodolitoi aisiriebio ŋipeŋin koinacanakus luejaas akwapin. 10
Konye Edeke lokasianut kere, lokinyarakinit yes aibusesuke naka ikar kere toma Kristo, kidaut aisiriebio apak naedit, kitetemikini yes, kiticat, ka aitogogoŋ da. 11Kojai ayait mamakeŋ ikar ka ikar. Amen.
12
Kejukakinit yes ebalua lo kakan naka Siluano, loaomit eoŋ araut onace lomunot, kewadikakinit kakiro nuikidioko, kecor ka aitejenikin noi ebe ŋin ŋes asianut obe naka Edeke, kobwaata yesi kotomakeŋ. 13
Kiyogai yes ŋin naejai Babulon, naseuna aupakus, ka Marako da okokuka. 14
Kiyoganakinos kainu nakamin.
Ainapakin kojai mamakus kere luijaas toma Kristo.

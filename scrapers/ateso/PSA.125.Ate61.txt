Ekosio loka Adokanar.
1Ŋul luemunos EJAKAIT esubito bala emoru Sion,
Lomam epedorio aijanar, konye ebwoi ikar kere.
2Kwape irimoritotor iroŋan Yerusalem,
Irimorit kwaŋin EJAKAIT ikeruna,
Amear lolo aitodol ikar kere.
3Naarai ebela loka aronis mam esalakini airumet naka ikadolitok;
Tetere mam ikadolitok eriorete akecekanin mama ejaas nwnam adolit.
4Wu EJAKAIT, kikonak ejok ŋul luejok,
Ka ŋul da luitenara kotauonokec.
5
Konye ŋul luepaarete orotinikec lumagul,
Itolomari EJAKAIT kes nepepe kikeswamak numam adolit. Kinapakin Isirael.

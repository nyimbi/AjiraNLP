Ekosio loka Adokanar; loka Solomon.
1Dimikito keduk EJAKAIT etogo,
Itaaritos atai luedukete:
Dimikito kidar EJAKAIT ere,
Ekwenyi lokedaran atai.
2
Idilokinete yesi akwenyun atai, ka aitumor aiyeŋun,
Ainyam da atap naka aitapasitoi:
Naarai einakini kwaŋin iminanake ajo.
3
Koany, idwe kes airumet naka EJAKAIT:
Aur da naka akoik ŋes ainakineteke.
4Kwape kimalia kakan naka ekagogoŋon,
Esubito kwaŋin idwe luka atumun.
5Iyalama etuŋanan loilelebit kes ocoeke:
Emameete ileic,
Naineratar kalukasurupokec orute.

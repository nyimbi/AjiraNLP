1
Akaulo kasabato, napecila kide kapaaran nasodit nakasabato, koloto Mariam Magadalene ka Mariam ace aanyun ates. 2Ido koany, kejai amaran naka akwap; naarai kodok emalaika loka Ejakait kokuju, kobu kikokoro amoru awai, kiboikin kujuke. 3
Kotakanu bala eimilia, ikenapen da kiliacanito kiac. 4Komaranata ikedarak kanuka akurianar ŋes, kosubata bala lukatwak, 5Koboŋok emalaika kotema aŋor atiar, “Sirikikuriakaros yesi; naarai ajeni ebe imootos Yesu lokibubukinitoi. 6
Emamei kane; naarai ajaru, kwape eneritor. Kopotu koanyut aibois naeperikinitoi. 7
Koloto yesi da atipet, kolimokis ikekesisiak ebe, ‘Ajaru kotoma okatwak’; ido koany, kiŋarenikini yes alosit Galilaya; ianyikinete ŋes ŋina; koany kadau alimokin yes.”
8Konyout atipet kates kakurian ka aiyalama naepol, kokerata alimokin ikekesisiak. 9Ido koany, kiriamakin Yesu kakes, kobala ebe, “Yoga!” Kikunyakis kitiŋata akekejen komaata ŋes. 10
Kosodi Yesu kotema kes ariar, “Sirikikuriakaros; koloto kolimokis ikainacan koloto Galilaya, ŋina kaanyikinete.”
11Napelosete, koany, kolomasi ice kokedarak ore, kolimokis isasedotin lupeti akiro nukedoikinete, 12Kotukokinos nepepe kimakio kinerut, koinakis iserikalei arupiyan nuipu, 13
kobalas ebe, “Kinerata yesi ebe, ‘Apotu ikekesisiak kopotu kuare, kokokwasi ŋes kijotoi isio.’ 14Arai kiirar akirot na kama ekayaiton, kimaimai isio ŋes, kinyeis yes aiticanio.” 15Kolemasi arupiyan kiswamata kwape kisisianakinitere; kisiarakin akirot na toma oYudayan kiton lolo.
16
Konye kolot ikesisiak itomon ka lodiopet Galilaya, oroŋat lokicorakinit kes Yesu, 17Nakeanyuniata ŋes, komaata; konye kobuonokinos ice kakesi. 18
Kobu Yesu neejaas kinera kakes, kotema atiar, “Kainakin eoŋ apedor kere kokuju kakwapin da. 19
Aso kolot kituluosi lukatekerin kere ikesisiak, kibatisaete kes kokiror loka Papa, ka loka Okoku, ka loka Emoyo Lokalaunan; 20
kisisianakinete kes aricit akiro kere nukecorakinit yes; ido koany, ajai eoŋ nenipe kayes apaarasia kere, kiton awasia naka ikar kere,”

1   Weche mag Ndalo 

16  .

23  -

33  ) 

1  Weruru wer manyien ni Ruoth Nyasaye, un ji duto mae piny ngima, werneuru. 

2  Weruru ni Ruoth Nyasaye, kendo pakuru nyinge. Pile nyisuru ji kaka osekonyou. 

3  Huluru ni ji duong’ne maler, mondo ji duto owinj timbene madongo. 

4  Ruoth Nyasaye duong’, kendo nyaka pake malich! Adier, nyaka miye duong’ moloyo nyiseche mamoko duto, 

5  nikech nyiseche mag ogendini mamoko duto gin mana gik manono. Ruoth Nyasaye to ochueyo polo. 

6  Duong’ maler gi duong’ mar loch okete diere, kendo teko gi ber duto ni e Hekalu mare. 

7   Un ji duto modak e piny, pakuru Ruoth Nyasaye. Dendeuru nikech duong’ne gi tekone. 

8  Miuru nyinge duong’ mowinjore kode, kendo keluru chiwo e Hekalu mare. 

9  Ka Ng’ama Ler othinyore, to un ji duto manie piny, kulreuru e nyime, kutetni. 

10  Nyisuru ogendini duto niya, “Ruoth Nyasaye e Ruoth!” En ema oseguro piny motegno, ma ok nyal ng’ingni, kendo obiro ng’ado ni ji duto bura kare. 

11  Un polo gi piny, beduru mamor, to in nam, wuo, kaachiel gi gik moko duto manie iyi! 

12  Un pewe, beduru mail, kaachiel gi gik moko duto ma un go, kendo yien duto mae bunge bende mondo okog gi mor e nyim Ruoth Nyasaye, 

13  nikech obiro mondo orit piny Adier, obiro rito ji duto mae piny gi bura makare kod adiera.

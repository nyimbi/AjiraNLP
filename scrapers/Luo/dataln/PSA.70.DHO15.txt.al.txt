1  A Nyasaye, resa! Reti piyo mondo ikonya, yaye Ruoth Nyasaye. 

2  We joma dwaro ketho ngimana wigi okuodi, mi ng’eng’ nono, bende jogo ma giyo ka mor ni ahinyora, onego dwok chien ka wigi okuot. 

3  Ee, joma goyona siboigo onego odhier nono nikech ologi. 

4  To ji duto mabiro iri onego bed mamor kendo mail. Onego ji duto mohero warruok maa kuomi wach pile niya, “Mano kaka Nyasaye duong’!” 

5  An jachan kendo ok anyal yudo kaka akonyora, omiyo yie ibi ira piyo, yaye Nyasaye. In e jakonyna kendo jawarna. A Ruoth Nyasaye, kik ideki!

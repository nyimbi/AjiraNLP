41  ) Ng’at makare gi ng’at ma jaricho 

1  Ng’ama ok oyie gi puonj joma richo, kendo ma ok luw timbe joricho, kata ma ok riwre gi joma ochayo Nyasaye, en jahawi. 

2  Chik Ruoth Nyasaye ema miye mor, kendo opuonjore chikno odiechieng’ gotieno. 

3   Ochalo gi yath madongo e bath aora, manyago olemo e kinde mowinjore kendo ma pote ok ner. Gimoro amora motimo dhi maber. 

4  Joricho to ok chal kamano kata matin, to gichalo mihudhi ma yamo tero. 

5  Kuom mano bura nolo joricho, kendo ok ginibed e kanyakla mar joma kare. 

6  Ruoth Nyasaye rito ng’at makare, jaricho to nolal chuth.

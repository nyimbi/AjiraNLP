14  ) 

1   Joma ofuwo wacho e chunygi niya, “Nyasaye ongee!” Giduto gin joma okethore, kendo gisetimo gik maricho miwuoro. Onge kata ng’at achiel matimo gima ber! 

2  Nyasaye rango ji gie polo, mondo one ane ka nitie joma riek kendo malame. 

3  To giduto giseweye, kendo gisedwanyore mana maromre. Onge kata achiel kuomgi matimo tim makare. 

4  Nyasaye penjo niya, “Joma timbegi richogi onge gi rieko koso? Gichamo joga ka joma chamo kuon, to ok gilama ngang’!” 

5  To kihondko ma pok ginenoe nyaka nene biro makogi, nikech Nyasaye keyo choke joma kwede. Nologi chuth, mi giyud wichkuot, nikech Nyasaye osedagigi. 

6  Mad jo-Israel yud resruok moa Sayun! Nyikwa Jakobo nobed mamor, e kinde ma Ruoth Nyasaye nochak omi joge dhi nyime maber. Ee, jo-Israel nobed moil!

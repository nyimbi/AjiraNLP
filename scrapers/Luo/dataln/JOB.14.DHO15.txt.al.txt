1  Ndalo dhano nok, kendo oneno thagruok mathoth. 

2  Odongo kendo onur piyo, mana ka maua, bende olal nono ka tipo makadho. 

3  A Nyasaye, ng’at ma kamano bende dilokri, mi ing’i? Koso dikela e nyimi mondo iyala? 

4  Onge gima ler ma diwuog kuom gima ochido ka dhano. 

5  Kar romb hike nosechan chon. Ee, kata mana duechene bende ong’ere. In Nyasaye isechane, omiyo ok nyal loke. 

6  Lok wang’i oa kuom dhano mondo iweye. Kata obedo ni ngimane tek kamano, to we one maber kaka onyalo. 

7  Ka osetong’ yath, to nitie geno ni onyalo loth kendo. 

8  Kata obedo ni tiendene top, kendo osikine tho ei lowo, 

9  to kowinjo much pi, to oloth mana ka yien mochako dongo. 

10  Dhano to tho, kendo mano e gikone, to ka osetho, to erego? 

11  Kata aora dipo ka chieng’ moro oduono, kendo kata mana nam bende pige nyalo rumo. 

12  Dhano to kosetho, to ok nyal chier kendo. Ok onyal chiew ka polo pok okethore, kendo gimoro ok nochiewe e nindoneno. 

13  A Nyasaye, mad iyie ipanda e piny joma otho, mondo apond kuno nyaka mirimbi kue. Bang’e iket kinde ma ibiro parae kendo. 

14  Ka ng’ato otho, to bende dochak obed mangima? An to abiro dhil gi thagruokna nyaka kinde maber chopi. 

15  Bang’e iniluonga, to anadwoki, kendo inibed mamor kineno ng’at mane ichueyo. 

16  Eka inirang kamoro amora ma anyono, to ok inidew richona. 

17  Ibiro rucho oko timbena duto mamono. 

18  Kamoro dipo ka kinde obiro ma gode nyalo mukoree, kendo ma lwendni bende imukoe oko. 

19  Pi nyalo loko lwendni, kendo ohula nyalo ywero lowo. Kamano e kaka iketho geno ma dhano nigo! 

20  To mogik, ihewo dhano chuth, kendo itere mabor, mi tho bam wang’e. 

21  Kata bed mana ni yawuote yud duong’, to en ok ong’e, kata ka giyudo wich kuot, to ok one. 

22  Rem mar dende kende ema owinjo, kaachiel gi kuyo mar chunye.”

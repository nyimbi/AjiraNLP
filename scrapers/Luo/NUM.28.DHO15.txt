Misengni mitimo pile
(Wuok 29.38-46)
1Ruoth Nyasaye nowacho ni Musa niya, 2Chik jo-Israel mondo ochiw gik mitimonago misengni miwang’o mondo obed chiemba madum tik mamit e kinde moketi.
3“Bende nyisgi ni misengni miwang’o ni Ruoth Nyasaye e magi: Odiechieng’ kodiechieng’ nyaka chiw nyiimbe ariyo maonge songa ma hikgi achiel, mondo otimgo misango miwang’o pep. 4Nyaim achiel unutimgo misango gokinyi, to machielo unutimgo misango godhiambo mar ang’ich welo. 5Misango mar cham michiwo kodgi to nobed mogo moromo kilo adek moruw gi mo maber moromo lita ariyo. 6(Mano e misango mitimo miwang’o mitimo pile mondo odum mamit ni Ruoth Nyasaye, mane ochak tim e got Sinai.) 7To misango miolo piny mitimego nobed divai moromo lita ariyo kuom im ka im. Nyaka uol divai makechno ei Kama Ler ka misango ni Ruoth Nyasaye. 8Nyaim machielo mutimogo misango godhiambo mar ang’ich welo nyaka chiw gi misango mar cham kod miolo piny maromre gi mago michiwo gokinyi. En misango miwang’o mondo odum tik mamit ni Ruoth Nyasaye.
Misengni mitimo chieng’ sabato, kendo mitimo due ka due
9“Chieng’ sabato to nyaka uchiw nyiimbe ariyo maonge songa ma hikgi achiel achiel mondo otimgo misango. Nyaka chiwe gi misango miolo piny, mitimego kaka chik dwaro. 10Misango miwang’o pep mitimo chieng’ sabato ka sabatono nyaka utim kumedo ewi misango miwang’o pep mitimo pile, kod misango miolo piny mitimego.
11“To chieng’ due achiel mar due ka due nyaka uchiw nyiruedhi ariyo gi im achiel kod nyiimbe abiriyo ma hikgi achiel achiel mondo otimgo misango miwang’o pep. Jamnigo duto nyaka bed maonge songa. 12Misango mar cham michiwo gi nyarwath ka nyarwath nobed mogo moromo kilo apar moruw gi mo, to michiwo gi im nobed moromo kilo abiriyo, 13kendo michiwo gi nyaim ka nyaim, kilo adek. Mano en misango miwang’o pep mondo odum tik mamit ni Ruoth Nyasaye. 14Misango miolo piny mitimo gi nyarwath ka nyarwath nobed divai moromo lita ang’wen, to michiwo gi im nobed lita ariyo, kendo michiwo gi nyaim ka nyaim lita achiel, mano en misango miwang’o pep ma nyaka tim due ka due e higa ngima. 15Bende nyaka uchiw nywok mondo otimnigo Ruoth Nyasaye misango mar golo richo. Mano nyaka utim kumedo ewi misango miolo piny michiwego.
Misengni mitimo e sewni moketi
16
“Nyaka utim Pasaka mar Ruoth Nyasaye due apar gang’wen mar due mokwongo, 17
to due apar gabich unutim sawo, kensdo nyaka ucham mana makati ma ok ketie thowi kuom ndalo abiriyo. 18Chieng’ mokwongo to nyaka ubed gi chokruok mar lemo, kendo ok unuti tijeu mapile chieng’no. 19Nyaka uchiw nyiruedhi ariyo gi im achiel kod nyiimbe abiriyo ma hikgi achiel achiel mondo otimgo misango miwang’o pep, madum tik mamit ni Ruoth Nyasaye. Jamnigo duto nyaka bed maonge songa. 20Misango mar cham michiwo gi nyarwath ka nyarwath nobed mogo moromo kilo apar moruw gi mo, 21to michiwo gi im nobed kilo abiriyo, to michiwo gi nyaim ka nyaim, kilo adek. 22Bende nyaka uchiw nywok mondo otimgo misango mar golo richo, upwodhrugo. 23Mago duto nyaka uchiw kumedo ewi misango miwang’o pep mitimo gokinyi pile. 24Unutim misengnigo pile nyaka chieng’ mar abiriyo. En misango mitimo mondo obed chiemo madum tik mamit ni Ruoth Nyasaye. Nyaka uchiwe kumedo ewi misangoo miwang’o pep mitimo pile, kod misango miolo piny michiwego. 25Chieng’ mar abiriyo bende nyaka ubed gi chokruok maler, kendo ok unuti tijeu mapile.
26
“Chieng’ muchiwoe chamb gwel, ka ukelo ni Ruoth Nyasaye chiwo mar cham manyien e Sap Jumbe, to nyaka ubed gi chokruok maler, kendo ok nuti tijeu mapile. 27Unuchiw nyiruedhi ariyo gi im achiel kod nyiimbe abiriyo ma hikgi achiel achiel mondo otimgo misango miwang’o pep madum mamit ni Ruoth Nyasaye. 28Misango mar cham michiwo gi nyarwath ka nyarwath nobed mogo moromo kilo apar moruw gi mo, to michiwo gi im nobed kilo abiriyo, 29kendo michiwo gi nyaim ka nyaim, kilo adek. 30Bende nyaka uchiw nywok mondo otimgo misango mar golo richo, upwodhrugo. 31Jamnigo duto nyaka bed maonge songa, kendo misengnigoo duto nyaka utim kumedo ewi misengni miwang’o pep mitimo pile, kaachiel gi misango miolo piny michiwogigo.

Nathan okwero Daudi
1
Ruoth Nyasaye nooro Nathan ir Daudi, kendo ka nobiro ire, nokone niya, “Ji ariyo nodak e dala moro, achiel ne japith, to machielo ne jachan. 2Japith ne nigi jamni gi dhok mogundho, 3to jachan ne onge gi gimoro, mak mana nyarombo achiel kende, ma noseng’iewo. Nopidhe kendo nodongo e ode kaachiel gi nyithinde, kochamo mana chiembe. Nometho e kikombene, kendo nonindo e kore, mobedone kaka nyare. 4Chieng’ moro ng’ato nobworo e dala japithno, to japithno ne ok oyie kawo chiayo moro kuom jambe owuon mondo oyang’ ni wendono, to nokawo mana nyarombo ng’at ma jachan cha, ema noyang’o ni wendone.” 5Kane Daudi owinjo wachno, mirima nomake ahinya gi japithno. Nowacho ni Nathan niya, “Gi nying Ruoth Nyasaye, ng’at mosetimo kamano owinjore gi tho! 6Onego ochul nyading’wen kar nyarombono, nikech notimo kamano koonge gi chunye.”
7Eka Nathan nowacho ni Daudi niya, “In e ng’atno! Ruoth Nyasaye ma Nyasach Israel owacho kama: ‘Ne ayieri mondo ibed ruodh Israel, kendo naresi e lwet Saulo. 8Ne amiyi od ruodhi, kod monde bende obed magi, kendo namiyi dhood Israel gi dhood Juda. Kata da ni mano pod tin, to da amedi gik moko moloyo mago. 9Marang’o nichayo wach Ruoth Nyasaye, mitimo gima ok long’one? Isenego Uria ja-Hiti kuom golo chik otho e lwet jo-Amon, kendo isekawo chiege mi obedo chiegi. 10Emomiyo koro lweny ok norum e dhoodi, nikech isechaya, kendo isekawo chi Uria ja-Hiti obedo chiegi.’
11
“Ruoth Nyasaye omedo wacho niya, ‘Abiro keloni thagruok mowuok mana e odi iwuon. Nakaw mondi, kendo namigi ng’at moro mana kineno, mi noterre kodgi e lela, godiechieng’. 12Gima nitimo, nitimo ling’ling’, an to anatim gini e nyim jo-Isarel duto, odiechieng’ alanga.’ ”
13Daudi nowacho ni Nathan niya, “Aseketho e nyim Ruoth Nyasaye.” Eka Nathan nomedo wachone niya, “Ruoth Nyasaye bende oseweyoni richoni; koro ok nitho. 14To kata kamano nyathi monywolni biro tho, nikech isechayo Ruoth Nyasaye.” 15Nathan nodok e dalane. Bang’e Ruoth Nyasaye nomiyo tuo ogoyo nyathi mane chi Uria onywolo ni Daudi, mi nohewore ahinya. 16Daudi nosayo Nyasaye mondo kik nyathi tho, koriyo kech, kendo konindo piny e lowo otieno duto. 17Jodong ode ne biro, kendo chung’ bute, ka dwaro chunge oa e lowo. To notamore kendo ne ok oyie mondo ochiem kodgi. 18Chieng’ mar abiriyo, nyathi notho, to jodong od Daudi noluoro nyise ni nyathi osetho. Ne giwacho niya, “Kane nyathi pod ngima, ne wawuoyo gi ruoth, to ne ok owinjwa. To koro obiro timore nadi ka wanyise ni nyathi osetho?” 19To kane Daudi oneno jotichne ka kuodho wach kendgi, nofwenyoo ni kara nyathi osetho. Omiyo nopenjogi niya, “Kara nyathi osetho?” Ne gidwoke ni, “Ee, osetho.”
20To Daudi noa malo mi olwokore, kendo owirore gi mo, moloko lepe. Eka nodonjo e Od Ruoth Nyasaye, molemo. Bang’e nodok e ode, mokwayo mondo okelne chiemo. 21Jotichne nopenje niya, “En ang’o mitimoni? Kane nyathi pod ngima, nitueyo chiemo, kendo niywak. To ka koro nyathi osetho, to ia malo, michiemo!” 22Nodwokogi niya, “Kane nyathi pod ngima, natueyo chiemo kaywak, nikech naparo niya, ‘Ng’ama ong’eyo kata ka Ruoth Nyasaye biro ng’wonona mondo nyathi obed mangima?’ 23To koro osetho, ere gima datuene chiemo? Anyalo duoge koso? An ema nadhi ire, to en ok noduog ira.”
24Daudi nohoyo Bathsheba chiege, modhi ire, kendo noriwore kode, mi Bathsheba nonywolo wuowi, mochako ni Solomon. Ruoth Nyasaye nohero nyathino. 25Kuom mano, nomiyo Nathan janabi owacho ni mondo ochake ni Jedidia, nikech mano e dwaro mar Ruoth Nyasaye. Tiend nying’no en ni, ‘Ng’ama ohero Ruoth Nyasaye’.
Daudi okawo Raba, dala jo-Amon
(1 Weche mag Ndalo 20.1-3)
26Joab nokedo gi Raba, dala jo-Amon, mokawo Yap Ruoth. 27Eka nooro joote ir Daudi, kowacho niya, “Asekedo gi Raba, kendo asekawo Yap Ruoth. 28Omiyo koro chok joma odong’ duto, ibi ilwor dalano, kendo ikaw, nikech ka ok itimo kamano to dipo ka an ema akawe, mi ji diluonge gi nyinga.” 29Kuom mano Daudi nochoko oganda lweny duto, modhi Raba. Nokedo gi dalano, mokawo. 30Nogolo osimbo ewi Milkom ma nyasach jo-Amon, moyudo ni pek mar dhahabu molosego oromo kilo piero ang’wen, kendo kidi ma nengone tek ochom kuome, mi nosidho osimbono e wiye owuon. Noyako bende gik mathoth e dalano, 31kendo nodaro joma nodakie, moketogi e tich ng’ado bao, kod kunyo kite, gi tong’o yien, kod wang’o matafari. Kamano e kaka Daudi notimo e miech jo-Amon duto. Bang’e nodok Jerusalem gi jogo duto.

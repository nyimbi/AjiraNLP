Jawmiraawo woni laamiiɗo duumiiɗo
1Jawmiraawo woni laamiiɗo.
O ɗon ɓorni maŋngu.
Jawmiraawo ɓornake ngu, ɗon moji baawɗe.
Duniyaaru tabiti, dimmbataako.
2Leeso laamu ma ɗon tabiti diga fuɗɗam,
diga ɓooyma, a ɗon.
3Maaje mawɗe ɓaŋgti sawtu, yaa Jawmiraawo,
ɗe ɓaŋgti sawtu, ɗe kooli.
4Ammaa maŋngu Jawmiraawo Ɓaŋiiɗo
ɓuri sawtu ndiyamji ɗuuɗɗi
e hoolo ƴuuge mbeela ƴaggiiɗe.
5Yaa Jawmiraawo, seedamkuuji ma ɗi koolniiɗi masin,
senaare fottani suudu ma haa foroy.

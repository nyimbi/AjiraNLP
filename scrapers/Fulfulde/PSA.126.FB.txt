Seyo dahaaɓe
1Yimre jahaaŋgal haa Urusaliima.
Saa'i Jawmiraawo wartiri en haa Siyona diga jeyaaŋgu,
en laati bana hoyɗuɓe,
2en ɗuɗɗini jaleeɗe, en ngimi bee seyo.
Nder ummaatooje goɗɗe, yimɓe mbi'i:
“Jawmiraawo huuwani ɓe kuuɗe mawɗe!”
3Fakat, Jawmiraawo huuwani en kuuɗe mawɗe,
enen boo en ceyi.
4Yaa Jawmiraawo, wartir yimɓe amin diga jeyaaŋgu,
bana maaje haa ladde hamaadaare Lesdi Fombina.
5Aawanɓe bee gonɗi
codan bee welwelo.
6Ɓe ngurti bee bojji, ɓe aawi aawdi.
Ammaa fakat ɓe ngartan bee seyo, ɓe kooran ƴaamle maɓɓe.

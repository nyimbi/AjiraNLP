1  Yimre jahaaŋgal haa Urusaliima. Saa'i Jawmiraawo wartiri en haa Siyona diga jeyaaŋgu, en laati bana hoyɗuɓe, 

2  en ɗuɗɗini jaleeɗe, en ngimi bee seyo. Nder ummaatooje goɗɗe, yimɓe mbi'i: “Jawmiraawo huuwani ɓe kuuɗe mawɗe!” 

3  Fakat, Jawmiraawo huuwani en kuuɗe mawɗe, enen boo en ceyi. 

4  Yaa Jawmiraawo, wartir yimɓe amin diga jeyaaŋgu, bana maaje haa ladde hamaadaare Lesdi Fombina. 

5  Aawanɓe bee gonɗi codan bee welwelo. 

6  Ɓe ngurti bee bojji, ɓe aawi aawdi. Ammaa fakat ɓe ngartan bee seyo, ɓe kooran ƴaamle maɓɓe.

Nastuki
Bataakewol Yaakuba mooɓi ekkitinolji feere feere ballanɗi ummaatoore Allah, nuɗɗinɓe Yeesu Almasiihu saŋkitiiɓe haa duniyaaru fuu. Binnduɗo ngol huuwtiniri balndi ɗuuɗɗi ngam haa o faamtina anndal laaɓŋgal dow yahdu Masiihiŋkeejo yeeso tampa-diina'en. Ekkitinolji maako fuu ɗon tokkindiri booɗɗum. O winndi dow bolle mawɗe ɗe'e: Nuɗɗinki e hikma, laafere e risku, jarribooje e esto, nanugo e waɗugo, deentinki dow senndindirgo yimɓe, nuɗɗinki e kuuɗe, naŋgtingo ɗemŋgal, hikma diga dow, soobrugo bee duniyaaru, deentinki dow hiitugo woɗɓe, deentinki ngam mawnitiiɓe, deentinki ngam riskuɓe, munyal, hunayeere e tornde, nyawndeeki e hakkeeji.
Bataakewol ngo'ol ɗon wi'a laaɓɗum: “To nuɗɗinki hawtaay bee kuuɗe, ki waati.”
Tokkindirki haalaaji:
Koofŋgol 1:1
Nuɗɗinki e hikma 1:2-8
Laafere e risku 1:9-11
Jarribooje e esto 1:12-18
Nanugo e waɗugo 1:19-27
Deentinki dow senndindirgo yimɓe 2:1-13
Nuɗɗinki e kuuɗe 2:14-26
Baawɗe ɗemŋgal 3:1-12
Hikma diga dow 3:13-18
Soobrugo bee duniyaaru 4:1-10
Deentinki dow hiitugo woɗɓe 4:11-12
Deentinki ngam mawnitiiɓe 4:13-17
Deentinki ngam riskuɓe 5:1-6
Munyal 5:7-11
Taa kune 5:12
Tornde, nyawndeeki e hakkeeji 5:13-20

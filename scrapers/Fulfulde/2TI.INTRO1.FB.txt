Nastuki
Nder bataakewol ngo'ol boo, Pol tokkitini wasaago Timote haa o munyana boneeji e o tabito nder nuɗɗinki e nder njaggu Jawmiraawo (1:8; 3:10-11; 4:5,11). Pol yiɗi Timote laato soojeejo Yeesu Almasiihu koolaaɗo mo semtataa Linjiila sam e o laato boo gaazoowo cuusɗo (2:3,15; 4:2). Pol reentini Timote ngam taa o nasta gaabi paataaɗi, kumaaɗi (2:23), ammaa o samɗina Deftere Seniinde (3:16-17). O anndini mo boo ko tammi laataago nder nyalɗe garanɗe (3:1-9).
Tokkindirki haalaaji:
Koofŋgol 1:1-2
Dartaare nder njaggu 1:3-18
Soojeejo Yeesu Almasiihu koolaaɗo 2:1-13
Kuuwoowo gerdaaɗo 2:14-26
Deentinki dow halleende nder nyalɗe ragareeje 3:1-9
Cuusɗinki ngam tokkaago misaalu Pol 3:10-17
Wasuyeeji ragareeji 4:1-5
Umrooje ragareeje e koofli 4:6-22

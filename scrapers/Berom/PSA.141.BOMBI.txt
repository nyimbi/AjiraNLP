Won mwat de e simo Dagwi a dul ɛ
Lwɛlɛ̂ Davit
1À Dagwi Darwey, ma yila hwo, sɛlɛ me dey.
Wet betong jeng de me yila hwo.
2Sey won hom, na hɛ sè sede nɔrɔ̂ pyɛ̂ nesang hyɔɔlɔl e nuyel mo,
ná narâ vɔ hom sede narâ nɔrɔ̂ nawiri.
3À Dagwi Darwey, tɛ̀ me gyeng nu hom,
dul bwɛrɛk nu hom.
4Hwo tik na me raa cwey pyɛ̂ rebek,
me masa tomong ves tanga bemât râ neves wɛt,
hwo tik me re pyɛ̂ nzem mɛn wɛt.
5Tik mwat rwat a tabak me, hane ha sé nerat,
tik a gyong me, ha sé sede něy e rato hom.
Won hom jojot ha sé e ra neves de be ragas.
6Jeng de bê barak bemât nuyel mɛn yi e beraku,
yɛn ê wok neha hom yaga na ta.
7Sede cɔgɔt de ba vugus ba cɛrɛng yɛ,
ano de ba shara bekup mot e nû kwɔ̂n bevuvwel.
8Ma sɛt bayis e ra hwo, à Dagwi Darwey, Darwey hom,
ma tɛ̀ gap e ra hwo, hwo kwon tik na me ku wɛt.
9Dul me yi e besoro de ba ga rene yaga me,
yi e besorô vê neves.
10Tik vê neves be hwo e besoro mɛn,
ko me nju hom mé hywit me.

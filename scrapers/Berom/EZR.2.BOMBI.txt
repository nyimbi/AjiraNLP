Ngyɛrɛ̂ bazâ vemo de ba toyo hɛn
(Nɛh 7:6-73)
1Bemat de bá sè e de sesam o pyak bá sogo yi e bak vwêl Babilɔn, ko yɛn á to ɛn vey e Yɛrusalɛm na vwêl Yuda. Kawɛ e ji mɛn a kwey vɔk e repomo mɛ. Belɔ mɛn ba kana sè sangal e de sesam e vwêl Babilɔn yimo de Gbɔng Gwɔ̀m Nɛbukadnɛzar á kana hashe hɛn vɔk e hwomo e jeng como. 2Bemât nuyel mɛn vɛ a Zɛrubabɛl, na Yɛshuwa, na Nɛhɛmiya, na Seraya, na Reelaya, na Mɔrdɛkai, na Bilshan, na Bigvai, na Rehum tanga Baana.
Ngyɛrɛ̂ nepyak BeIsrayɛl a nemo e rùhup mɛn, mwa na nepyak bemat de ba toyo yi e kagwɛ ruhup.
3Ruhûp Parɔsh — bemat gu baba, na naga gwining kuru bataama na vɛ beba (2,172)
4Ruhûp Shefatiya — bemat naga betat na kuru bataama na vɛ beba (372)
5Ruhûp Ara — bemat naga betaama na kuru bataama na vɛ betungun (775)
6Ruhûp Pahat-Mowap, yi e lɔ Yɛshuwa na hê Yowap — bemat gu baba na naga rwit kuru rining na vɛ beba (2,812)
7Ruhûp Ɛlam — bemat gu rining na naga beba kuru batungun na vɛ benaas (1,254)
8Ruhûp Zatu — bemat naga shabetat kuru banaas na vɛ betungun (945)
9Ruhûp Zakai — bemat naga betaama kuru batyiimin viisis (760)
10Ruhûp Bani — bemat naga betyiimin kuru banaas na vɛ beba (642)
11Ruhûp Bebai — bemat naga betyiimin kuru baba na vɛ betat (623)
12Ruhûp Azgat — bemat gu rining na naga beba kuru baba na vɛ beba (1,222)
13Ruhûp Adonikam — bemat naga betyiimin kuru batyiimin na vɛ betyiimin (666)
14Ruhûp Bikvai — bemat gu baba kuru batungun na vɛ betyiimin (2,056)
15Ruhûp Adin — bemat naga benaas kuru batungun na vɛ benaas (454)
16Ruhûp Ater, de ba to yila hɛ a Hɛzɛkiya — bemat kuru shabatat na vɛ rwit (98)
17Ruhûp Bezai — bemat naga betat kuru baba na vɛ betat (323)
18Ruhûp Yora — bemat naga gwining kuru rining na vɛ beba (112)
19Ruhûp Hashum — bemat naga beba kuru baba na vɛ betat (223)
20Ruhûp Gibar — bemat kuru shabatat na vɛ betungun (95)
21Bemat de bedabeda mɛn bá kana sangal e bapomo vemo bá vɛsɛ hɛn arɔng:
Bemât Bet-Lɛhɛm ba sé naga kuru baba na vɛ betat (123)
22Bemât Netofa ba sé kuru batungun na vɛ betyiimin (56)
23Bemât Anatot ba sé naga gwining kuru baba na vɛ rwit (128)
24Bemât Bet-Azmavɛt ba sé kuru banaas na vɛ beba (42)
25Bemât Kiriyat-Yɛyarim, Kefira na Bɛɛrɔt ba sé naga betaama kuru banaas na vɛ betat (743)
26Bemât Rama na Geba ba sé naga betyiimin kuru baba na wɛ gwining (621)
27Bemât Mikmash ba sé naga gwining kuru baba na vɛ beba (122)
28Bemât Bet-Ɛl na Ai ba sé naga beba kuru baba na vɛ betat (223)
29Bemât Nebo ba sé kuru batungun na vɛ beba (52)
30Bemât Magbish ba sé naga gwining kuru batungun na vɛ betyiimin (156)
31Bemât Ɛlam ba sé gu rining na naga beba kuru batungun na vɛ benaas (1,254)
32Bemât Harim ba sé naga betat kuru baba viisis (320)
33Bemât Lɔt, na Hadit tanga Ono ba sé naga betaama kuru baba na vɛ betungun (725)
34Bemât Yeriko ba sé naga betat kuru banaas na vɛ betungun (345)
35Bemat Senaa ba sé gu batat na naga betyiimin kuru batat viisis (3,630)
36Ngyɛrɛ̂ rùhup bepiris de ba toyo à vemo:
Yi e ruhûp Yɛdaya, yi e lɔ Yɛshuwa — bemat ba sé naga shabetat kuru bataama na vɛ betat (973)
37Ruhûp Imer — bemat ba sé gu rining kuru batungun na vɛ beba (1,052)
38Ruhûp Pash-hur — bemat ba sé gu rining na naga beba kuru banaas na vɛ betaama (1,247)
39Ruhûp Harim — bemat ba sé gu rining kuru rining na vɛ betaama (1,017)
40Ngyɛrɛ̂ rùhup BeLevi de ba toyo à vemo:
Yi e ruhûp Yɛshuwa na Kadmiyɛl, yi e lɔ Hɔdaviya — bemat ba sé kuru bataama na vɛ benaas (74)
41E ji bemât lwɛ̄lɛ Gbɔng Duk Re Fongol, yi e ruhûp Asaf, bemat de ba toyo ba sé naga gwining kuru baba, na vɛ rwit (128)
42E ji bemât gyêng ri Gbɔng Duk o, yi e rùhup Shalum, na Ater, na Talmɔn, na Akup, na Hatita tanga Shobai, bemat ba sé naga gwining kuru batat na vɛ shabetat (139)
43E ji bemât fwôm Gbɔng Duk Re Fongol, ngyɛrɛ̂ bazâ rùhup bemat de ba toyo a vemo:
Rùhup Ziha, na Hasufa tanga Tabayɔt
44Kɛrɔs, na Siyaha na Padɔn
45Lɛbana, na Hagaba na Akup
46Hagap, na Shalmai na Hanan
47Gidɛl, na Gahar na Reyaya
48Rezin, na Nekoda na Gazam
49Uza, na Paseya na Besai
50Asna, na BeMewun na BeNefis
51Bakbuk, na Hakufa na Harhur
52Bazlut, na Mehida na Harsha
53Barkɔs, na Sisera na Tema
54Neziya tanga Hatifa
55E ji bemât fwôm Sɔlɔmɔn, ngyɛrɛ̂ bazâ rùhup bemat de ba toyo a vemo:
Rùhup Sotai, na Sɔfɛrɛt na Perida
56Yaala, na Darkɔn na Gidɛl
57Shefatiya, na Hatil, na Pɔkɛrɛt-Hazɛbayin na Ami
58Nepyak yére bemât fwôm Gbɔng Duk Re Fongol tanga ne bemât fwôm Sɔlɔmɔn de ba toyo na sé naga betat kuru shabatat na vɛ beba (392).
59-60Bemat naga betyiimin kuru batungun na vɛ beba (652) yi e rùhup Delaya, na Tobiya na Nekoda ba vey yi e bapomô Tel Mela, na Tel Harsha, na Kɛrup, na Adon tanga Imer, ko yɛn a tɔk dura, belɔ mɛn ba sé yi e yerê BeIsrayɛl wɛt. 61-62Ko e ji rùhup bepiris, hàk ba sé arɔng de ba tɔk sè kwɔn de ba jɛk ɛ bazâ nelɔ mɛn wɛt, vɛ a rùhup Hɔbaya, na Hakos na Barzilai. Dâ ruhûp Barzilai, á kana ra wenêng hwông Barzilai wo Gileyat, ko a jut zâ lɔ tyà mɛ. Ko yaga rùhup tat vemo ba tɔk dura, bedabeda mɛn ba sé yi e yére bepiris wɛt, bemât nuyel ba ye, yɛn a jam na be sè bepiris wɛt, ko ba paa ɛn yi e ji mo.
63Ano kwong, gwɔmna a nɔng ɛn nu be kwon re pyɛ́ de ba nára a Dagwi yɛ wɛt, sɛ̌ ba sɛ̀ piris de e tɔk pyɛ de Dagwi e simo yi na râ Urim na Tumim e ngyen.
64Mwa nepyak regyɛng bemat de ba toyo mo na sé gu kuru banaas na vɛ baba na naga betat kuru batyiimin viisis (42,360), 65sana tɛ̀ bemât fwom mɛn beras na beha gu bataama na naga betat kuru batat na vɛ betaama (7,337). Yɛn a vɔk bemât lwɛlɛ naga beba (200) beras na beha mo arɔng.
66Yɛn a to vɔk bedwa naga betaama kuru batat na vɛ betyiimin (736), tanga bedwa-madung naga beba kuru banaas na vɛ betungun (245), 67tanga bemaryat naga benaas kuru batat na vɛ betungun (435), tanga bemadung gu batyiimin na naga betaama kuru baba viisis (6,720).
68Jeng de yɛn a wul Gbɔng Duk Re Fongol wo Dagwi Darwey e Yɛrusalɛm, behak e ji bedanelɔ rùhup o ba nɔng nɔrɔ̂ yey pyɛng, yaga na be to lɔk mɛ Gbɔng Duk o e kwɔn de wá kana sè ɛ. 69Yɛn a nɔng tyɛng na gap mɛn kilo naga betungun (500) vê dyam shinang, na kilo gu baba na naga rwit (2,800) vê dyam pyɛng tanga berugû bepiris naga gwining (100). Yɛn a nɔng yɛ e vwɔ̂ mwât bâng yas Gbɔng Duk o.
70Ano kwong, bepiris, na BeLevi, tanga bwang bemat o behak ba wɔ̀ sangal e Yɛrusalɛm kɛ bagat na wɛ. Bemât lwɛlɛ, na bemât gyêng ri Gbɔng Duk Re Fongol na bemât fwôm Gbɔng Duk o ba wɔ̀ sangal e bapomo vê bagat, ko bwang BeIsrayɛl o ba wɔ̀ sangal e bapomo mɛn.

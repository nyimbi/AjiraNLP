1  À Dagwi Darwey, hwo gyong me na shom wɛt! Hwo tabak me vět wɛt! 

2  Tosho reyɔm hom, ma ga tɛ́ me gbɛrɛk! Me ra dedan, tɛ̀ me wɛng-wɛng! 

3  Ma saa e ji reyɔm, gwoněng reyɔm, ko hwó gyeng, na hwo sɛlɛ me e yɛ̌ jeng ɛ? 

4  Yē rɔ̀s a shom mō! Sey me, à Dagwi Darwey! Sey me yi e reku, yaga hwà tɛ̀ yey na me. 

5  Jeng de ma ku, mê tɔk to bare na hwo wɛt, vit a hwak de ê tɔk somo hwo e kwɔ̂n bevuvwel. 

6  Ma ga tɛ́ me fɛkfɛk yaga rus. Mwa turuk ma sé tosho, gwat hom gwa tɛ́ ɛ varak na nemwishil. 

7  Bayis hom ba sé hywiinin, ba ga tɛ́ ɛn shǔt yaga sesin, bemât repugu hom vɛ de ba tɛ̀ ano. 

8  Gas in na me, a yin bemât ragâs neves! Dagwi Darwey a ga wok tosho hom. 

9  Dagwi Darwey a ga sey dâk gabɔk hom yɛ a ga sey won hom. 

10  Ê tɛ̀ bemât repugu hom, be sɔ rekyen, be vow, bê pere tele, ko rekyen rê kyiri hɛn.

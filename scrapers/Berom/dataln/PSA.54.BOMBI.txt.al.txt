1  À Dagwi, sey me na gbɔng reto mō, dura, vit a me na nɛn! 

2  À Dagwi, wok won hom, wet betong na neha hom! 

3  Bemat beduk ba ga hwo e ra me. Bemat de vit a hɛn na nemwishish, pyɛ mɛn cak ya sé na hwo wɛt, bé tē ngyɛ́k na be mɔrɔ me. 

4  Ko hwo à Dagwi, hwà sé mwât sɛlɛ hom, à Darwey hom, hwó gyeng neken hom. 

5  Hwô tala a vemo de be jama bol hom neves mɛn. Sila vonu de hwà ra a me, ko hwo wey na yɛn. 

6  Ano kwong, ma rɔda nára a hwo nɔrɔ̂ yey pyɛng. Ma rɔda somo reza mō, à Dagwi Darwey, yaga hwà sé wo nerat. 

7  Hwà ga pat me yi e reyɔm hom mwa, ko me bɛrɛng vê repugu hom de ba ga di wɛng e ra yɛn.

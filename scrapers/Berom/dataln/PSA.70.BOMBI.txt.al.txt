1  À Dagwi, sey me! À Dagwi Darwey, vey sɛlɛ me dey-dey! 

2  Tik be dɔnɔ vemo de be jama be mɔrɔ me, tik nerey mɛn ne gaba. Tik vemo de be feng yaga reyɔm de me sɔ be vo rekyen. 

3  Tik vemo de be feng ɛ wɛng e gaba hom, be man nenu na rekyen. 

4  Ko mwa vemo de be simo, be se bagat na hwo, tɛ̀ ɛn, be vɔk yey pyɛng na nzem! Tɛ̀ vemo de be feng, yaga hwà sey ɛn, be dopse tē, “Dagwi a sé wo nerey!” 

5  Me, ma sé pwɛng, me simo sɛlɛ. À Dagwi Darwey, hwà sé mwât sɛlɛ na mwât sey hom. Vey na hwo sɛlɛ me dey-dey! Hwo lashe jeng wɛt!

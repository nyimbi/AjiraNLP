Dagwi e nàra pang na bemɛ ka e ji reyɔm
Lwɛlɛ de bê ra e gwong vɔk e Yɛrusalɛm
1Ba ga tak a me ding-ding yimo nebot hom,
Tik Israyɛl a ha.
2Ba ga tak a me ding-ding yimo nebot hom,
ba ga sang di wɛng e ra me wɛt.
3Ba vugus a me rama,
sede bemât cap be how nerɔ.
4Dagwi Darwey a sé wo tsak o,
a ga tɛ̀ me washak yi e vɔ vê neves.
5Tik mwa vemo de be bɛ̀ɛ Siyɔn ves,
be sɔ rekyen.
6Tik be tɛ́ ɛn sede sɔng de gwa ta e ra bushâ duk,
de gwo hworok gyam gwo ras.
7Mwât yɛ̂t gwɛ e tɔk sila vwɔ wɛt,
mwat rɔgɔ̂s bato mo ê sɛ̀ pyɛ de ya vey rɔgɔs wɛt.
8Tik vemo de be kweres be kyɛ́ ɛn na ye:
“Nerât Dagwi Darwey ne sè na yin!”
Ko bemât yɛt o be toyo a yɛn, be tē,
“Wot a tɛ̀ a yin nerat e ji rezâ Dagwi Darwey!” wɛt arɔng.

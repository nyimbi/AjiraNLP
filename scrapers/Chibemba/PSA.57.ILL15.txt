Kuli kembisha mukalamba. Mikitamu wakwa Davidi.
Ku ciunda ca “Wionaula.” Ulwimbo lwakwa Davidi, ifyo apepele ilyo abutwike Saulo no kuya belama mu lucengo.
1Umbelele uluse we Lesa wandi,
umbelele uluse pantu ine naisa mukushina uko uli.
Mu cintelelwe ca mapindo yobe emo nakulauba,
mpaka napo icinkunka cikapwila.
2Ndalilila kuli Lesa Mukulu,
kuli Lesa uufikilisha ubufwayo bwakwe pali ine.
3Euntumina ipusukilo ukufuma ku mulu no kumpususha,
no kukalipila bonse abansupila.
(Selah)
Lesa eutuma icitemwiko cakwe icishipwa ne cishinka cakwe.
4Ine ndi pakati ka nkalamo,
ndala pakati ka fiswango ifyakalipisha nganshi,
abantu abo ameno yabo yaba kwati mifwi na mafumo,
ne ndimi shabo shatwa ngo lupanga.
5Ube uwasumbulwa ukucila na pa myulu we Lesa,
no bucindami bobe bukumane isonde lyonse.
6Abalwani bandi nabanteya isumbu ilyakuti njikatwemo,
no mweo wandi wacululuka.
Baimba ubucinga ku ntanshi yandi,
lelo abene ebawililemo.
(Selah)
7 We Lesa umutima wandi ulifye uwashipa, ee ucilifye uwashipa,
ine nakulakwimbila inyimbo sha malumbo.
8Buuka we mutima wandi!
Buuka we seese, na iwe we nsango!
Ine nalashibusha amacaca.
9Nakulakulumbanya mu mishobo ya bantu, we Mfumu,
ine nakulaimba palwa iwe pakati ka benafyalo.
10Pantu icitemwiko cobe cikulu nganshi cafika na mu myulu,
ne cishinka cobe cafika na mu makumbi.
11Ube uwasumbulwa ukucila na pa myulu we Lesa,
no bucindami bobe bukumane isonde lyonse.

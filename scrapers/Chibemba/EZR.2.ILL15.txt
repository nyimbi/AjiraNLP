Amashina ne mpendwa ya bantu babwelele ukufuma muli bunkole
1 Aba e bantu ba mu citungu ababwelele mu calo cabo, ukufuma ku Babiloni uko Nebukadinesa imfumu ya ku Babiloni abasendele muli bunkole. Kanshi babwelele ku Yerusalemu pamo pene na ku Yuda, na cila muntu aya ku mushi kumwakwe. 2Aba ebaishile na Serubabeli, Yeshua, Nehemia, Seraya, Reelaya, Modekai, Bilishani, Misipari, Bigifai, Reumu na Baanaya.

Abena Levi ababwelele
40Abena Levi ni aba: Abana baume bakwa Yeshua na Kadimieli, abeshikulu bakwa Hodafiya, bali amakumi cine lubali na bane (74).
41Elyo bakemba abana bakwa Asafu bali umwanda umo na makumi yabili na cine konse konse (128).

58Ababomfi be Tempele na bana baume ba babomfi bakwa Solomoni bonse pamo bali imyanda itatu na makumi pabula na babili (392).
Abena Israeli na bambi
59Kabili aba, ebaninine ukufuma ku Tele Mela elyo na ku Tele Hasha, Kerubi, Adoni na ku Imeri, lelo calibanshishe ukulumbula uko amayanda ya bashibo yali, nangufye uko yatantikwe, nangu ukutilafye bena bali abena Israeli. 60Abana bakwa Delaiya, abana bakwa Tobia, abana bakwa Nekoda, bali imyanda mutanda amakumi yasano na babili (652).
61Nomba kuli bashimapepo ni aba: Abana baume bakwa Hobaya, abana baume bakwa Hakosi elyo na bana baume bakwa Basilai uwabuulile umukashi wakwe ukufuma ku lupwa lwakwa Basilai umwina Gileadi no kwinikwako ishina limo line. 62Aba ebo bafwailishe amashina ya fikolwe fyabo mu fitabo lelo tabafimwene, kanshi epakubafumya pa mulongo wa bashimapepo no kubapendela pa bashasangululwa. 63Umulashi wa calo apeele icebo icakuti takuli kulya icakulya camushilo mpakafye bakakwate shimapepo uukapendula ukubomfya Urimu na Tumimu.
64Abana bonse ababwelele ukufuma mu busha bali amakana amakumi yane na yabili ne myanda itatu na makumi mutanda (42,360). 65Babwela pamo na babomfi abaume na banakashi abali amakana cine lubali ne myanda itatu na makumi yatatu na cine lubali (7,337), aba bena bali na bakemba abaume na banakashi imyanda ibili (200). 66Bakabalwe babo bali imyanda cine lubali na makumi yatatu na mutanda (736). Imisepela ya bapunda bakwete imyanda ibili amakumi yane na basano (245), 67ingamila shabo nasho shali imyanda ine amakumi yatatu na shisano (435), elyo bapunda bali amakana mutanda imyanda cine lubali na makumi yabili (6,720).
68Ilyo abali mu busha ku Babiloni bafika ku ŋanda yakwa Lesa iyali mu Yerusalemu, abakalamba ba ndupwa bamo batuula ifyabupe ifyakuitemenwa ifyakwafwilishako ukukuula iŋanda yakwa Lesa apo yakuulilwe kale. 69Ukulingana na maka bakwete, bapeele indalama drakimasi amakana amakumi mutanda ne ikana limo (61,000) isha golide ne ndalama minasi amakana yasano (5,000) isha silifa, kabili ne fyakufwala ifya bashimapepo nafyo umwanda umo (100). 70Bashimapepo, abena Levi, bakemba, bakalinda wa mpongolo na babomfi be Tempele baikele mu misumba yabo, na bena Israeli abashala nabo mu misumba yabo.

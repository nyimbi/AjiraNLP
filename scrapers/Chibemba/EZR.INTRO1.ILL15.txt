Amashiwi yakulondolola pali ici citabo
Icitabo cakwa Esra emwaba amashiwi ayatwalilila ukukonka pa mashiwi aya mu citabo ca milandu. Muli ici citabo emo balondolola ifyo bamo Abayuda babwelele ukufuma muli bunkole ku Babiloni ukubwelela ku Yerusalemu, uko baishile tendeka ukwikala no kwamba ukupepa ngefyo balecita kale. Ne milandu yalecitika yatantikwa ifi.
1. Ibumba lyakubalilapo ilya Bayuda abali muli bunkole lyafuma ku Babiloni ukulingana ne fyapingwile Sairasi uwali Kateeka mukalamba wa ku Pesia.
2. Bakuula Itempele no kulituula kuli Lesa, elyo no kupepa Lesa kwatendeke kabili mu Yerusalemu.
3. Ilyo papitile imyaka, na limbi ibumba lya Bayuda lyabwelela ku Yerusalemu ninshi liletungululwa na Esra, uwali uwacenjela nganshi mu mafunde yakwa Lesa. Esra ewayafwile abantu ukwishiba amapepo elyo nefyo bafwile ukulaikala bwino pakusunga ifishilano fya mapepo ya bena Israeli.
Imilandu yatantama ifi
Ibumba lyabalilepo ukubwela ukufuma muli bunkole. 1:1—2:70
Itempele lyakuulwa cipya cipya no kutuulwa kuli Lesa. 3:1—6:22
Esra abwela pamo ne bumba na limbi ilya bafumine muli bunkole. 7:1—10:44

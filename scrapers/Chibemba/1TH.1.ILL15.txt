1Ine Paulo na Sailasi na Timote, twamulembela mwe lukuta lwa mu Tesalonika, mwe baampana na Lesa Tata ne Mfumu Yesu Kristu. Icikuuku no mutende fileba na imwe.
Paulo atasha Lesa pa cisumino ca bena Tesalonika
2Lyonsefye tulatasha Lesa pa mulandu wa imwe bonse, kabili lyonsefye tulamupepelako mu mapepo yesu. 3Lyonse ilyo tupepa Lesa wesu kabili Shifwe, tulebukisha ifyo mubomba pa mulandu wa cisumino cenu. Twibukisha no kubombesha kwenu pa mulandu wa citemwiko cenu. Twibukisha nefyo isubilo lyenu mu Mfumu yesu Yesu Kristu lyaba ilyakosa. 4Mwe bamunyinefwe, twalishiba ukuti Lesa alimutemwa, kabili alimusala. 5Pantu ilyo twamuletele Imbila Nsuma, tatwaishile na mashiwi yekafye iyoo, lelo twaishile na maka na Mupashi Wamushilo nefyo twashininkisha umupwilapo. Mwalishiba ifyo twaleikala ilyo twali na imwe, fyali fyakumwafwilishako. 6Mwasukile mwalapashanya ifwe pamo ne Mfumu. Nangu mwaculile nganshi, mwapokelele Imbila Nsuma ne nsansa isho Mupashi Wamushilo apeela. 7Eico, imwe mwaba nomba icilangililo kuli bonse abasumina aba mu Makedonia na mu Akaia. 8Amashiwi ya Mfumu tayasalanganafye mu Makedonia na mu Akaia ukufuma kuli imwe, lelo ilyashi lya pa cisumino cenu muli Lesa, lyalisalangana mpanga yonse. Awe tapali ico twingalandapo pali ici. 9Abantu abene ebalanda pafyo mwatupokelele bwino. Balanda na pafyo mwafuma kukupepa utulubi, mwayalukila kukupepa Lesa, pakuti mulebombela Lesa wine wine kabili uwamweo. 10Lyena epakulalolela ukwisa kwa Mwana wakwe Yesu ukufuma ku mulu, uo abushishe ku bafwa, eutupususha ku bukali bwakwa Lesa ubuleisa.

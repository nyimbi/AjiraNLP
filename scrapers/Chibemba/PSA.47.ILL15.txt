Kuli kembisha mukalamba. Ulwimbo lwa bana bakwa Kora.
1Toteni amapi mwe bantu ba mishobo yonse,
sansuleni amashiwi pakwimbila Lesa.
2Mulekwatila Yawe Mukulu akatiina,
e mfumu nkalamba iyiteeka isonde lyonse.
3Ewatucimfishako imishobo ya benafyalo,
abantu abo twanyantilila.
4Ewatusalilako ne calo cabupyani bwesu,
icakusekelela cakwa Yakobo uo atemenwe.
(Selah)
5Lesa alenina pakuya ikala pa cipuna cakwe icabufumu,
baleawila Yawe utupundu no kumulishisha amapenga.
6Imbileni Lesa isha malumbo, mwimbileni amalumbo!
Imbileni Imfumu yesu, mwimbileni!
7Lesa euteeka pesonde lyonse,
mwimbileni masikili wakumulumbanya.
8Lesa euteeka imishobo ya benafyalo,
Lesa aikala pa cipuna cakwe icamushilo.
9Elyo baisa longana bakateeka ba mishobo ya benafyalo,
pamo nga bantu bakwa Lesa wakwa Abrahamu,
pantu ishamfumu shonse pano isonde shakwa Lesa,
uwacilisha ukupulama.

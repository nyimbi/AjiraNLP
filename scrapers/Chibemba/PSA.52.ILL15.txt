Kuli kembisha mukalamba. Masikili
wakwa Davidi, ilyo Doegi umwina Edomu aile eba Saulo ati, “Davidi engile mu ŋanda yakwa Aimeleki.”
1Bushe cinshi ico uitakishisha pa bubi, we mwaume impalume?
Bushe ni cinshi ico uitakishisha akasuba konse
iwe we wasebana mu menso yakwa Lesa?
2Ululimi lobe lupangafye ubonaushi,
lwaba ngo lwembe ulwatwa,
iwe we ubomfya ubucenjeshi.
3Watemwa ububifi ukucila ubusuma,
no kulanda ifyabufi ukucila icishinka.
(Selah)
4Watemwa ukulanda amashiwi ayakucena abantu,
we wa lulimi lwabucenjeshi.
5Ee Lesa akakutentemuna umuyayaya,
ali nokukukusanga akakwimye akakufumye na mwitenti lyobe,
ali nokukushulumuna akakufumye na mu calo ca bamweo.
(Selah)
6Abacita ubololoke pakumona ici cintu bakalatiina,
bakalamuseka no kusosa abati,
7“Moneni, ulya muntu uwakene ukulashina kuli Lesa,
ena aposelefye amano yakwe ku cuma cakwe icali icingi,
akosha no bwikashi bwakwe mukonaula bambi.”
8Lelo ine nabafye ngo muti wa muolifa,
uukulila mu ŋanda yakwa Lesa.
No mweo wandi watetekela pe mu kutemwa kwakwa Lesa ukushipwa.
9Nakulakutotela umuyayaya pali fyonse ifyo wacita,
nakulasumbula ishina lyobe ilishaiwamina,
nakulakulumbanya pa cinso ca bakupetamina.

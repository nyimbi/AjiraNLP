Amashiwi ayakulondolola pali ici citabo
Ulwimbo lwa Nyimbo yaba mashiwi ayalondolola ifyo umuntu engaitatakula mu mashiwi ayakutemwa. Ngefyo amashiwi ayengi aya muli ici citabo yaba pa mashiwi ya lwimbo ayalefuma ku mwaume ukuya ku mwanakashi na mashiwi ayalefuma ku mwanakashi ukuya ku mwaume. Mu misango imbi intu bapilibwilamo amashiwi, ici icitabo bacita ukuti ulwimbo lwakwa Solomoni ukulingana nefyo calandwa mu Cihibulu. Ishi inyimbo shonse Abayuda bashibika ukuba nge cikope cakulangilila ubusuma bwa kutemwa kwakwa Lesa pa bantu bakwe, elyo ku bena Kristu nabo nge cikope cakulanga ubusuma bwa kutemwa kwakwa Kristu pa lukuta lwakwe.
Imilandu yatantama ifi
Ulwimbo lwantanshi 1:2—2:7
Ulwimbo lwacibili 2:8—3:5
Ulwimbo lwacitatu 3:6—5:1
Ulwimbo lwacine 5:2—6:3
Ulwimbo lwacisano 6:4—8:4
Ulwimbo ulwalenga mutanda 8:5-14

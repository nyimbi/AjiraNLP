Kuli kembisha mukalamba. Ku ciunda ca “Wionaula.” Ulwimbo lwakwa Asafu.
1Twakutotela we Lesa, twakutotela,
tulekulumbilisha peshina lyobe ilyaba mupepi,
tuleshimika ne fintu fyakupapa ifyo wacita.
2Lesa alanda ati, “Pa nshita iyo ine nkapima,
epo ine nkapingwilapo no mulinganya.
3Ilyo icalo nabekalamo bonse fyatenkana,
nine nkacikatilila ku maka yandi.
(Selah)
4Abafilumba ine ndabeba ukuti, ‘Mwilaitakisha,’
ababi nabo ndabeba ukuti, ‘Muleke amataki.
5Mwicita ifyamataki ku wa mu mulu,
muleke no kulalanda ifyakumupontela.’”
6Pantu ukusumbulwa takufuma ku kabanga nangu ku masamba,
kabili takufuma ku matololo nangu ku mpili kwisa,
7lelo ni Lesa eupingula,
eucindamika bamo no kucefya bambi.
8Yawe ali no lukombo mu minwe ulwaisulamo umwangashi uwakantuka,
kabili uwalula nganshi.
Ilyo alupongolola, na babi bonse pano calo bali nokunwamo,
bafipilile na mashi.
9Lelo ine nshakatale ndeka ukulumbanya Lesa wakwa Yakobo,
nkalamwimbila inyimbo shakumulumbanya.
10Ine nkaputula insengo sha babifi bonse,
lelo insengo sha bololoke shikasumbulwa.

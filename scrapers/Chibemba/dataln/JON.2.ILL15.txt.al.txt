1  Elyo Yona apepele kuli Yawe Lesa wakwe ilyo ali mu nda ya cisabi ati, 

2  “Mu kupelelwa kwandi nalilile kuli Yawe, nao anjasuka. Nalilile ninshi ndi mu nda ya ku calo ca bafwa, ne shiwi lyandi walyumfwa. 

3  Pantu wamposele muli bemba uwashika, pakati nankati ka menshi. Amenshi yeka yeka eyanshingulwike, na matamba yobe yonse yalempita pamulu. 

4  Na ine epakutila, ‘Iwe nauntamfya pa cinso cobe. Kanshi Itempele lyobe ilyamushilo ine nkalimona shani nakabili?’ 

5  Amenshi nayanjibisha, yalepita na pamulu, ine ndekafilafye. Itenga elinshingulwike, icani ca muli bemba nacintabilila na ku mutwe. 

6  Nintotobela mwisamba lya mpili, njile ku calo uko imipilikisho nainjisalila, lelo iwe we Yawe Lesa wandi, naufumya umweo wandi mu nindi. 

7  “Ilyo ndi mupepi no kuleka umweo, elyo naibukisha iwe we Yawe, ne pepo lyandi lyafika uko uli, mwi Tempele lyobe ilyamushilo. 

8  “Abapepa ifilubi ifyabuwelewele, bena balileka ukukupetamina. 

9  Lelo ine kwishiwi ilyakutotela nakulakutuula ne lambo, no kucita fyonse ifyo nalaile. Ala niwe we Yawe uupususha abantu.” 

10  Elyo Yawe aeba icisabi, naco epakuya lukila Yona ku mulundu.

1  Kutika ku kulila kwandi, we Lesa umfwa kwipepo lyandi. 

2  Ndelilila kuli iwe ukufuma ku mpela ya pano isonde, ine umutima wandi naunaka, untwale pa cilibwe icalepa ukuncila. 

3  Pantu niwe ine nshintililako, niwe ulupungu lwandi ulwamaka uluncingilila ku balwani. 

4  Uleke ndeikalilila mwi Tenti lyobe pe na pe, leka ndeuba mu mapindo yobe. (Selah) 

5  Pantu ulomfwa ifilayo fyandi we Lesa, wampeelako icikwatwa cilya upeela abatiina ishina lyobe. 

6  Ulundileko imfumu ya calo imyaka iyingi, uleke imfumu ikokole. 

7  Uleke ileteeka umuyayaya pa cinso cobe we Lesa, uleisunga ku citemwiko cobe icishipwa pamo ne cishinka cobe. 

8  Eico ine nakulaimba inyimbo shakulumbanya ishina lyobe pe, pakuti na ine inshiku shonse nkalafikilisha ifilayo ifyo nakulaile.

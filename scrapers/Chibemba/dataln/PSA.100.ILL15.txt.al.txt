1  Imbileni Yawe mulemwawila utupundu pano isonde lyonse 

2  Bombeleni Yawe ne nsansa, iseni pa cinso cakwe ne nyimbo shansansa. 

3  Ishibeni ukuti Yawe e Lesa. Ewatubumba, na ifwe tuli bakwe, tuli bantu bakwe kabili nifwe mpaanga sha bucemi bwakwe. 

4  Ingileni mu mpongolo she sano lyakwe no kutotela, mulemulumbanya pakwingila mu mansa shakwe, mulemutotela no kulumbanya ishina lyakwe. 

5  Pantu Yawe musuma, ne citemwiko cakwe camuyayaya. Icishinka cakwe citwalililafye umwakonkanina inkulo.

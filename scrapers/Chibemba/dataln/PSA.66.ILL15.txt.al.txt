1  Auleni utupundu, mwe bantu bonse! 

2  Imbileni ubucindami bwe shina lyakwe, amalumbo yakwe yamulenge ukucindama. 

3  Ebeni Lesa amuti, “Ificitwa fyobe fyacipeshamano! Amaka yobe yakalamba nganshi, pakuti abalwani bobe balekusembeleka. 

4  Aba pano isonde bonse niwe bapakata, niwe bembila inyimbo shakulumbanya, balumbanya ishina lyobe.” (Selah) 

5  Iseni mumone ifyo Lesa acita, uwalinga ukutiina pa milimo abombela abantu. 

6  Asangwile bemba ukuba umushili uwauma, ifikolwe fyesu epakwabuka umumana apauma ku makasa, eico lekeni tuleyanga ne nsansa. 

7  Euteeka pe ku maka yakwe, amenso yakwe eyasopa imishobo yonse. Mwileka abasangu balaitutubula apo ali. (Selah) 

8  Akulumbanyeni Lesa wesu, imwe mwe bantu bonse, lekeni inyimbo shakumulumbanya shumfwike. 

9  Ewatusunga abomi, no kulenga ukuti ifwe twiwa. 

10  Pantu ifwe walitwesha, we Lesa, walitwengulula filya bengulula silifa. 

11  Niwe watuleta mu cifungo, no kutwilikisha ifyafina mu numa. 

12  Niwe walekele abalwani besu baletunyantaula, ifwe twalipita mu mulilo na mu lyeshi, lelo walituleta ku cifulo ica fintu ifingi. 

13  Ndeleta ku ŋanda yobe amalambo ayakoca, no kufikilisha ifyo nakulaya, 

14  ifilayo imilomo yandi yalaile na kanwa kandi kalandile, ilyo ine nali mu bucushi. 

15  Nkakulekela amalambo ya nama ishaina, no mutuulo wa basukusuku, no kulatuula ilambo lya bacilume ba ŋombe na basawe. (Selah) 

16  Mwe bakwatila Lesa akatiina, iseni mumfwe bonse, pakuti mwebeko ifyo Lesa acitila umweo wandi. 

17  Nalilile kuli Lesa na kanwa kandi, no kumulumba no lulimi lwandi. 

18  Umutima wandi ausaminwa ububi, Yawe nga taŋumfwile iyoo. 

19  Lelo Lesa ine aliŋumfwile icine cine, akutika na kwipepo lyandi. 

20  Lesa apaalwe na matotelo pantu tasuulile ipepo lyandi, nangu ukuleka elankwatila icitemwiko cakwe icishipwa.

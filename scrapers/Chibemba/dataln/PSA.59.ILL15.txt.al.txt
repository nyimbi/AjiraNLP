1  Mpokololako ku balwani bandi we Lesa, uncingilile ku balenjimina. 

2  Mpokololako kuli aba bantu bacita ifyabubifi, untuleko kuli aba bantu basumya umulopa. 

3  Umone ifyo balalilila umweo wandi, abantu abankalwe nabantengamina, te pa mulandu wakuti ine nimbifya nangu ukucita icabipa we Yawe. 

4  Apabula umulandu mbacitile beminefye ukwisa mukunsansa. Wime wise untuleko, ulolekeshe na pa bufwayo bwandi. 

5  Iye we Yawe Lesa umwine wa fita, Lesa wa bena Israeli, ala ima pakuti ukande imishobo yonse! Wibabelelako no luse aba bacisanguka wa bantu. (Selah) 

6  Bena babwela icungulo, no kupemekesa nge mbwa ilyo baleendauka mu musumba. 

7  Nga wakutika ku fifuma mu tunwa twabo, amashiwi ya mu tunwa twabo yatwa ngo lupanga, bena benda balelanda ati, “Ngo wingatumfwa nani?” 

8  Lelo we Yawe ubasekafye, no kusenteka imishobo ya bantu bonse. 

9  Niwe maka yandi, ala niwe ndoleshako, we Lesa wandi niwe cubo candi. 

10  We Lesa wandi, we wantemwisha walantangilila, walalenga nokuti moneko uko abalwani bandi balecimba. 

11  We Yawe, we nkwela yesu, wibepaya bwangu abantu aba, epali abantu bandi balabako, basalanganye ku maka yobe elyo ubalofye. 

12  Pa mulandu wakuti ifyabipa fyaba pa tunwa twabo, no kulanda ifyabipa e lyashi lyabo, eico leka icilumba cabo cibalye. Kabili na pa mulandu wa fitipu elyo no bufi ubo babepa, 

13  ubonaule ku cipyu cobe, ubalenge balobelele. Lyena abantu bonse bakeshiba ukuti we Lesa niwe uteeka Yakobo, bakeshiba ukufika na ku mpela shonse isha pano isonde. (Selah) 

14  Bena babwela icungulo, ninshi balepemekesa nge mbwa, ilyo baleendauka mu musumba. 

15  Bekalilafye ukululumba mukufwaya ifyakulya, no kulailishanya nga fyacepa. 

16  Lelo ine nakulalumbanya amaka yobe, ulucelo lonse ine nakulapongomoka pakwimba palwa citemwiko cobe icishipwa. Iwe uli cubo candi, uli ibutukilo lyandi mu nshiku sha kupelelwa kwandi. 

17  Nakulakulumbanya, we bulamba bwandi, pantu we Lesa uli cubo candi, we Lesa we wantemwisha.

1  Bushe mulandu nshi imishobo ya bantu ipondokela? Bushe cinshi abenafyalo bakwatila amapange ayabuwelewele? 

2  Ishamfumu sha pano isonde shileipekanya ku bulwi, na bakateeka nabo nabalongana pamo, balepangila Imfumu no wasubwa wakwe. 

3  Balelanda no kuti, “Natuputaule iminyololo yabo, kabili natukaane na mafunde yabo.” 

4  Uyo uwaikala pa cipuna cabufumu ku mulu ena abaseka, Yawe asuka abasenteka. 

5  Elyo abasosha mwishiwi lyabukali, no kubatiinya mu cipyu, ati, 

6  “Ine ninsonta imfumu yandi pa Sioni, ulupili lwandi ulwamushilo.” 

7  Ine ndi nokulondolola icipope cakwa Yawe, ico asosele kuli ine ati, “Iwe uli mwana wandi, ilelo naba wiso. 

8  Ulombe kuli ine, na ine ndekupeela imishobo ya benafyalo ibe ubupyani bobe, ne sonde lyonse apo lyapela likaba icacishilano cobe. 

9  Ukalabateeka ku nkonto ya cela, ukabatobaula mu tupimfya tupimfya nge nongo.” 

10  Eico shilimukeni mwe shamfumu, cenjeleni imwe mwe bakateeka ba pano isonde. 

11  Mulebombela Yawe na katiina, mu mwenso no kututuma. 

12  Mulemucindika na katiina, pantu nga cakuti akalipa kuti amulofya, ico icipyu cakwe cilabilimuka bwangu bwangu. Balishuka bonse abafisama muli ena.

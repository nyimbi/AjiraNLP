1  Umbeleleko uluse we Lesa, umwabela ubwingi bwa luse lobe. Ukulingana ne cikuku ico wakwatisha, ufuute bumpulamafunde bwandi. 

2  Unsambe bwino bwino ukufuma ku fyabupulumushi fyandi fyonse, kabili unsangulule ku fyabubifi fyandi. 

3  Pantu bumpulamafunde bwandi ine nalibwishiba, ne fyabubifi fyandi ndafibukisha lyonsefye. 

4  Ni kuli iwefye weka nabifyako, no kucita ifintu ifyabipa pa cinso cobe. Ilyo ulanda, bonse balamona ukuti ulalungika, kabili taulufyanya mu kupingula kobe. 

5  Cine cine ine ni mu bubifi emo nafyalilwe, na lyonse nabafye umubifi ukutula apo naimitilwe kuli mayo. 

6  Apo ufwaya umutima wabupete, eico nsambilisha amano yakwishiba mukati ka mutima wandi. 

7  Ufuute ifibi fyandi ku tumisambo twa hisopo, uleke mbefye uwapwililika kuli bukaele, unsambe uleke mbeke ngo bunga bwa mfula. 

8  Leka ŋumfwe ukusekelela ne nsansa, pakuti nangu wanshonaula no kumfunaula, lelo ulenge amafupa yandi ukuba ne nsansa nakabili. 

9  Utalushe icinso cobe ku fibi fyandi, upase ubulungani bwandi bonse. 

10  Umbikemo umutima uwakaele we Lesa, ubukulushe no mupashi wandi pakuti ube uwashipa. 

11  Wintamfya pa cinso cobe, kabili wimpokolola Umupashi obe uwamushilo. 

12  Umbweseshe insansa sha kupususha kobe, umpeele no mutima uusekelela mukumfwila. 

13  Lyena elyo nakulafunda bampulamafunde umwakwishila kuli iwe, na babifi bakulabwelela kuli iwe. 

14  Umpusushe ku milandu ya mulopa we Lesa, we Lesa uumpususha, na ine nakulalumbilisha ubulungami bobe. 

15  Isula imilomo yandi we Yawe, epakuti na ine ndekulumbanya. 

16  Ine kuti natuula ilambo, lelo ifi tefyo ufwaya, na malambo yakoca nayo yene tayakutemuna iyoo. 

17  Lelo umutima uwabupete e lambo lyakwa Lesa, pantu umutima uwaicefya mukulapila, we Lesa tawakausuule. 

18  Cita ifisuma kuli Sioni mu nshita ya kutemwa kobe, ukuule na malinga ya Yerusalemu. 

19  Elyo ukasekelela amalambo ayalungama, amalambo yakoca ayakukusansamusha, elyo na bacilume ba ŋombe bakulapeelwa pa bulambo bobe.

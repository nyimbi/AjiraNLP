1  Yangu icifulo cobe ubusuma, we Yawe umwine wa fita! 

2  Umweo wandi ulefuluka, kabili naucushiwa kukufuluka isano lyobe we Yawe. Umutima wandi no mubili wandi fyalilila kuli Lesa wamweo. 

3  Uluseba nalupanga iŋanda, akamimbi nako kakula icisansala, umwakulasungila abana, pa ncende iyaba mupepi ne fibumbili fyobe ifya malambo, we Yawe umwine wa fita, we mfumu yandi, we Lesa wandi. 

4  Balishuka abekala mu ŋanda yobe, abakulumbanya inshita shonse. (Selah) 

5  Balishuka abantu abo amaka yabo yaba muli iwe, abapanga ubulendo bwakwisa nina ulupili lwa Sioni pakupepa. 

6  Ilyo bena bapita mu mukonko wa ku Baka, ilya ncende balaisangula iya tumfukumfuku twa menshi, ne mfula ya mu lusuba lunono ilapangamo ifishiba. 

7  Amaka yabo yalacililako, mpaka cila umo asuke amonekele mu Sioni pa cinso cobe we Lesa. 

8  Umfwe ipepo lyandi we Yawe Lesa umwine wa fita, kutika kuli ine we Lesa wakwa Yakobo. (Selah) 

9  Uloleshe pa nkwela yesu, we Lesa, posa amenso yakwafwa pa musubwa obe. 

10  Ubushiku bumo bwawamishapo mwisano lyobe, ukucila pa nshiku imyandamishila mu ncende imbi. Kuti natemwa naba umulinshi wa pa mwinshi wa ŋanda yakwa Lesa wandi, ukucila ukwikala mu matenti ya babifi. 

11  Pantu Yawe Lesa e kasuba ne nkwela, Yawe alapeela ipaalo no bucindami. Ena tatana cisuma nangu cimo kuli abo abenda mu bololoke. 

12  We Yawe umwine wa fita, alishuka umuntu uutetekela muli iwe.

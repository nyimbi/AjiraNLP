1  We Lesa, ala abenafyalo nabasansa icalo ca bantu bobe, nabakowesha ne Tempele lyobe ilyamushilo, nababongolola no musumba wa Yerusalemu. 

2  Bapeele ifitumbi fya bantu bobe ku fyuni pakuti filelya, bafipeela na ku finama fya mu mpanga nafyo filelya. 

3  Nabasumya umulopa wa bantu nga menshi ukushinguluka Yerusalemu, tapashele nangu umo uwakushiika ifitumbi fya banankwe. 

4  Natuba abasebana ku bena mupalamano besu, baletuseka no kutuwela. 

5  Bushe kufika lilali we Yawe? Uli nokwikala uwafulwa inshita yonse? Bushe kufika lilali ubufuba bobe bwakulabilima ngo mulilo ku bukali? 

6  Shifulilwa abantu ba mishobo abashikupepa, elyo ne shamfumu ishishiita peshina lyobe, 

7  pantu nabepaulula Yakobo, nabonaula ne calo cakwe. 

8  Witubika pakukandwa pa bubifi bwa fikolwe fyesu, ifwe twalombafye ukuti, “Utubelele uluse, mukwangufyanya utukumanye, pantu ifwe amano nayapela.” 

9  Sotwafweko we Lesa, we Mupusushi wesu, pakuti ishina lyobe licindamikwe, utupokololeko no kulatwelela ububifi bwesu, pa mulandu we shina lyobe. 

10  Nga cinshi imishobo ingalasosela aiti “Nga Lesa wabo ali kwi?” Kanshi leka tumoneko uko ulekanda imishobo, pa mulandu wakwipaulula ababomfi bobe. 

11  Uleke ukulila kwa bafungwa kumfwike kuli iwe, ku bulamba bobe upokolole abo bapingula ukwipaya. 

12  We Yawe umusaalula onse uo abena mupalamano besu bakucitila, leka ubabwelele mu ceni cabo imiku cine lubali. 

13  Elyo ifwe fwe bantu bobe, fwe mukuni wa bucemi bobe, twakulakutotela pe na pe, ukufuma ku nkulo ukufika ku nkulo ibiye twakulalondolola amalumbo yobe.

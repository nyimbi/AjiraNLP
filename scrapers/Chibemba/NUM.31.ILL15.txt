Abena Israeli balwisha abena Midiani
1Yawe aebele Mose ati, 2“Citila abena Israeli icilandushi pa bena Midiani. Panuma ya ifi elyo uli nokuya lala ukwalala ifikolwe fyobe.”
3Elyo Mose aebele abantu ati, “Peeleni ifita fimo ifyanso tuleya ku nkondo kukulwisha abena Midiani, tuye cita icilandushi cakwa Yawe pali bena. 4Cila luko lwa bena Israeli musalemo abakuya ku nkondo ikana limo limo (1,000).”
5Kanshi abena Israeli epakusala abantu abakuya ku nkondo, ikana limo limo ukufuma kuli cila luko, bonse pamo bali amakana ikumi limo na yabili (12,000). 6Elyo Mose abatuma ku nkondo, ikana limo limo ukufuma kuli cila luko pamo na Fineasi umwana mwaume wakwa Eleasa shimapepo, aya nasenda ne fipe fya cifulo camushilo na mapenga ayakulisha pakubeshibisha. 7Baimina abena Midiani ngefyo Yawe akonkomeshe Mose, baipaya na baume bonsefye. 8Kabili baipaila kumo ne mfumu shisano isha bena Midiani, Efi, Rekemu, Suri, Huri na Reba. Kabili baipaya Balamu umwana mwaume wakwa Beori ku lupanga.
9Abena Israeli basenda na banakashi abena Midiani muli bunkole na bana babo. Basenda ifyakutapa fyonse, iŋombe, imikuni ya mpaanga ne mbushi, ne fyuma. 10Baoca imisumba yonse iya bena Midiani kumo kwine ne nkambi baleikalamo shonse. 11Elyo basenda ifitapwa fyonse nefyo baonawile, pamo pene na bantu ne nama. 12Elyo batwala bankole, ifyo baikete nefyo batapile, kuli Mose na Eleasa shimapepo no lukuta lwa bena Israeli pa nkambi yabo, mu mawanga ya Moabu mupepi na Yordani ukulungatana na Yeriko.
Ifita fyabwela ku nkondo
13Mose, Eleasa shimapepo ne ntungulushi sha lukuta shonse bafumine kunse ya nkambi kukukumanya ifita. 14Mose afulilwa bakangalila ba fita, abakalamba ba fita amakana na bakalamba ba fita imyanda ababwelele ku nkondo. 15Abepusha ati, “Bushe namuleka bonse abanakashi ukuba abomi? 16Banakashi bene ebakonkelele ifyo Balamu asosele, kabili ebalengele nokuti abena Israeli basangukile Yawe ku fyacitike ku Peori, pakuti kube ne cikuko pa lukuta lwa bantu bakwa Yawe. 17Kanshi nomba ipayeni bonse abana abaume. Kabili mwipaye umwanakashi onse uwasendemapo no mwaume, 18lelo muishilepofye abakashana abashatala abasendamapo na baume. 19Bonse imwe mwe baipeyepo umuntu, nangu ukukumyakofye ku citumbi ca muntu uo bepeye, mwikalefye kunse ya nkambi pa nshiku cine lubali. Pa bushiku bwacitatu no bwa cine lubali muli nokuisangulula mwe bene pamo na bankole benu. 20Muli nokusangulula ifyakufwala fyonse, ne fyapangwa ku mpapa fine fyonse, amasako ya mbushi nangu ifyapangwa ku cimuti.”
21Elyo Eleasa shimapepo aeba ifita ifyaile ku nkondo ati, “Ifi efifwaikwa mu mafunde ayo Yawe apeele Mose. 22Golide, silifa, umukuba, icela, icikopo, mutofwe, 23na conse icishingapya mu mulilo, muli nokufipisha mu mulilo, elyo fili nokusangululwa kukukowela. Lelo nafyo fine fili nokuposwa mu menshi yakusangulula. Elyo ifintu fyonse ifingapya mu mulilo muli nokufipisha mu menshi yalya yene. 24Pa bushiku bwalenga cine lubali, mukacape ifyakufwala fyenu, lyena mukasangululwa. Elyo nomba mukabwelela mu nkambi.”
Bayakanya ifitapwa
25Yawe aebele Mose ati, 26“Iwe na Eleasa shimapepo ne ntungulushi sha ndupwa sha lukuta, muli nokupenda abantu bonse ne nama ishatapilwe. 27Akanyeni ifitapwa ku fita fyaile ku nkondo, elyo ifyashala mupeele ulukuta. 28Ukufuma ku fita fyalwile inkondo, mupatwileko Yawe umutuulo icintu cimo pa fintu imyanda isano, cibe ni pa bantu, iŋombe, bapunda, impaanga nangu imbushi. 29Mubuuleko ku cakaniko ca fita no kupeela Eleasa shimapepo, ukuba ciputulwa cakwa Yawe. 30Ukufuma ku cakaniko ca lukuta lwa bena Israeli, salenipo cimo pa fintu amakumi yasano, cibe ni pa bantu, iŋombe, bapunda, impaanga, imbushi nangu inama shimbi. Mupeele abena Levi, ababombela mwi Tenti lyakwa Yawe.” 31Kanshi Mose na Eleasa shimapepo epakucitila umo Yawe akonkomeseshe Mose.
32Ifyashele ukufuma ku fitapwa ifyo ifita fyaile ku nkondo fyasendele, fyali impaanga amakana imyanda mutanda, na makana amakumi cine lubali na yasano (675,000), 33iŋombe shali amakana amakumi cine lubali na yabili (72,000), 34bapunda bali amakana amakumi mutanda ne kana limo (61,000), 35elyo abanakashi abashatalile abasendamapo no mwaume nabo bali amakana amakumi yatatu na yabili (32,000). 36Citika wa ciputulwa catapilwe ico bapeele ifita fyaile ku nkondo, cali impaanga amakana imyanda itatu na makana amakumi yatatu na cine lubali ne myanda isano (337,500). 37Ishakutuula Yawe shali impaanga imyanda mutanda na makumi cine lubali na shisano (675). 38Ifita bafipeele iŋombe amakana amakumi yatatu na makanapo mutanda (36,000). Ishakutuula Yawe babuulilepo iŋombe amakumi cine lubali na shibili (72). 39Ifita bafipeele bapunda amakana amakumi yatatu ne myanda isano (30,500). Abakutuula Yawe babuulilepo bapunda amakumi mutanda na umo (61). 40Ifita bafipeele abantu amakana ikumi na mutanda (16,000). Abakutuula Yawe babuulilepo abantu amakumi yatatu na babili (32). 41Mose epakupeela Eleasa shimapepo umutuulo wakwa Yawe ngefyo Yawe amukonkomeshe.
42Citika wa bena Israeli, uo Mose abapatwililepo ukufuma pa fita fyaile ku nkondo, 43citika wa fyapeelwe ku lukuta ali impaanga amakana imyanda itatu na makumi yatatu na cine lubali ne myanda isano (337,500), 44iŋombe shali amakana amakumi yatatu na makana mutanda (36,000), 45bapunda bali amakana amakumi yatatu ne myanda isano (30,500), 46elyo abantu bali amakana ikumi na mutanda (16,000). 47Ukufuma pali citika wa fyapeelwe ku lukuta lwa bena Israeli, Mose abuulapo umuntu umo pali cila makumi yasano aya bantu na pa nama napo imo, ngefyo Yawe amukonkomeshe, elyo apeela ku bena Levi, abalebombela mwi Tenti lyakwa Lesa.
48Elyo abakalamba abali pakati ka fita fya nkondo, abakalamba ba fita amakana na bakalamba ba fita imyanda, baile kuli Mose, 49balamweba abati, “Fwe babomfi bobe natupenda ifita twaletungulula tapali nangu umo uulubile. 50Kanshi natuleta kuli Yawe umutuulo wa tuntu twa golide uto cila umo anonkele, amakosa, insambo, imbalaminwe na masikiyo, mukuifutilako pa fya bubifi bwesu pa cinso cakwa Yawe.” 51Mose na Eleasa shimapepo bapoka kuli bena golide na conse icabashilwe. 52Golide onse ukufuma ku bakalamba ba fita amakana na bakalamba ba fita imyanda uo Mose na Eleasa shimapepo batuulile kuli Yawe, afinine amashekeli amakana ikumi limo na mutanda ne myanda cine lubali na makumi yasano (16,750). 53Cila cita caliibuulilepo icitapwa caciko. 54Kanshi Mose na Eleasa shimapepo bapoka golide ku bakalamba ba fita amakana na bakalamba ba fita imyanda batwala mwi Tenti Lyakukumaninamo, kukuba icibukisho ca bena Israeli pa cinso cakwa Yawe.

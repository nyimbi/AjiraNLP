1  Indirimbo y'abazamuka bajya i Yeruzalemu. Igihe Uhoraho yasubizaga Siyoni ishya n'ihirwe, twe twabonaga ari nk'inzozi! 

2  Mbega ukuntu twishimye tugaseka! Mbega ukuntu twavugije impundu z'urwunge! Abanyamahanga ni ko gutangara bati: “Uhoraho yabakoreye ibihambaye!” 

3  Koko Uhoraho yadukoreye ibihambaye, natwe twarishimye. 

4  Uhoraho, udusubize ishya n'ihirwe, nk'uko usubiza amazi mu migezi ya Negebu. 

5  Ababiba barira bazasarura bishimye. 

6  Ujya kubiba agenda arira yikoreye imbuto, nyamara iyo agiye gusarura, agaruka yishimye yikoreye imiba.

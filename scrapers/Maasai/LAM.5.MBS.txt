Enkomono te nkaraki olng'ur
1Tadamu Olaitoriani ena bae natadoikio iyiook;
ing'ura nidol enkurruna ang.
2Ore enjung'ore ang neibelekenyakaki iltung'ana leeiking'a,
ore nkang'itie ang neaku inoo ltung'ana leeiking'a.
3Kitaa iyiook okishin,
lemeeta loomenye etiu noo yieyio anaa nkolia.
4Meeta nchere mikilak enkare nikiok
ore ilkeek likitum aainok keinyiang'uni.
5Eturrumoyioki iyiook kiaata enchata oo murto
nanapishoreki too murto.
Kinaurate iyiook neitu eishori iyiook enkiyeng'iyeng.
6Kisiaayia iyiook Misiri o Asiria,
pee kitum endaa naraposh iyiook.
7Etaasa apa loopapa eng'oki, nemeekure eetae;
kinapita iyiook entorroni enye.
8Eitoreita iyiook irrindiki;
nemeetae olokordu iyiook aitayu too nkaik enye.
9Kipik iyiook enkishui ang batisho pee kitum endaa,
te nkaraki ilo alem tiong'ota.
10Eirowuaja iseseni lang anaa olkokuet,
te nkaraki enkolong napejisho o lameyu.
11Ebatata nkituaak te Sion,
o selenken too nkang'itie e Yuda.
12Neiki ilkituaak aaisiong too nkaik enye;
nemeyanyiti ilpayiani kituaak.
13Nearakini ilbarnot meidong'isho te wueji neidong'ishoreki
neitaari inkayiok ilolan loo lkeek.
14Eisha ntonat oo lpayiani too leng'ot
neisha eranyata o sinkolio too lbarnot.
15Eisha eng'ida oo ltauja lang,
neibelekenye eranyata ang osinkolio aaku enkishirata.
16Eiwuang'ieki enkopiyia e nkitoo te ndukuya ang;
oi iyiook, amu kitaasa iyiook eng'oki!
17Enkaraki ena pee etamuoita iltauja lang,
enkaraki ena pee eiliilaitie nkonyek ang,
18amu ore ilo doinyo le Sion otoliyie netamanya mbariak.
19Kake ore iyie, Olaitoriani, ntoreisho ntarasi;
ebikoo olorika lino le nkitoo too ishoritin pooki.
20Kainyoo iyie pee midamu iyiook ntarasi,
kainyoo iyie pee inturraa iyiook te nkata naado?
21Torrinyoki iyiook kewan ino Olaitoriani,
pee kitum aatorrinyunye!
Ntang'ejuko nkolong'i ang metotiwuo anaa apa!
22Anaa itanya iyiook tukul.
Itagorokine doi iyiook tukul?

Enkai Olarishani
1Kincho iyiook iyie enashe, Enkai,
kinchoru enashe;
kimpot iyiook enkarna ino nikinken nkiaasin inono e nking'asia.
2Ore te ina kata nategeluaki,
naa kaiguana nanu too sipat.
3Te neikirikira enkop o lelo pooki oomanya,
nanu ilo oitasheyie ing'apeta enyena.
4Atiaaka nanu ilo owasa, “Mintoki awuasa,”
najoki ilo torrono, “Midumu emowuo;
5mishum iyie emowuo ino ailepunye,
anaa iro imolesh emurt.”
6Amu mme oloo saen,
neme oloo ntoluo,
neme ong'ata enkilepata;
7kake Enkai naiguana te sipata;
neitadou obo neilepunye olikae.
8Amu enkaina o Laitoriani etii enkikompe,
etii enaisho naitokitok naing'oling'olaki aaitobiraki,
nepiku ninye,
neok lelo aarruok pooki.
9Kake kang'ida nanu ntarasi,
karanyaki enkisisa e Nkai e Yakobo.
10Ore nena mowuarak pooki e ilo torrono naa kedung'oo ninye,
ore mowuarak e ilo supat naa keilepieki.

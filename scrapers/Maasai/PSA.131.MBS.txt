Enkomono e nkisiligata e emborron
1Olaitoriani, eitu epashipashu oltau lai,
neitu eshumunye nkonyek aainei aaing'or shumata;
maitanyamal nanu kewan too mbaa kituaak
o too mbaa e nking'asia te nanu.
2Kake aitirring'ie nanu naitobir oltau lai,
anaa enkerai naitigiraki to lkina le ng'otonye;
etotiuo oltau lai anaa enkerai naitigiraki.
3Iyie Israel, isiliga Olaitoriani ebaiki ena olong o ntarasi.

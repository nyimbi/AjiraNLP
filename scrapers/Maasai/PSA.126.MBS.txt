Enkomono te nkaraki enkordunoto
1Ore apa pee errinyu Olaitoriani lelo ootaarayioki le Sion,
nikitiu iyiook anaa ilooidetidet.
2Neitoki enkuenia aiput nkutukie ang,
neiput ilng'ejepa lang mbuakunot e ng'ida;
nejo ninche ti atua nkuapi,
“Etaasa Olaitoriani mbaa sapuki te nkaraki ninche.”
3Etaasa Olaitoriani mbaa sapuki te nkaraki iyiook;
kitishipate iyiook.
4Torrinyu lelo lang apa ootaarayioki, Olaitoriani,
meikununye anaa nchorroi e Negeb!
5Nchoo lelo oounisho too lkiyio
eisampu too mbuakunot e ng'ida!
6Ore ilo olo eishirita,
enapita olantererai olo aun,
nelotu ang too mbuakunot e ng'ida,
enapita enkisampuare enye.

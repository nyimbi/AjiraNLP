1Neitoki Elihu ajo,
2“Idamu iyie ena nijo kesipata?
Ijo iyie, ‘Esipata ai ena te dukuya Enkai,’
3amu inkilikuanu ajo,
‘ainyoo tipat naata nanu?
Kaa sipata nanu aata alang tenataasa eng'oki?’
4Ekaawaliki nanu iyie
tenebo ilchoreta linono likiboitare.
5Ing'ura keper pee idol,
naa ng'ura nena atampo, naatii shumata oleng shumata iyie.
6 Amaa tinitaasa eng'oki,
kainyoo indim ataasa atigilunore ninye?
Naa amaa tenetoponari intorrok inono,
kaa iyie intaas ninye?
7Amaa tinira iyie asipani ainyoo inchoo ninye;
arashu ainyoo doi eng'amu ninye te nkaina ino?
8Ore intorrok inono naa oltung'ani laaijo iyie eipirta
ore esupatisho ino naa enkerai e tung'ani.
9“Ore enkaraki nena kibookinot kumok e nkop e tung'ani neishir iltung'ana;
eipotu ninche eretoto te nkaraki enkaina e ilo Magilani.
10Kake metii olojo,
‘Koree Enkai Olaitobirani lai,
ilo oishoru isinkolioitin te nkewarie;
11ilo oiteng'en iyiook alang nena ng'uesi enkop
neiteng'en iyiook aitalang imotonyi oloing'ang'e?’
12Eishir ninche teidie, mewaliki ninye ninche,
te nkaraki ilo wuasa loo ltung'ana torrok.
13Esipa tukul mening Enkai enkishirata e pesho,
nemeshilaa ilo Magilani ninye.
14Kebaa naa elus tenijo midolita ninye,
inchere etii enkiguana dukuya ninye,
naa ninye iyie iyanyu!
15Neaku ore naa taata amu meitameisho engoro enye,
nemeinining'u ninye oleng intorrok.
16Ebol Yoab enkutuk enye tenkiroroto e pesho,
eponaa irorei lemeeta eng'eno.”

Keeya e Saul o layiok lenyena
(1 Int 10.1-12)
1Nearare apa Ilfilisti Israel; neipirri iltung'ana le Israel te dukuya Ilfilisti neidaikieki to Ldoinyo le Gilboa. 2Neinepu Ilfilisti Saul o layiok lenyena; near Ilfilisti Yonatan o Abinadab o Malikishua, lelo ayiok le Saul. 3Neitirrishaki olarrabal Saul oleng, netum ninye ilang'orok loo mbaa, netum olbae torrono te lelo ang'orok loo mbaa. 4Nejoki Saul ilo anapani lenye loo ruparen, “Intayu olalem lino taremoki meipanga te nanu, maapuonu ake kulo lememurata aa ng'ung'ur nanu, naaitaa nanu enkiguran.” Kake meidim ilo anapani lenye loo ruparen; amu eturia oleng. Neaku eitayio Saul olalem lenye makewan, nebatatakino. 5Ore ake pee edol ilo anapani lenye loo ruparen ajo etua Saul, nebatatakino sii ninye olalem lenye, neye te nebo ninye. 6Neaku etua Saul o layiok lenyena pokira okuni, o lanapani lenye loo ruparen, o ltung'ana lenyena pooki, te nkolong nabo te nebo. 7Ore ake pee edol iltung'ana le Israel apa ootii likae kekun o erata o lekua ootii te idia alo Yordan nchere eisika iltung'ana le Israel, netua Saul o layiok lenyena, neing'uaa ninche inkang'itie enye neisik; nepuonu Ilfilisti nebik ti atua ninche.
8Ore pee ekenyu, ore pee epuonu Ilfilisti aapur lelo ootaraki metuata, netum ninche Saul o layiok lenyena pokira okuni eirrag to Oldoinyo le Gilboa. 9Nedung'oo ninche endukuya enye, nelaku iruparen neirriwaa ilkiliku ti atua enkop pooki oo lfilisti, mewaita ilomon sidain aayaki inkaitin enye o ltung'ana. 10Nepik iruparen o larrabal enkaji e nkitanyaanyukoto e nkai naji Ashtarot; nesheiki osesen lenye esuntai e Bet-shan. 11Kake ore ake pee ening lelo oomanya Yabesh-Gilead ina naitaasa Ilfilisti Saul, 12nedumunye iltung'ana pooki oopi, nepuo enkewarie pooki, neitayu osesen le Saul o seseni loo layiok lenyena aaitung'uaa esuntai e Bet-shan; nepuonu ninche Yabesh nepejoo ninche te ine. 13Neya iloik lenye nenukaa ti abori olchani oji olkuaju te Yabesh, nepukoo ninche nkolong'i naapishana.

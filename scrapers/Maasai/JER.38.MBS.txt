Yeremia tiatua olchorro lemetii enkare
1Ore apa Shefatia olayioni le Matan, o Gedalia olayioni le Pashur olayioni le Malkia, o Yukal olayioni le Shelemia nening lelo rorei apa oojoki Yeremia iltung'ana pooki, 2“Nji ejo Olaitoriani, ore ilo oton tena anasa neye to lalem, o to lameyu o to lmeitai; kake ore ilo oipang alo Ilkaldayo nepok, netum ninye enkishui enye anaa enkishoroto to larrabal, nepok. 3Nji ejo Olaitoriani, Esipa keishoori ena anasa aapik atua enkaina e njore o laiguanani le Babilon neyai ninye.” 4Neitoki lelo kituaak aajoki olaiguanani, “Nchoo ele tung'ani metaari metua, amu eitashalita ninye nkaik e lelo sikarini oo tung'uayioki tiatua ena anasa, o nkaik oo ltung'ana pooki, tena kiroroto nairorie ninye ninche kulo rorei. Amu ore ele tung'ani mme olmabarishoi le kulo tung'ana eing'oru, kake enkitamioto enye.” 5Nejo olaiguanani Sedekia, “Ng'ura, etii ninye atua nkaik inyi; amu meidim olaiguanani ataasa ae toki napaashare intae.” 6Neaku etudumutua ninche Yeremia nesulaki ninye atua olchorro le Malkia olayioni lo laiguanani, apa otii atua olgoriet le rripore, eitadoyio Yeremia abori too lgoso. Nemetii apa enkare atua ilo shorro, kake esarng'ab ake, neimis Yeremia tiatua ina sarng'ab.
7Ore ake pee ening Ebed Melek ilo apa le Etiopia, ilo apa sunash ogelema kitok otii atua enkaji o laiguanani, nchere etipika ninche Yeremia atua olchorro. Netonita apa olaiguanani tiatua enkishomi e Benchamen. 8Nelo Ebedmelek te nkaji o laiguanani nejoki olaiguanani, 9“Olkitok lai olaiguanani, etaasa kulo tung'ana entorroni teina pooki naitaasa ninche Yeremia oloiboni le Nkai tesulakinoto natusulakitia ninye atua olchorro: naa keye ninye te sumash, amu meetae emukate natelekua tiatua ena anasa.” 10Neitoki olaiguanani aitanap Ebedmelek ilo apa le Etiopia ajoki, “Iwa iltung'ana okuni tene nipuopuo aailepunye Yeremia oloiboni le Nkai aaitayu tiatua olchorro eton eitu eye.” 11Nerik Ebedmelek iltung'ana tenebo ninye enkaji o laiguanani, enetii osanduku loo nkilani teneshumishoreki, neitayu ninye teine mpurdat o nkilani naatopolosate, apa naaitadoikio ninye Yeremia tiatua olchorro too lgoso. 12Nejoki Ebedmelek ilo le Etiopia Yeremia, “Tipika nena purdat oo nkilani nkitikit inono o lgoso.” Neiko neijia Yeremia. 13Neitayu ninche Yeremia aailepunye too lgoso neilepunye ninye aaitayu tiatua ilo shorro. Nebik Yeremia to lgoriet le rripore.
Eikilikuanua Sedekia eutaroto e Yeremia
14Neirriwaa Sedekia olkilikuai meyauni Yeremia oloiboni le Nkai neng'amu ninye te nejing'ieki e uni e nkaji o Laitoriani. Nejoki olaiguanani Yeremia, “Kaaikilikuan nanu iyie enkikilikuanata; misudoo toki te nanu.” 15Nejoki Yeremia Sedekia, “Amaa tenaaliki nanu, aimintisip iyie pee kiar nanu matua? Naa tenaaisho iyie enkikoo nimikining iyie nanu.” 16Nenya olaiguanani Sedekia olmumai te nkisudoroto te Yeremia, “Anaa enaishu Olaitoriani, laa ninye oitayio iltauja lang, maar nanu iyie tua aashu aapik atua nkaik e kulo tung'ana ooing'oru enkishui ino.”
17Nejoki Yeremia Sedekia, “Nji ejo Olaitoriani, Enkai oo lororani, Enkai e Israel, tenintayu iyie kewan aishooyo too lkituaak lo laiguanani le Babilon, neitajeuni enkishui ino, nemepejori ena anasa te nkima, ore iyie o enkaji ino nipokipoko. 18Kake tenimintayu iyie kewan aishooyo too ilkituaak ooya lo laiguanani le Babilon, neishoori ena anasa aapik atua enkaina oo Lkaldayo, naa kepejoo ninche ninye te nkima, nimisik iyie apuku te nkaina enye.” 19Nejoki olaiguanani Sedekia Yeremia, “Kaure nanu lelo Yahudi apa ooshomo aajing atua Ilkaldayo, maapuoi ake aaitarasaki ninche naamor ninche.” 20Nejoki Yeremia ninye, “Mikipuoi aishooyo te ninche, toning'o taata oltoilo lo Laitoriani teina naajoki nanu iyie, amu kelo asidanu te iyie, neitajeuni enkishui ino. 21Kake tiniany iyie intayu kewan aishooyo, ena duaata nanu aaitoduaa Olaitoriani: 22Ng'ura, ore nkituaak apa pooki naatung'uayioki tiatua enkaji o laiguanani le Yuda netorikokoki ilkituaak lo laiguanani le Babilon nejoito ninche, ‘Ore ilchoreta linono apa lisilig nikiteleja iyie nikisulaa ninche iyie; ore taata eimiso nkejek inono tiatua esarng'ab kimbelekenyate ninche aing'uaa iyie.’ 23Ore nkituaak inono pooki o layiok linono naa keyookini aaipang'ie aarikoki ilkaldayo, ore iyie enkorrok ino nimijeu te nkaina enye, kake eikimbung olaiguanani le Babilon; ore ena anasa nepejori te nkima.”
24Nejoki Sedekia Yeremia, “Mincho hoo oltung'ani eyiolou kulo rorei aashu iye iyie. 25Tenening ilkituaak nchere aairorie nanu iyie nepuonu enitii nikijoki iyie, ‘Toliki iyiook ina nitiaaka olaiguanani naa kaa kitiaka olaiguanani, misudoo hoo toki te iyiook amu mikiar iyiook iyie tua.’ 26Tiaaki iyie ninche, ‘Atasayia nanu olaiguanani te nkanyit pee maaigil ake areu enkaji e Yonatan mashomo aye teine.’ ” 27Nepuonu ilkituaak pooki enetii Yeremia neikilikuan ninye, newaliki ninche anaa enatiaka ninye olaiguanani. Nepuo ninche aaing'uaa ninye, amu ore ina kiroroto neitu apa ening'i. 28 Nebik Yeremia te nkaji e rripore o metabau ina olong apa naawaki Yerusalem.

ENAITURUK
Ore ena kiti buku dorrop e OBADIA, netisiraki te rishata naidipa atabatatayu Yerusalem to lari le 586 (B.C.) Ore peyie ebatata Yerusalem neisho emang'atinta enye musana naji Edom too larin kumok, neshipa oleng. Etijing'aka sii aretu ilo oarare ninye neyai imasaa eina anasa.
Eibonoko oloiboni le Nkai Obadia ajo ore Edom naa keitamei neisulieki tenebo lelo kulikae oshon pooki laa ilmang'ati te Israel.

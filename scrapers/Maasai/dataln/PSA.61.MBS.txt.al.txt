1  Toning'o enkishirata, iyie Enkai, inining'o enkomono ai; 

2  ore too neeiting'ie enkop naaipot nanu iyie, elepori oltau lai. Torikoyioki iyie nanu mashomo ina dapidapoi naado alang nanu; 

3  amu iyie enkisudoroto ai, iyie ewueji negol nasaru too lmang'ati laainei, oltalet ogol lagilunore ilmang'ati laainei. 

4  Nchooki nanu matobiko ti atua olng'obor lino ntarasi! Ee, pee atum ataseriana tiabori inaipuko inono! 

5  Amu itoning'o, Enkai, ilmuma laainei, kinchoo iyie nanu enjung'ore e lelo ooure enkarna ino. 

6  Ntobikoi olaiguanani; metabaiki ilarin lenyena iishoritin pooki! 

7  Meitotonieki ninye olorika le nkitoo ntarasi te dukuya Enkai; irriu enyorrata nauno o esupatisho metorripo ninye! 

8  Naa karany enkisisa e nkarna ino anaake.

1  Te neme apa Olaitoriani otii enkalo ang nchoo taa Israel ejo tena kata 

2  te neme taa apa Olaitoriani otii enkalo ang, ina kata pee edumakino iyiook iltung'ana aagilunore, 

3  anaata etoijoitie ninche iyiook kinchu, ina kata pee eyupuyup engoro enye ti alo iyiook; 

4  naa anaata etooroyie iyiook oloirrag le nkare, anaata eimisie iyiook enkare; 

5  anaata eima ilopir le nkare shumata iyiook. 

6  Meisisi Olaitoriani, amu eitu ninye eishooyo iyiook matotiu anaa eng'ues natarreshoki te rishata oo lala lenye. 

7  Kilanyishote iyiook anaa emotonyi te ilo rreshet lo larreshoni etigile orreshet, nikilanyishote iyiook. 

8  Etii eretoto ang atua enkarna o Laitoriani, laa ninye oitobira keper o enkop.

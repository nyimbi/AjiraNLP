1  Etii Enkai ewueji enye ti atua eumata enye; ore te mpolos oo nkaitin neiguana ninye: 

2  “Kenkata nabaa iyie inguana too ntorrok, nintodolu oloshomitore te ilo torrono? 

3  Nguanaki te sipata ilo omena o ilo okishi; mbung'a esipata e ilo oata osina o ilo okishi. 

4  Tokordu ilo oshal o ilo aisinani; tokordu ninche ntung'uai enkaina e ilo torrono.” 

5  Meeta ninche eng'eno nemedamisho, nepuo ninche aamanimanaa te naimin; eikirikirate ilkitamanyunot pooki le nkop. 

6   Najo nanu, “Irara intae nkaitin, nkera e ilo Otii Shumata Aisul, intae pooki; 

7  ore ake eyia, iyeye intae anaa iltung'ana, nibatatata anaa pooki tung'ani kitok.” 

8  Tudumunye, iyie Enkai, nguanare enkop; amu ilkulinono iloreren pooki!

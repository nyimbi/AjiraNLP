1  Meisisi Olaitoriani! Entaranyaki Olaitoriani osinkolio ng'ejuk, entarany enkisisa enye te umata oo supati! 

2  Nchoo Israel eshipakino ilo oitobira ninye, nchoo ilayiok le Sion eshipakino Olaiguanani lenye! 

3  Nchoo ninye eisis enkarna enye neinoe, neranyaki ninye te ntampuri o to lwulul loo usin kumok. 

4  Amu keshipakino Olaitoriani iltung'ana lenyena; neikod ninye olobor te nkisulata. 

5  Nchoo lelo supati metishipata te nkitoo; nchoo ninche eranyisho te ng'ida too ndapashi enye. 

6  Nchoo enkisisa kitok e Nkai ejing iltoiloishi lenye, nejing ilalema oopi nkaloli pokira nkaik enye, 

7  pee eitalaku elap too loshon neitame lelo lemeure Enkai, 

8  pee een ilaiguanak lenye too lpisia neen lelo kituaak lenye too sekenke, 

9  pee edung'oki ninche enkiguana naigero! Enkitoo ena te lelo pooki lenyena supati. Meisisi Olaitoriani!

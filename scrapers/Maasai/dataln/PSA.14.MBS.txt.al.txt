53  ) 

1   Ejo ilo modai to ltau lenye, “Meetae Enkai.” Etarruoitie ninche, neas mbaa o lminong, meetae hoo obo oas mbaa sidain. 

2  Eing'or Olaitoriani kop etii keper, neing'or nkera e tung'ana, pee edol tenaa ketii iloasisho te ng'eno, lelo ooing'oru Enkai. 

3  Eshomo ninche pooki aapong'ori, enyaanyuk pooki; meetae hoo obo oas isupati, meetae, metii hoo obo. 

4  Aimeeta ninche eng'eno, kulo aasak loo ntorrok pooki, ooinos iltung'ana laainei, anaa enainos ninche endaa, nemeserem ninche Olaitoriani? 

5  Ore te ine kepuo ninche aajing olkirutoto sapuk, amu etii Enkai te nebo ina ishoi e lelo supati. 

6  Inyalinyala intae nena kitobirat e ilo aisinani, kake Olaitoriani esarunoto enye. 

7  Oi siaake elotu enkordunoto e Israel eing'uaa Sion! Te neitopok Olaitoriani iltung'ana lenyena, naa keng'ida Yakobo, neshipayu Israel.

1  Te ngolon ino eng'ida olaiguanani iyie, Olaitoriani; naa te retoto ino eisulie ninye oleng. 

2  Inchoo iyie ninye eyieunoto oltau lenye neitu iyie mbooyo eomonunoto e nkutuk enye. 

3  Amu inang'are iyie ninye too mayianat sidain, ninchopoki ninye enkopiyia e nkitoo e sahabu nasila endukuya enye. 

4  Neomonu ninye enkishui te iyie, nincho iyie ninye, nkolong'i kumok ntarasi o ntarasi. 

5  Eisapuk enkitoo enye te nkaraki eretoto ino; nchopoko iyie ninye enkikodo o enkitoo. 

6  Ee, ntaa iyie ninye olomayiana oleng ntarasi, ninting'id iyie ninye te dukuya enkomom ino. 

7  Amu Olaitoriani eisilig olaiguanani; naa ore te nkaraki ina nyorrata nauno e Ilo otii Shumata Aisul, nemeigusugusuieki ninye. 

8  Ore enkaina ino ninye nalo aing'oru ilmang'ati linono pooki; neing'oru enkaina ino e tatene lelo pooki likimba. 

9  Nintotiu iyie ninche anaa olang'at le nkima te liooto ino. Keijoo Olaitoriani ninche te ngoro enye; neinos ninche enkima. 

10  Nindaikie iyie eishoi enye te nkop, niwuang'ie nkera enye aishie ti atua ltung'ana. 

11  Ti nikintobiraki ninche iyie entorroni, te neiguenu entaboi nemerasaa ninche. 

12  Amu ncho iyie ninche mekueta aailanyisho, niimul iyie nkomomi enye too mbaa inono. 

13  Nchoo kisisi iyie Olaitoriani, te nkaraki engolon ino! Eikiranyisho iyiook nikisis engolon ino.

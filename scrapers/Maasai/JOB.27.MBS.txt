Ewalata e Yoab
1Neitoki ake Yoab airo nejo:
2“Ore anaa enaishu Enkai, ilo laaitung'uayie esipata ai,
o ilo Magilani, ilo otipika oltau lai emion;
3ore ake ninye aishu,
netii enkiyang'et Enkai nkumeishin aainei,
4mairo te nkutuk ai mbaa e lejare,
nemeibalie olng'ejep lai enemesipa.
5Metalakuana te nanu elimunoto nchere, itisipa intae
ore omatua, nemaiwuang'ie aekata enkitashoto ai e sipata.
6Ekaibung akurraki esinyatisho ai, nemaitiak,
maaitukurru oltau lai too nkolong'i aainei pooki.
7“Inchoo olmang'atinta lai eaku oltorrono,
nincho ilo laadumakino aarare eaku olarruoni.
8Amaa kainyoo osiligi le ilo lemeure Enkai
teneiwuang'ie Enkai ninye,
teneiwuang'ie Enkai enkishui enye?
9Kening Enkai enkishirata enye,
tenebaiki ninye enyamali?
10Keshipakino ninye ilo Magilani?
Keipot ninye Enkai pooki kata?
11Ekaaiteng'en nanu iyie enkipirta enkaina e Enkai;
ore pooki natii atua Olmagilani nemaisudoo.
12Ng'ura, indipa pooki aatodol maate;
kainyoo naa pee itaa pooki ilooiro mbaa e pesho?
13“Ena erubata oltung'ani torrono te Nkai,
naa ina enjung'ore nang'amu ilooitorogonyisho te ilo Magilani:
14Teneponari inkera enyena, naa kemut olalem,
ore eishoi enye nemetum endaa naraposh.
15Ore lelo ootelekutua neye to lmeitai
ore nkolia enye nemeidim aaishira.
16Ore hoo neiturrurr enkosholai anaa enterit,
nerup inkilani anaa esarng'ab;
17eidim aiturrurro, kake lelo supati ooishop,
ore lelo lemeeta entioto neor enkosholai.
18Ore enkaji nashet ninye netiu anaa enkaji o lkedi,
anaa olng'obor oitobiru olarriponi.
19Elo ninye endapash aa karsis,
neyooki ainyototo eisha karsisisho enye
ebol inkonyek enyena,
nenyorriki eshomo enkarsisisho enye.
20Eipang'aki ninye oloputukunyi anaa olairrag le nkare;
ore kewarie nenap ninye osiwuo sapuk.
21Edumu ninye olkutati lo loo saen neya;
eoroo ninye aitung'uaa ewueji enye.
22Ekekut olkutati ninye meeta olng'ur
ina kata etem ninye ailany engolon enye.
23Keoshoki olkutati ninye nkaik,
neiture ninye aitupuku te wueji enye.

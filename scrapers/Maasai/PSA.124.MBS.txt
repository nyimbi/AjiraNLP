Enkai olarriponi loo ltung'ana lenyena
1Te neme apa Olaitoriani otii enkalo ang
nchoo taa Israel ejo tena kata
2te neme taa apa Olaitoriani otii enkalo ang,
ina kata pee edumakino iyiook iltung'ana aagilunore,
3anaata etoijoitie ninche iyiook kinchu,
ina kata pee eyupuyup engoro enye ti alo iyiook;
4naa anaata etooroyie iyiook oloirrag le nkare,
anaata eimisie iyiook enkare;
5anaata eima ilopir le nkare shumata iyiook.
6Meisisi Olaitoriani,
amu eitu ninye eishooyo iyiook
matotiu anaa eng'ues natarreshoki te rishata oo lala lenye.
7Kilanyishote iyiook anaa emotonyi te ilo rreshet lo larreshoni
etigile orreshet, nikilanyishote iyiook.
8Etii eretoto ang atua enkarna o Laitoriani,
laa ninye oitobira keper o enkop.

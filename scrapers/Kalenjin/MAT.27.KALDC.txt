Kimutu Jeso taitap Pilato
(Mar 15:1; Luk 23:1-2; Jhn 18:28-32)
1Ye kingo kagoeech, korwooch tisiik tugul che eecheen ak poisyegap pichooto, korwookyi ole partoi Jeso. 2Ki rat ineendet, koomut ak ipkogoochi Pilato pounindet.
Meetap Judas
(Poi 1:18-19)
3 Ye kingo keer Judas anyun kole kagirwookyi Jeso, komwa ndara ak koweekyi tisiik che eecheen ak poisyek fedhaisyechooto sosom. 4Ki mwa kole, “Kaalel amu kaang'wekte korotiik che ma tinyei tengek.” Ago ki wol icheek kole, “Tiing'enech nee ng'aleeguk? Rar inyegei.” 5Ki wiirchi fedhaisyechooto Hekaluut, komanda ak kwo kogeetkei. 6Ak ki nam tisiik che eecheen fedhaisyechooto ak kole, “Ma chamdaat eng' ng'atuutyet kituiyo chu ak konunoigap Hekaluut amu po koroti.” 7Ki irwooch ak kotil koboisyee fedhaisyechooto koale mbareetap cheptereniot si koek ole kitupchin toek. 8Amu nooto anyun ko ki kiguuree mbaranooto mbareetap korotiik agoi rani. 9 Ki isulda anyun ne ki kagimwa eng' maotyot Jeremiah keele, “Ki ip icheek fedhaisyek sosom, alyetap ineendet ne kiga kigaamanji, ne kigaaman piigap Israel, 10ak ki igoito icheek koale mbareet ne po cheptereniot ko uu ye ki kang'ata Kiptaiyat.”
Koteepseen `Pilato Jeso
(Mar 15:2-5; Luk 23:3-5; Johana 18:33-38)
11Ki tonon Jeso anyun eng' taitap pounindet ak kiteebe pounindet koleenji, “Ii Laitoryatap Yahudik?” Koleenji Jeso, “Kaimwa.” 12Ago ye kingo taam ineendet tisiik che eecheen ak poisyek, ko mawol kiy. 13Komwachi anyun Pilato ineendet koleenji, “Megase ole tee paornateet ne paoryanjinin pichu?” 14Ago mawolji kiy, agot ng'olyoo agenge kot kokwong' miising' pounindet.
Barabbas anan Jeso?
(Mar 15:6-15; Luk 23:13-25; Jhn18:39-19:16)
15Ki cham kotyakyin pounindet tuiyet kimonjororyot agenge ne kacham icheek eng' peetuutap igoorto. 16Eng' kasarata, kogimitei kimonjororyot age ne kinaat kot ne ki kiguure Barabbas. 17Ye kingo kagoyumgei anyun, koleenji Pilato, “Ng'oo ne omache atyakwok? Barabbas anan Jeso ne kiguure Kristo?” 18Ki ingen kole kagigoito ineendet eng' ng'etkonda.
19Ye kingo ta kotononjin kirwooget, koyookyi osotyonnyi ng'olyoo kole, “Ameite kiy chiichoo ma tinyei leluut, amu kaanyooru nyalilwogik che chaang' rani eng' karwotitoet agobo ineendet.” 20Kisaisai tisiik che eecheen ak poisyek tuiyosyek kole kosoom kityaach Barabbas ak kebar Jeso. 21Ki leenji pounindet icheek kogeny, “Omache atyakwok ng'oo eng' chu aeng'?” Kole icheek, “Barabbas.” 22Koleenji Pilato, “Alyonji anyun Jeso ne kiguure Kristo?” Kole icheek tugul, “Ingigartaat ineendet!” 23koteebee koleenji, “Amu nee? Kayai nee ne ya?” Ago ki polyo miising' koleen, “Ingigartaat!”
24 Ye kingo keer Pilato kole mata komuuch koyai kiy ago ki neegit kotoigei chemargeet, konam peek ak koun eunekyik eng' taitap tuiyet koleen, “Ma tinyei leluut agobo korotiigap chiichi po iman. Orarati okweegei.” 25Ki wol piik tugul kole, “Ingotebenech korotiikyik ak lagokyok.” 26Ki ityakyi Barabbas ak ye kingo kagobir `Pilato Jeso, kogoito ineendet kigartaat.
Kochotei `kiplugotinik Jeso
(Mar 15:16-20; Jhn 19:2-3)
27Ki imut anyun kiplugotinigap pounindet Jeso kwo Praitorium, ak koyumjigei ineendet kiplugotinik tugul. 28Ki ireegune ngoroikyik ak kolaakyi sambut ne piriir. 29Kitaa ngatyetap katook ak konde metinnyi. Ki inde tegat eunnyi ne po tai ak ko kutung'so eng' tainnyi ak kochotei koleen, “Kigatin, Laitoryatap Yahudik!” 30Kising'ilji ineendet ak konam tegat kokwere metinnyi. 31Ye kingo kagochot ineendet, koreegune sambut ak kolaakyi ineendet ngoroikyik, yeityo komut kwo kigartaat.
Kigartati Jeso
(Mar 15:21-31; Luk 23:26-43; Jhn 19:17-27)
32Ye ki mi kobendi, konyoor chiitap Kurene ne kiguure Simion ak kobirchi ineendet kola kimurtoiyondennyi. 33Ye kingo it ole kiguure Golgota, ne kaguiyennyi ko emetap terta, 34 kogoochi ineendet siki koe che ki kiburukto tuguuk che ng'woneen, ak ye kingo chamcham, koeesyo koe.
35 Kingo kagogartaat ineendet, kopchechigei ngoroikyik eng' seretap parbareek, 36ak kotep ng'wony koriip ineendet eng' olooto. 37Ki inde paragutap metinnyi kataamset ne ki kisir keele, “Ni ko Jeso Laitoryatap Yahudik.” 38Ki kigartaat ineendet ak rebiik aeng' eng' yooto, age eng' euut ne po tai ak age eng' euut ne po kaataam.
39 Kichurachur ineendet che kisiirtoi ak koleglegoti metowekwak 40 koleen, “Inyee ne ing'eme Hekaluut ak iteech eng' peetuusyek somok, sarugei! Ngo ii Weeriitap Kamuktaindet, ichoorugei eng' kimurtoiyot.” 41Kichurachur ko uu nooto ak icheek tisiik che eecheen kobooto siriik ak poisyek koleen, 42“Ki sar alak; ama imuuchi kosargei. Ineendet Laitoryatap Israel; ingochoorgei nguno eng' kimurtoiyot, si kiyan ineendet. 43 Imang'u Kamuktaindet; ingosar ineendet nguno ngot komachei amu ki le, ‘Aa Weeriitap Kamuktaindet.’ ” 44Kichurochur ak icheek ko uu nooto rebiik che kiga kigartaat kobooto ineendet.
Meetap Jeso
(Mar 15:33-41; Luk 23:44-49; Jhn 19:28-30)
45Ki imenit anyun emet komugul kong'etee sait lo agoi sait sogool. 46 Ye kingo olo po sait sogool, koguurso Jeso eng' tuugeet ne oo kole, “Eli, Eli, lama sabaktani?” Nooto ko, “Kamuktaindet nenyu, Kamuktaindet nenyu, kalya si ibagaakta?” 47Kingo kas alak eng' icheek che kitonondos eng' olooto, kole icheek, “Kuurei chiichi Elijah.” 48 Eng' yooto kolabat age eng' icheek kwo konem sponjit ak konyiit siki, ak konde tegat ak kogoochi koe. 49Ki mwa chuun alak kole, “Omete, ongegeer ngo nyoonei Elijah si kosar ineendet.” 50Ki kuurso Jeso kogeny eng' kutit ne oo, ak kogoito saboondanyi.
51 Ogeero, ki kereer angeet ne ki kioiye Hekaluut koek aeng' kobooch parak agoi ng'wony, ak kobetogis rwandosyek. 52Ki yaatagis kereeriisyek ak ki king'eet poorwegap piik che chaang' che tiliileen che ki ruitos. 53Kingo kagong'eet Jeso, komong' pichooto ak icheek eng' kereeriisyekwak komong'ji nganaseet ne tiliil ak koboorchigei che chaang'.
54Ye kingo keer chemogetap kiplugotinik pogol ak icheeget che ki mi kobooto ineendet koriibei Jeso potaneetap ng'wony, ak tuguuk che kayaagiis, koywei miising' ak kole, “Iman Weeriitap Kamuktaindet chiichi.”
55 Ki mi eng' olooto kobogorisyek che chaang' che kigeerei eng' ye loo, che kiga isubot Jeso kobooch Galilaya koboisyechinoti ineendet. 56Ki Maria Magdalene age eng' kobogorisyechooto kobooto Maria kametap Jakobo ak Joses, ak kametap weeriigap Zebedayo.
Konoretap poortap Jeso
(Mar 15:42-47; Luk 23:50-56; Jhn 19:38-42)
57Kingo kagoek koskoleng', konyo mogoryot kobun Arimatea ne keguure Joseph ne ki kasubindetap Jeso agine. 58Ki wo chiichooto ole mi Pilato, kwo kosoom poortap Jeso; ak ki ng'at Pilato anyun kole kigoochi. 59Ki ip Joseph poorto ak kotaae angeetap katanit ne tiliil, 60ak konde kererinnyi ne leel ne kiga kobal eng' rwandet. Kimilji koita ne oo kurgeetap kererit kogeree yeityo kwo. 61Ki mi yooto Maria Magdalene ak Maria age kotebyei eng' taitap kererit.
Mageetap keriip kererit
62Eng' peetuut ne ki isubi, ko kaibata peetuutap chobeet, koyumgei tisiik che eecheen ak Pharisaik eng' taitap Pilato 63 ak koleenji, “Kiptaiyat, kibwaati keele ki mwa kaberberindonooto kota kosabei kole, ‘Ye ibata peetuusyek somogu, ang'eete kogeny.’ 64Ng'at anyun keriip kererit komyee agoi peetuut ne po somok, si ma pwa kasubikyik kochoor ineendet ak koip, ak komwachi piik koleenji, ‘Kagong'eet ineendet eng' che ki kobek,’ si koyait kaberberisyet ne po let kosiir ne po tai.” 65Koleenji Pilato icheek, “Otinye riibiik, obechigei ak oriip komyee ko uu ye omuuchi.” 66Koba anyun ak ribiik koriip kererit; king'ooch koita ak koriip kererit komyee.

Ng'aleek che indou
Mwaiwech kitabuuni agobo kwonyiik che ki po Moab che ki pegunee manong'otoik. Kwonyichooto ko ki Ruth ak Orpa che ki meng'iisyei ak korgennywa po yugoi ne ki kiguuree Naomi, ne agot agine ko kiga komee manong'otyot. Ko uu ye kiga kobegunee manong'otoik Ruth ak Orpah, ko kimach korgennywa po yugoi kotyakta icheek kole koweektagei age tugul koinnywa. Inga no, ko ki eesyo Ruth.
Ki naak anyun ineendet agobo tononennyi ne ki eesyo ak mwaet ne ki mwachi Naomi koleenji “Amegimnjo ile abagachin anan ile aweek matkebe tuwai! Ole kewe, awendi, ak ole itebee atebee agane. Egu piiguk piikyuk, ak egu Kamuktaindeng'ung' Kamuktaindennyu. Ole imechini, amechini, ak ketupchino yooto. Ingoyaiywo Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ko uu nooto ak kotes. Meet ineegei ne ibestoseech anee ak inyee.” (1:16-17)

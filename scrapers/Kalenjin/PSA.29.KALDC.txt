Tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ye mi wasetapkei
Tyendap Kalasunet ne po Daudi.
1 Ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ee okweek weeriigap che kiimeen,
ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor teegisto ak kimnateet.
2Ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor teegisto ne nyoljin kainennyi;
ogutung'ji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' telunatetap tiliilindo.
3Miitei tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor nyanjosyek;
tulei Kamuktaindetap teegisto,
nooto Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ne mi paragutap peek che chaang'.
4Tinyei kimnateet tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor;
nyiitaat `etunateet tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.
5Iriei `tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor taragonik;
tonatoni `Kamuktaindet ne Toroor taragonik che po Lebanon.
6Igoochin `Kamuktaindet ne Toroor tuloonoogap Lebanon kotorombenso ko uu moita;
ak tulweetap Sirion, ko uu moitap soet.
7Petei tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor laletap maat.
8Itumdumi tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor surkwen;
itumdumi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor surkwen ne po Kadesh.
9Igoochin tweguunetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kenyelok koiiyo,
ak chorori keetiigap timiin;
ak mwaei age tugul ne mi hekalunnyi kole,
“Po Kamuktaindet ne Toroor Toroornatet!”
10Tebee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor maraneet ko uu laitoryat;
tebyei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kolaitorani koigeny.
11Igoochin `Kamuktaindet ne Toroor piikyik kimnateet!
Ingoberuuri `Kamuktaindet ne Toroor piikyik eng' kaalyeet!

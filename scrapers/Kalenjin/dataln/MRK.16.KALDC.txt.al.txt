1  Ak kingo kagosiirto sabato koal Maria Magdalene, ak Maria kametap Jakobo, ak Salome, nyamdutik, si kobwa koiil ineendet. 

2  Ak eng' karon inne, eng' peetuutap tai ne po wigiit, koit kererit ko kachor asiista. 

3  Ak kong'alaaljigei icheegei kole, “Mildaiywech ng'oo koita eng' kurgeetap kererit?” 

4  Ak kingo ng'eet met, kogeer ko kagemilda koita: amu ki oo miising'. 

5  Ak kingo chut kererit, kogeer muren kotebyei eng' komostap tai, ne ilaachi ngoryeet ne leel; ak ki kwong'yo. 

6  Ak ki leenji icheek, “Amokwong'ye; ocheeng'e Jeso, Nazaretindet, ne kiga kigartaat: kagong'eet; ma komi yu: ogeer ole kiga kinde ineendet! 

7   Ago, oba, omwachi kasubikyik ak Petero, oleenji, Kagondoenak kwo Galilaya: ogeere ineendet eng' olooto, ko uu ye kiga komwaiwok.” 

8  Ak kimanda saang' eng' kererit, ak komwei; amu kiga potanso ak kokwong'yo: ak ma mwachi chii kiy; amu ki iyweis. Koboorchingei Jeso Maria Magdalene (Mat

9  Ak kingo ng'eet Jeso anyun karon miising' eng' peetuutap tai ne po wigiit, kochunji korook Maria Magdalene, ineendet ne kiga kocherunee Jeso oik tisap. 

10  Ki wo komwachi icheeget che ki mi kobooto ineendet, ye kita koarongendos ak koriirtos. 

11  Ak kingo kas icheek kole sabei, ak kole, kagogeer ineendet chebyosonooto, koma iyan. Koboorchingei Jeso kasubik aeng' (Luk

14  Ak kingo pata nooto, kong'aang'jigei icheeget taman ak aeng' kotebyei eng' amisyet; ak koger kong' amu pistoennywa ak uuindap mugulelwekwak, amu ki ma iyan icheeget che ki keer ineendet ko kagong'eet. 

15   Ak ki leenji, “Oba ng'wony komugul, oamjinote logoiywek che myach piik tugul. 

16  Ineendet ne iyani ak kibatisan kesaru; ago ineendet ne ma iyani ko tuun kirwookyin. 

17  Ak isubi kaboorunoichu icheek che iyani: oonei oik eng' kainennyu; ak kong'alaali ng'alaalet ne leel;

19   Ye kingo kagong'alaal ak icheeget `Kiptaiyat Jeso, kimut anyun ineendet kwo kipsengwet, ak kotebee eutap tai ne po Kamuktaindet. 

20  Ak ki pa anyun, koamdaat eng' oldo tugul, kobois Kiptaiyat kobooto icheek, ak kogimit ng'olyoot eng' kaboorunoik che ki isuboti.

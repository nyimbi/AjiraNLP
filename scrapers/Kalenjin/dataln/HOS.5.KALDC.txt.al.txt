1  Ogas niito, ee okweek tisiik! Oep iit, okweek kootap Israel! Ogas komyee, ee kootap laitoryat, amu nyoonjinok kirwooget; amu ki oegu mesteet eng' Mispa, ak mesteet ne uu regereiywot ne kiitoi eng' Tabor. 

2  Ki kotes miising' `pistoik parisyet; ago agere kong' icheek tugul. 

3  Angen Ephraim, ama ung'aat Israel eng' anee. Amu koiyai kugoinatet inyee, ee, Ephraim, ki kong'wanit Israel. 

4  Poisyonikwak koma chamjin icheek koweekyigei Kamuktaindennywa amu tinyei sabeetap kugoinatet, ama ingen Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 

5  Ak paoryani menoteetap Israel eng' tainnyi; ibutos anyun Israel ak Ephraim eng' tengektonywa; ibuti Judah agine kobooto icheek. 

6  Pendi icheek kobooto akwotisyekwak che po keechiirek ak che po tuuga kocheeng'ei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; ago ma nyooru ineendet, kigoistoegei `ineendet icheek. 

7  Kigoberber `icheek Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; amu kigosich lagok che kipsagarek. Amei `arawet ne kaleel icheek kobooto mbareenikwak. Judah ak Israel 

8  Oguut igoondit eng' Gibea, ak oguut iryageet eng' Ramah. Owaach eng' Beth-aben; keer ole po let eng' inyee, ee Benjamin. 

9  Egu karaatya Ephraim eng' peetuutap kereetap kong'; ki abooru eng' kwenutap pororyosyegap Israel kiit ne tuun piitu. 

10  Uu pounigap Judah piik che inaktoe kiwoteet. Aroong'jini icheek nereegekyuk ko uu peek. 

11  Kisastoe Ephraim, kityekyin ng'wony eng' kirwooget; amu ki chamei koosup ng'atuutyet. 

12  Auu anyun eng' Ephraim kiptorotyot, ak auu nunateet eng' kootap Judah. 

13  Kingo keer Ephraim myandanyi, ak kogeer Judah mookyik, ko ki wo anyun Ephraim Assyria, ak koyookto keguur laitoryat Jareb. Ago ma imuuchi kosoobok, amainyaiywok mookwok. 

14  Amu aigeitu ng'etundo eng' Ephraim, ak aigeitu ng'etundo ne neran eng' kootap Judah. Agereeri aneendet ak awe; amuti ama piitu chii ne saru. 

15  Aweekyinigei anyun oldanyu, agoi kong'aany chalwoktanywa ak kocheeng'a. Eng' nyalildanywa ko cheeng'a icheek eng' kagiiletapkei.

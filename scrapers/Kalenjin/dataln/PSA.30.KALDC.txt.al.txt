1  Alasun, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, amu ki ieeta, ama igoochi puunikyuk kogasenagei. 

2  Ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindennyu, ki ariirchin, ak ki isooba. 

3  Ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ki imoong'u saboondanyu eng' ye mi meet; ki igoono asap, si ma we keriinget. 

4  Otyenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kalasunet, okweek che oo chechiik che tiliileen, oweekyi kongoi kainennyi ne tiliil. 

5  Tebyei nereegekyik ne matya; ichuldos chametap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' sabeetap chii. Mi riirek kemout, ago piitu poiboiyet karon. 

6  Ak ki ale eng' perurtanyu, “Ma kiistaa.” 

7  Eng' chameng'ung', ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ko ki igoono atonon ko uu tulweet ne kiim. Ago ye ki iuny togeng'ung'; ko ki aiyweisye. 

8  Ki ariirchin, inyee, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; ak ki ariirchin ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor: 

9  “Itinyei keljino nee ye kaamei, ye kaawe keriinget? Ilasun temburyek? Tos imuuchi kobarta imandang'ung'? 

10  Kas, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ak rirenagei; egu toretindennyu, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor!” 

11  Ki iwal kateennyu koek ureryet; ki ireegunena kuniet, ak ilaakwa poiboiyet. 

12  Si kobiit kolasun saboondanyun ama siisei. Ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor Kamuktaindennyu, aweegun kongoi koigeny.

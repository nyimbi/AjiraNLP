1  Nda ki ma ko Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ne ki mi komostanyo, ingomwa Israel nguno kole 

2  nda ki ma ko Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ne ki mi komostanyo, ye king'eetyinech piik, 

3  ta ki koluguech kesaptosi, ye kilaldos nereegekwak agobo acheek; 

4  kita imaluuchech peek, kita roote aineet saboonwekyok; 

5  kita roote saboonwekyok peek che wasegei. 

6  Iberuurot Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ne ki ma igoitech kibutyi kelekwak. 

7  Chilildos saboonwekyok ko uu ye chilili toriityon eng' mesteetap kiplogotinik che parei toriitik; kagong'et mesteet, ak kagechililse. 

8  Miitei toretennyo kainetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ne ki yai kipsengwet ak ng'wony.

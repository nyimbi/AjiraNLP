1  Aguurin, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor! Ameger itiiguk, rwandennyu, eng' komostanyu, amu, ngot isiis eng' anee, aige icheek che pendi keriinget. 

2  Kas soomutikyuk, ye ariirchinin, ye anamjini parak eunekyuk atokyini oldang'ung' ne tiliil. 

3  Amewiirta kobooto kiptengeginik, anan kobooto che yaei yaityo; che mwachingei kaalyeet ak piigap kokwet, ago tinyei suryet eng' mugulelwekwak. 

4   Igoochi ko uu poisyonikwak, ko uu ole tee yaetap yautikwak. Igoochi ko uu poisyetap eunekwak; yookyi che nyoljin. 

5  Amu ma mage kiy poisyonigap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, anan poisyetap eunekyik, putei `Kamuktaindet ne Toroor icheek ama koteechei kogeny. 

6  Iberuurot Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, amu ki kogaswa soomutikyuk. 

7  Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ko kimnateennyu ak long'ennyu; imang'u muguleldanyu ineendet. Kigetoreta ak igasegei muguleldanyu miising'; alasu ineendet eng' tyendanyu. 

8  Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ko kimnateetap piikyik; ineendet irimeetap yetunetap nenyinet ne ki kiil. 

9  Saru piiguk, ak iberuur poroteng'ung'; pai icheek, ak imutate agoi koigeny.

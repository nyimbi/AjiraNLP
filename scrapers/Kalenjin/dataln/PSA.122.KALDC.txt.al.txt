1  Ki abaibaitu ye kimwaiwo icheek kole, “Ongebe kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.” 

2  Tonondos keelyekyok eng' ormariichoguk, ee Jerusalem. 

3  Jerusalem, ne teegat ko uu nganaseet ne tuiyot. 

4  Ole togoste pororyosyek, pororyosyegap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, koek paornateet eng' Israel, si komwa kongoi agobo kainetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 

5  Amu ki kinde olooto ng'echerogap laitornatet che po kirwooget, ng'echerogap laitornatet che po kootap Daudi. 

6  Osoomji kaalyeet Jerusalem: “Nyooru poroteet icheek che chamin.” 

7  Ingotebee kaalyeet irimoiguk, ak ingotebee poroteet kooriguk che eecheen. 

8  Amu tupchosyekyuk ak choronokyuk, amwae nguno ale, “Ingotebenen kaalyeet.” 

9  Amu kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindennyo acheeng'un myeeindo.

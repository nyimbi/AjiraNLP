1  Igeni Kamuktaindet kityo saboondanyu, punu yetunennyu ineendet. 

2  Ineendet ineegei rwandennyu ak yetunennyu, ineendet ko irimennyu ne kooi; ma ki istoa miising'. 

3  Opookyini chii kot kotyanit, ole si kobiit obar ineendet, okweek tugul, ko uu inaatut ne kogoimom, ak ko uu ng'ootweet ne neegit koluul? 

4  Irwoochi kityo komachei kotoorta ineendet amu telunatennyi; poiboi eng' lembechek. Iberuuri eng' kutusweekwak, ago sasisyei eng' mugulelwekwak. 

5  Ee saboondanyu, igen Kamuktaindet kityo; amu punu kamang'unennyu ineendet. 

6  Ineendet ineegei rwandennyu ak yetunennyu, irimennyu ne kooi ineendet; ma ki istoa. 

7  Tinyei Kamuktaindet yetunennyu ak toroornatennyu; rwandetap kimnateennyu ak ole amwechini ko Kamuktaindet. 

8  Ee okweek pichu, omang'u ineendet kotugul; oroong'ji mugulelwekwok ineendet, Kamuktaindet ole kemwechini. 

9  Iman puch piik che nyerootiin, ak kiplembechonik piik che taagunotiin. Ma ing'eeti kiit ne kigeeree nyigiisindo, wisisen icheek tugul kosiir kabuuset. 

10  Amomang'u sastaet, amomene rebisyet; ngo tesak mogornatosyek ko mat otiegei. 

11  Kitwek Kamuktaindet konyil agenge, ki agas nooto konyil aeng': ale po kimnateet Kamuktaindet; 

12   Ee Kiptaiyat, neng'ung' rireenet agine; amu inyee ne iyaakyini chii tugul ko uu poisyennyi.

1   Ariirchini Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' kutinnyu; atwekyini Kamuktaindet ne Toroor aipchini soomennyu. 

2  Aroong'jini tainnyi riirekyuk; aboortoi eng' tainnyi nyalildanyu. 

3  Ye kingo tanui saboondanyu, inai ooreennyu. Kigouny icheek mesteet agobo anee eng' ooreet ne abune. 

4  Kusyi kong' eunnyu ne po tai, ak igeer; amu ma mi chii ne ingeno; ki kobetwa ole amwechini, ma mi chii ne riibei saboondanyu. 

5  Ariirchin inyee, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; ki amwa ale, “Ii oldanyu ole amwechini, ii kebebertanyu eng' emetap che saptos.” 

6  Epchi iit riirekyuk, amu kinyerata miising'; sarwo eng' usiikyuk, amu kiimeen kosiiro. 

7  Imoong'u saboondanyu eng' korgoreet, si aweekyi kongoi kaineng'ung'. Yiema che po iman komoswekyuk tugul, amu iyaiywo eng' aiyebindo.

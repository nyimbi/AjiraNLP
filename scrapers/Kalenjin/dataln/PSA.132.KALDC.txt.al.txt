1  Ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ibwaat agobo Daudi ibwaat nyalilwogikyik tugul che ki nyoor; 

2  Ko uu ye ki iyasyechi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ak ki iarorchi Ineendet Ne po Kamuktaet ne po Jakobo, koleenji: 

3  “Iman, ma wendi emait oriit ne po koonnyu, anan atogoste itogunnyu; 

4  ma kere konyekyuk eng' rwondo, anan anuuch; 

5  agoi acheeng'ji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor oldo, meng'ootetap Ineendet Ne po Kamuktaet ne po Jakobo.” 

6   Ogeero, kigegas agobo nooto eng' Ephratah; ki kinyoorchi keeweet ne mi timiin. 

7  Kibendi meng'otosyekyik; kigutunyi eng' ng'echereetap keelyekyik keel. 

8  Ng'eet, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ui ole imuung'ee; inyee ak moingetap kimnateng'ung'. 

9  Ingolaach tisiiguk imanda; ak ingogaskei eng' kutit ne oo eng' poiboiyet cheguuk che tiliileen. 

10  Amewelel togeetap neng'ung' ne iilaat amu kiboityondeng'ung' Daudi. 

11   Ki iyasyechi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor Daudi eng' iman; ma weechegei let: Andoi age ne punu poortang'ung' kotebee ng'echereeng'ung' ne po pounatet. 

12  Ngot koriip lagoguk arorutyennyu ak pounatennyu ne tuun aneeti, kotebee anyun lagokwak ak icheek ng'echereeng'ung' ne po pounateet agoi koigeny. 

13  Amu ki kolewen Kamuktaindet ne Toroor Zion; kimakyigei koek ole tebee. 

14  Yuto ko komosta ole amuung'e koigeny; atebee yuto; amu ki amach. 

15  Aberuuri miising' amitwogikyik; abae kibananookyik amitwogik kot kobiyong'so. 

16  Alaakyini tisiikyik ak icheek yetunet; ak kogaskei eng' kutit ne oo eng' poiboiyet chechiik che tiliileen. 

17   Ayae eng' olooto kuinetap Daudi kong'at; ki achop tait agobo nenyu ne iilat. 

18  Alaakyini puunikyik kalilanet; ago tuneni eng' ineendet naaryeennyi ne po pounateet.

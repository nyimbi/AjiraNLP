1  Ki wol anyun Zophar Naamatindet kole 

2  tos ma nyoljin kewol ng'aleechu chaang'? Tos ki ityin imanda chii ne chaang' ng'ala? 

3  Tos igoochin piik kosiisyo kachaunasyeguk pogei? Ye ichotisyei, ko tos ma mi ne koonin ililan? 

4  Amu imwae ile, tiliileen kaneetisyosyekyuk, ak aa tiliil eng' taing'ung'. 

5  Kata myee kong'alaal Kamuktaindet, ak kong'alaal kogirinden, 

6  ak koboorun ung'ootik che po utaatyet, amu utaatyet ko tinyei kebeberwek che chaang'. Nai anyun ile ma magenen Kamuktaindet ne tee ne yamegei ak yaitwogiguk. 

7  “Tos imuuchi inyooru Kamuktaindet ngisyach? Tos imuuchi inyooru ole poochin Ineendet Ne po Kamuktaet? 

8  Toroor kosiir kipsengwet, imuuchi iyai nee? Loo kosiir ye mi che ki kobek; nee ne imuuchi inai? 

9  Kakwautyennyi ko kooi kosiir ng'wony, ak ko tebees kosiir nyanjet. 

10  Ngot kosiirto inee, ak kogeris, ak kuguurso keba kirwooget, ko ng'oo ne imuuchi kogirinda ineendet? 

11  Ingen `ineendet piik che ma tilei kiy; ye keerei ineendet tengekto, ko tos ma mage kiy? 

12  Ago ma tinyei kaguiyet chiito ne ma tilei kiy, kisichei chii ko uu moitap oloitigeet. 

13  Ngot ichop muguleldang'ung' kolititiit, ak iitoochi ineendet euneguk, 

14  ngo mi tengekto eung'ung', iwiirte kolooit, ameng' komeny emaisyeguk kiy ne ma po imanda. 

15  Ngiyai ko uu nooto, itokyi anyun parak togeng'ung' koma mi tisya; ee, igimitu, ameiyweisyei. 

16  Iutye nyalildang'ung', ibwaati ko uu peek che ki kosiirto. 

17  Lapkeitu sabeeng'ung' kosiir peet kween; agot ngo mi mesundeito kogergeitu ak karon. 

18  Ak itebi eng' nyiganateet, amu mi kamang'unet; iriiboksei, ak imuuny eng' chametapkei. 

19  Irue anyun, ama piitu ne imuin; ee, cheeng'in che chaang' agobo toreteeng'ung'. 

20  Ago keroksei konyegap kiptengeginik, petyin icheeget ooreet ne mwee, ak kamang'unennywa ko kabuset ne po let.”

1   Ep iit, ee Mestowotap Israel, Inyee ne indochini Joseph ko uu akweetap keechiirek; Iluu, inyee ne itebee kerubisyek. 

2  Ing'eet kimnateng'ung' eng' taitap Ephraim, ak Benjamin, ak Manasseh, ak inyoo isarwech. 

3  Ee Kamuktaindet, weektech kogeny; ak ingokweng'is togeng'ung', si kesarwech. 

4  Ee Kamuktaindet ne po Toroornatet ne po lugoosyek, inereekyini saaetap piiguk kot kotyanit? 

5  Ki ibae icheek amitwogigap peegap kong', ak ki inage peegap kong' koee eng' kerutyet ne oo. 

6  Iyaech keegu che moitoseech pororyosyek che neegiteenech: ak roritos puunikyok icheegei eng' icheegei. 

7  Ee Kamuktaindet ne po lugoosyek, weektech; Ak ingokweng'is togeng'ung', si kesarwech. 

8  Ki ibu inyee sabibuk eng' Misiri: ki ioon pororyosyek, ak imin sabibunooto. 

9  Kisip ichopchi poroindo, Ak ki pa ng'wony oriit tigitikyik, ak koyom emet. 

10  Ki iuur urwennyi tuloonook, Ak ki kergei mooriikyik ak taragonigap Kamuktaindet. 

11  Ki iwaakta mooriikyik kot koit nyanjetap Mediterenian, Ak kong'atos che ng'orng'or kot koit aineet. 

12  Amu nee si ibut ng'otonokyik, Si kobut age tugul ne siirtoi logoekyik? 

13  Ng'emei icheek toret ne punu timiin, Ak koamei tyong'igap warenget. 

14  Kisaain, acheek ee Kamuktaindet ne po lugoosyek, keele, iweege: Keer oli eng' kipsengwet, ak igeer, ak irutoochi sabibuniito, 

15  Ak keetit ne ki imiine eung'ung' ne po tai, Ak temeet ne ki igimityige. 

16  Kigebeel eng' maat, ak kigeep: Lustos eng' kereetap kong' ne po togeng'ung'. 

17  Ingotebee eung'ung' chiitap eung'ung' ne po tai, Eng' weeriitap chii ne ki igimityige. 

18  Si ma kegetyinige let kemetoin: Isoobech, si keguur kaineng'ung'. 

19  Ee Kamuktaindet ne po Toroornatet ne po lugoosyek, weektech kogeny; Ingokweng'is togeng'ung', si kesarwech.

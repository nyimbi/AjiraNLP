Amandla kaMoridekayi
1Inkosi u-Ahashiveroshi yathelisa izwe neziqhingi zolwandle. 2Zonke izenzo zegunya lakhe namandla akhe, nayo yonke indaba yobukhulu bukaMoridekayi inkosi eyayimkhulisela kubo angithi kulotshiwe encwadini yezindaba zemihla ngemihla yamakhosi aseMediya nasePheresiya na? 3Ngokuba uMoridekayi, umJuda, wayelama inkosi u-Ahashiveroshi ngobukhulu, emkhulu phakathi kwabaJuda, ethokozelwa ngabafowabo abaningi, efuna okuhle kwesizwe sakubo, ekhuluma ukuthula kuyo yonke inzalo yabo.

Enkisisa te nkaraki esupatisho e Nkai
1Te neitu eshet Olaitoriani enkaji,
ore lelo ooshet pesho eekenya.
Te neitu errip Olaitoriani enkanasa,
ore lelo oorrip pesho eshololo.
2Te mpesho inyototo intae tadekenya,
nimutietie eitu ipuopuo aairura,
ninosinoso endaa osina;
amu eisho ninye ilo onyor lenye njo.
3Ng'ura, nkera ninche enjung'ore naing'uaa Olaitoriani,
ore nkera naa nkishorot naaing'uaa ninye.
4Ore nkera naatum oltung'ani te kitisho,
netiu anaa mbaa te nkaina olmurrani
5Emayiana ilo tung'ani oiputa emootian enye mbaa!
Medol ninye enkurruna te neirorie ilmang'ati lenyena te kishomi.

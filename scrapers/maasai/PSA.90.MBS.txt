EMBUKU E ONG'UAN
(Olkerempe le Nkai 90–106)
Ene Nkai o ne tung'ani
1Olaitoriani, iyie emanyisho ang,
ti atua iishoritin pooki.
2Ore apa eton eitu eitobiruni ildoinyo,
aashu enkop pooki,
ebaiki ntarasi o ntarasi,
iyie Enkai.
3Irrinyoki iyie tung'ani meshomo enterit,
nijo, “Entorrinyoto, na kera e tung'ana!”
4 Amu ore ilarin enkalifu nabo too nkonyek inono
netiu anaa ng'ole te nelusoo,
aashu anaa erripore e nkewarie.
5Ioroo iyie iltung'ana aiwuang'ie;
netiu ninche anaa enkidetidet,
netiu anaa nkujit naaipilipil tadekenya;
6ore tadekenya neitapuku neipilipil,
ore teipa nemuguta netoyu.
7Amu eidaikie iyiook engoro ino;
ore te nkaraki engoro ino nikiruto iyiook.
8Itipika iyie nkitarruorot ang dukuya iyie,
ore nena ng'ok ang naaisudoro netii dukuya nkonyek inono.
9Amu etulusoitie nkolong'i ang pooki tiabori engoro ino,
naa keiting ilarin lang anaa enkiyang'ata.
10Ore larin te nkishui ang naa ntomoni naapishana,
naa ntomoni isiet te nikigol;
ore to lelo arin naa enkisiaayiare ake o sina;
nalusoo aasiooyo, nikipuo sii iyiook aish.
11aing'ae oiguanare engolon e ngoro ino,
o empushuna ino te risioroto e uriata ino?
12Nteng'ena naa iyiook enkikenata oo nkolong'i ang,
pee kitum aanoto oltau le ng'eno nagut.
13Torrinyunye, Olaitoriani,
O anu akenya?
Ing'urrie isinkan linono!
14Ntaraposho iyiook anaake tadekenya
te ina nyorrata ino nauno,
pee kitum aating'idata
nikishipa too nkolong'i ang pooki.
15Nting'ida iyiook too nkolong'i kumok
naaba anaa ininosie iyiook osina,
too larin ooba anaa ilikitodolie entorroni.
16Nchoo meibalunyeki esiaai ino too sinkan linono,
neibalunyeki ina golon ino kitok too nkera enye.
17Nchoo esupatisho o Laitoriani Enkai ang elotu shumata iyiook,
nintagol iyie esiaai oo nkaik ang te shumata iyiook,
ee, ntagolo iyie esiaai oo nkaik ang.

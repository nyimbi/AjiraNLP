ENAITURUK
Ore embuku eare oo LAIGUANAK nelo dukuya te wueji netung'uarie embuku e dukuya oo Laiguanak. Eidimi aatoor ena buku kat are: (1) Enkatini oo nkitoriat are eiteru te nkitoria o lari le 950 (B.C.) omeshomo embatatata e Samaria o enkiting'oto e nkitoria e moikuape olari le 721 (B.C.) (2) Enkatini enkitoria e Yuda eiteru te mbatatata e nkitoria e Israel o metabaiki enkata naibung'i neinyali Yerusalem olaiguanani oji Nebukadnesa le Babilon to lari le 586 (B.C.) Eishunye ena buku eutu imbaa e Gedalia aa olbalusui le Yuda otii abori Babilon, o enkatini e lakunoto e Yehoiakin le Yuda te korokoroni te Babilon.
Ore lelo buketa oonoto olosho naa tenkaraki nemeisiligayu ilaiguanak lenye tenebo iltung'ana loo Siraeli o le Yuda. Ore enkidaaroto e Yerusalem tenebo eararoto natarayioki iltung'ana kumok le Yuda kulie kuapi naa enkibelekenyata kitok ena te nkatini e Israel.
Ore oloiboni le Nkai olioo oleng te mbuku e are o Laiguanak naa Elisha laa ninye otusujua Eliya.

Enkomono te nkaraki eretoto
(Olke 40.13-17)
1Tonyorrai iyie Enkai, tokorduaki nanu!
Olaitoriani, tasioki taretuoki nanu!
2Nchoo metukurru nemilo lelo ooing'oru enkishui ai!
Nchoo meibelekenyi ninche nesuli,
lelo ooyieu naainyal!
3Nchoo meing'asiata ninche te nkaraki enkurruna enye,
lelo oojo, “Alda, Alda!”
4Nchoo lelo pooki liking'oru iyie
meting'idata neshipa ti atua iyie!
Nchoo lelo pooki oonyor enjeunoto ino
metejo ntarasi, “Eikitok Enkai!”
5Kake ara nanu aisinani namena;
Tasieku enatii, iyie Enkai!
Iyie eretoto ai naa iyie likikordu;
Olaitoriani, mimutie!

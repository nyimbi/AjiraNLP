Enkomono te nkaraki emirata oo lmang'ati le Israel
1Iyie Enkai, mintoki iyie agira;
migira nemintirring'a, Iyie Enkai!
2Amu ng'ura, ebuakita ilmang'ati linono;
neilepie lukuny lelo likimba iyie.
3Neitobir nkitobirat torrok too ltung'ana linono,
neiguanakino te nebo pee ear lelo litomituo iyie.
4Nejo ninche,
“Wootu, matujutu ninche aaishie anaa olosho,
nchoo enkarna e Israel eaku meigili ae kata aadamu!”
5Ee, neiguanakino ninche to ltau obo;
neitobir ninche olning'o pee kitum aataarare iyie -
6o lelo ng'oborri le Edom o le Ishmael,
o le Moab o le Hagari,
7o Gebal, o Amon o Amaleki,
o Filistia te nebo lelo oomanya Turo;
8eibung'are sii Asiria ninche;
ninche engolon oo nkera e Lutu.
9 Intaasa ninche enapa nintaasa Midian,
o Sisera o Yabin to lkeju le Kishon,
10lelo apa ooidaikieki te Endor,
lelo otaa nkik te nkop.
11 Intotiwuo lelo kituaak lenye anaa Orebu o Seebu,
nintotiu ilkituaak lenye pooki anaa Seba o Salmuna,
12lelo apa ootejo,
“Mawaita ena kop e Nkai aaitaa enaang.”
13Iyie Enkai ai, ntaa ninche olemua,
nchoo etiu anaa suut naakutaa osiwuo.
14Ore anaa enaaibung enkima entim,
o anaa enalo olng'ejep le nkima enchumata o ldoinyo,
15neijia nkuna, tusujai ninche to lkutati lino,
nturia ninche to lemua lino!
16Mputa nkomomi enye enkurruna,
pee etum ninche aaing'oru enkarna ino,
iyie Olaitoriani.
17Nchoo ninche meitukurruni, neidang ntarasi;
nchoo ninche meidaa te sulare.
18Nchoo ninche metayiolo nchere iyie ake openy eji enkarna Olaitoriani,
iyie Otii Shumata Aisul te nkop pooki.

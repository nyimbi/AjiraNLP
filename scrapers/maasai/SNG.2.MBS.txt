1Ara nanu entapukai e Sharon,
entapukai oo erat.
Olee
2Anaa entapukai natii atua ilkiku,
neija etiu enyorrata ai too ntoyie.
Enkitok
3Anaa enatiu olchani loo lng'anayio tiatua entim,
neija etiu olanyor lai tiatua iltung'ana ooti.
Atotona te nchipai sapuk tiatua oloip lenye,
nemelok olng'anayioi lenye te nkutuk ai.
4Aatorikuo ninye nanu enkaji o sirua,
naadumaki ninye nanu aitaman oltaa le nyorrata.
5Intasheyie eng'olon ai te ndaa oo lng'anayio,
nikintagol too lng'anayio;
amu aata nanu emuoyian e nyorrata.
6Oi duoo tenetii enkaina enye e kedienye abori elukunya ai,
ore enkaina enye e tatene naaibung!
7Esiligieki intae natoyie e Yerusalem,
enosa olmumai too ntarakueti naaten inchere
mipututu enyorrata ang.
Osinkolio liare
Enkitok
8Aning'ito oltoilo lo lanyor lai, ng'ura elotu,
edoku too ildoinyo, elang'u indoinyo.
9Etiu olanyor lai anaa enkolii,
arashu oloing'oni le nkolii.
Ng'ura, nelido oitashe tioriong esuntai ang,
eing'or atua enkaji te lusie,
eing'orisho too lusien.
10Airorie nanu olanyor lai naajoki ninye,
Olee
“Inyo enyorrata ai, ina nanyor aisul,
naa wou tung'uai;
11Amu ng'ura, ore ina kirobi neisha
eishunye enchan neshomo alusoo.
12Etubulutua ntapuka tioriong enkop,
netabawua enkata eranyare,
netoning'oki oltoilo loo nturkuluni te nkop ang.
13Ore olng'aboli neitayio ilng'abolo,
ore osabibu neitapuka eitayu olng'usil orropil.
Inyo, enyorrata ai,
enaikishiaan ai wou tupuku.
14Oi iyie enturkulu ai natii atua olbaata le ndapidapoi,
natii olbaata le marti,
inchooki matoduaa enkomom ino,
inchooki matoning'o oltoilo lino,
amu ore oltoilo lino naa kemelok,
naa sidai enkomom ino.
15Imbung'aki iyiook mbariak,
nena bariak kutiti,
nena naainyal inkurman ang oo sabibu,
amu ore inkurman ang oo sabibuni neitapuka.”
Enkitok
16Ore olanyor lai naa olalai nara nanu enenye,
eirrita ninye ntare enyena tiatua ntapuka.
17O metaba anaa nemanaa,
nelusoo enkewarie
imbelekenyunye olanyor lai,
totiwuo anaa enkoilii
arashu itiu anaa oloing'oni le nkolii
te nchumata oldoinyo osur.

ENAITURUK
Meyioloi oleng imbaa naaipirare oloiboni le Nkai oji YOEL; nemeibala oleng erishata apa natii. Kake ketiu oleng anaa ore ena buku netisiraki te rishata e (500-400 B.C.) te nkata e rishata e nkitoria e Parsia. Etuutua Yoel olmeitai kitok loo lmaat o lameyu kitok te nkop e Palestina. Ore te nena baa etodolua ninye enkolong nalotu o Laitoriani, enkata nalotu Olaitoriani aitame lelo ooibooyo eyieuna enye nasipa neitoriori. Eibalie oloiboni le Nkai Yoel aliki olorere aipot meirridu, o enkisiligata enye enchukunoto e mayianata too ltung'ana lenyena. Ore entoki sapuk nikidol naa enkisiligata nayau Olaitoriani airriu enkiyang'et enye shumata iltung'ana lenyena, ilewa o nkituaak, ilooti o lmoruak.

1   BWANA, moyo wangu hauna kiburi, Wala macho yangu hayainuki. Wala sijishughulishi na mambo makuu, Wala mambo yanayozidi nguvu zangu. 

2   Hakika nimeituliza nafsi yangu, Na kuinyamazisha. Kama mtoto aliyeachishwa kunyonya Kifuani mwa mama yake; Roho yangu ni kama mtoto, Aliyeachishwa kunyonya. 

3  Ee Israeli, umtarajie BWANA, Tangu leo na hata milele.

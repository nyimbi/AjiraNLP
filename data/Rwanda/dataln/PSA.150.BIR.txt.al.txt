1  Haleluya! Nimusingize Imana muri mu Ngoro yayo! Mu ijuru ryayo nimuyisingize kuko ari nyir'ubushobozi. 

2  Nimuyisingize kubera ibyo yakoze bikomeye, nimuyisingize kubera ubuhangange bwayo buhambaye. 

3  Nimuyisingize muvuza amakondera, nimuyisingize mucuranga inanga nyamuduri n'inanga y'indoha. 

4  Nimuyisingize muvuza ishakwe kandi mubyina, nimuyisingize mucuranga ibinyamirya n'imyironge. 

5  Nimuyisingize muvuza ibyuma birangīra, nimuyisingize muvuza ibyuma binihīra. 

6  Ibifite ubuzima byose nibisingize Uhoraho! Haleluya!

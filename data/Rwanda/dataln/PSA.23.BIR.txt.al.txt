1  Zaburi ya Dawidi. Uhoraho ni umushumba wanjye, ntabwo nzagira icyo nkena. 

2  Ampa kuruhukira mu rwuri rutoshye, akanyuhira amazi y'urubogobogo. 

3  Ni we ungaruramo intege, ni indahemuka anyuza mu nzira nziza. 

4  Nubwo nanyura mu gikombe gicuze umwijima, nta kintu cyantera ubwoba. Kuko wowe Uhoraho, uba uri kumwe nanjye, uranyobora ukanandengera, ibyo ni byo bimpumuriza. 

5  Untegurira ibyokurya byiza, abanzi banjye bakabura uko bangenza. Unyakira iwawe nk'umushyitsi w'imena, ukanzimanira ukandabagiza. 

6  Koko ineza yawe n'imbabazi zawe bizambaho, bizambaho igihe cyose nkiriho, nanjye nzajya ngusengera mu Ngoro yawe, nzahagusengera igihe cyose nzaba nkiriho.

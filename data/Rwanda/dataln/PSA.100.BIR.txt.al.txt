1  Zaburi yo gushimira Uhoraho. Mwa batuye ku isi yose mwe, nimuvugirize Uhoraho impundu! 

2  Nimuramye Uhoraho munezerewe, nimuze imbere ye muririmba. 

3  Nimumenye ko Uhoraho ari we Mana, ni we watwiremeye natwe turi abe. Turi ubwoko bwe turi n'umukumbi yiragirira. 

4  Nimwinjire mu marembo y'Ingoro ye mumushimira, nimwinjire muri urwo rugo mumusingiza. Nimumushimire mumutake ibisingizo. 

5  Koko Uhoraho agira neza, imbabazi ze zihoraho iteka ryose, umurava we uhoraho uko ibihe bihaye ibindi.

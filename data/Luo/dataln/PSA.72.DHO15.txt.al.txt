1  A Nyasaye, puonj ruoth mondo ong’ad bura mana gi adierani, kendo ipuonje timni makare. 

2  Kamano enorit jogi gi adiera, kendo notel ni jochan e yo makare. 

3  Ji modak e gode madongo gi matindo onego odag maber gi kue. 

4  Ruoth onego ong’ad bura kare ni joma chandore, kendo okony jochan, mi olo joma thirogi. 

5  Onego jogi sik kalami ndalo duto, mana kaka chieng’ gi due osiko ka rieny. 

6  Ruoth onego ochal gi koth machue kendo miyo piny bedo mang’ich. 

7  Tim makare onego olandre e ndalone, kendo ji onego odhi maber nyaka chieng’. 

8   Pinye nochakre e bath nam man yo podho chieng’, kendo nochakre aora Yufrate nyaka tung’ piny. 

9  Ogendini modak e piny motwo nokulre e nyime, kendo wasike noriere piny tir kobolore. 

10  Ruodhi mag Spania kod mag pinje manie dier nam nogolne osuru, kendo ruodhi mag Arabu gi mag Ethiopia bende nokelne mich. 

11  Kamano ruodhi duto nokulrene, kendo ogendini duto notine. 

12  Ka joma odhier oywakne, to oreso, kaachiel gi jochan kod joma onge gi ng’ama dikonygi. 

13  Okecho joma ithiro kendo modhier, bende oreso ngima jochan. 

14  Ogologi e thagruok, kendo e timbe mahundu, nimar okwano ngimagi ka gima nengogi tek. 

15  Ruoth mondo odag aming’a. Onego oyud chiwo mar dhahabu moa Arabu. Bende onego lemne kinde duto, kendo hap Nyasaye mondo obed kode pile. 

16  Onego chiemo obed mang’eny e piny, kendo cham machiek e gode mondo obed mogundho ka chamb Lebanon. Ji onego opong’ mier mana ka lum manie pap. 

17  Wich kik wil gi nyinge, kendo humbe mondo osiki nyaka chieng’. Ogendini duto mondo opake, kendo onego ji duto kwa Nyasaye mondo oguedhgi kaka oseguedho ruoth. 

18  Pakuru Ruoth Nyasaye ma Nyasach Israel! En kende ema otimo gik malichgi. 

19  Pakuru nyinge maduong’ nyaka chieng’ kendo duong’ne maler mondo opong’ piny! Amin! 

20  Mano e giko lemo mag Daudi wuod Jesse.

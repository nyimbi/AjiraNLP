1  Un jo-Jerusalem, ng’oluru dendu manyiso ni un gi kibaji, nimar wasikwa olworowa, kendo gigoyo lemb jatend Israel gi luth. Loch jawar moa Bethlehem 

2   Bethlehem Efratha, in e dala matinie moloyo miech Juda duto. To kuomi ema jatend Israel biro wuokie, ma anywolane nitie nyaka nene. 

3  Kuom mano, Ruoth Nyasaye biro weyo joge e lwet wasikgi nyaka dhako ma muoch kayo nywol wuode. Bang’e jowete wuowino modong’ mak otho e tuech, noduogi mi chak riwre gi jowetegi. 

4  To wuowino nobi, mi nobed jatend joge, koritogi gi teko kod duong’ moa kuom Ruoth Nyasaye. Joge nodag maber, nikech ji duto manie piny noyie lochne, 

5  mi enobed ng’at makelo kue. Ka jo-Asuria omonjo pinywa, mi omwomo miechwa mochiel motegno, to wanaor jotendwa marateke moloyo, mondo oked kodgi, 

6   mi giniketh piny Asuria gi lweny. Ginireswa ka jo-Asuria monjo pinywa. 

7  Jo-Israel modong’ mak otho nochal ni ogendini mang’eny ka koth ma Ruoth Nyasaye okelo ni cham, nikech dhano ok nyal kelo koth, to Nyasaye ema kele. 

8  Ee, jo-Israel motony nobed e kind ogendini mana ka sibuor ma dwaro le e bungu, kata madwaro rombe e lek. Ka odonjo kuom rombe to opodho kuomgi kendo okidhogi matindo tindo ma onge ng’ama resogi. 

9  Jo-Israel biro loyo wasikgi mi tiek pep. 

10  Ruoth Nyasaye wachonu un jo-Israel niya, “E kindeno ananeg fareseu kendo naketh gecheu mag lweny. 

11  Anaketh miechu, mi amuk kuondeu duto mochiel motegno mag siro lweny. 

12  Abiro ketho timbe ajuoke duto, kendo ok nuchak ubed gi jokor wach kata achiel. 

13  Abiro ketho nyiseche manono gi sirni milamo, kendo ok unuchak ulam gik ma uloso uwegi. 

14  Abiro muko sirni milamo e pinyu, mi aketh miechu, 

15  to bende abiro chulo kuor gi mirimba mager ni ogendini ma ok oseluora.”

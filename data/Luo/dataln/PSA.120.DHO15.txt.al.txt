1  Kane an e chandruok, to ne aywak ni Ruoth Nyasaye, mi odwoka. 

2  A Ruoth Nyasaye, konya kuom jomriambo gi jomganga. 

3  Un jomriambo, Nyasaye notimnu ang’o? Nokumu machal nadi? 

4  Nokumu mana kichielou gi aserni mabitho mag jolweny, kata kiwang’ou gi mach moowore haa! 

5  Dak e kindu rach mana ka dak e piny Meshek, kata ka dak e kind jo-Kedar! 

6  Asedak aming’a mokadho gi joma ok dwar kue. 

7  An adwaro kue, to gin gidwaro mana lweny.

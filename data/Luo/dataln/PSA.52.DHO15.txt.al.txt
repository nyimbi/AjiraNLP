1  In ng’ama ratego, ang’o momiyo isungori nikech richoni? Hera mar Nyasaye osiko pile nyaka chieng’. 

2  Ichano timbe ma idwaro hinyogo ji, kendo lewi mihangogo ji bith ka wembe. 

3  Ihero timbe maricho moloyo mabeyo kendo miriambo berni moloyo adiera. 

4  Ihero chwanyo chuny ji gi weche mag miriambo. 

5  Kuom mano, Nyasaye notieki chuth. Obiro maki, mi oyudhi oko e piny joma ngima! 

6  Joma oluoro Nyasaye none gima otimoreni, mi ginibed maluor. Gininyieri, ka giwacho niya, 

7  “Neyeuru ng’at mane ok ogeno kuom Nyasaye, to ogeno mana kuom mwandune mathoth, kendo pakore gi richone!” 

8  To an, achalo gi yadh zeituni, ma dongo maber e bath od Nyasaye, nimar ageno kuom hera mosiko mar Nyasaye nyaka chieng’. 

9  A Nyasaye, abiro goyoni erokamano pile kuom gik ma isetimo, kendo ka an e kanyakla mar joma oheri, to anahul ni iber.

1  Kitabu mar Chik Musa mag jonabi 

2  kod mag jondiko moko mane oluwo bang’ jonabi osekelonwa mich mang’eny miwuoro. 

3  Kuom mano, owinjore ji opak rieko mipuonjo e Israel. 

4  To tich ma joma somo kitepe maler nigo, ok en mana ng’eyo weche mondikie kende, 

5  to en konyo jomoko bende, 

6  gi wach kendo gi ndiko. 

7  Mano emomiyo Joshua kwara nochiwore ni somo Chik 

8  gi kitepe Jonabi 

9  kod kitepe moko mag kwerewa machon. 

10  kane osetiegore kuom wechego, 

11  nochako ndiko weche moko manyalo puonjo ji rieko, 

12  mondo joma ohero puonjore okonyrego, 

13  mondo gimed bedo 

14  ka gidak e ngima ma Chik dwaro. 

15  Omiyo, in jasomo, akwayi 

16  mondo isom kitabuni 

17  gi chuny maber, kendo kitimo kinda. 

18  ,

19  To kiyudo kuonde moko ma ok walokoe wach moro malong’o, to yie ing’wonnwa, 

20  nikech wasetemo matek mondo waler tiend weche duto maber. 

21  Wahero paronu ni, kiloko kitabu e dhok moro, 

22  to kamoro ok inyal yudo wach ma tiende chal chutho gi mano mane okwong ndik gi dho Hibrania. 

23  Ok kamano kende, 

24  to kinono Kitap Chik, gi kitepe Jonabi 

25  kod kitepe mamoko, 

26  to ibiro yudo ni koselokgi e dhok moro, to tiend weche mantie opogore gi mago mane ondik gi dhok mokwongo. 

27  ,

28  E higa mar piero adek gaboro mar loch ruoth Yuergete, ma koro osetho, kane oyudo asedak Misri kuom kinde moko, 

29  ne ayudo kitabu moro moting’o puonj mabeyo ahinya. 

30  Mano nomiyo achiwora mondo alok kitabuno, 

31  ,

32  kendo nakawo ndalo mang’eny kaketo riekona duto kuom tijni 

33  ,

34  mondo atiek kitabuno mondo okony joma odak oko madwaro puonjore kaka digilok timbegi mondo gibed ka gidak e ngima ma Chik dwaro. NGECHE MOKO

Kum ma Nyasaye nokumgo dhano
1
Kuom mano, ka ing’ado ni wadu bura, to ng’e ni bura oseloyi, bed ni in ng’at manadi, nikech ka ing’ado ni jomoko bura, to ing’ado bura mana ni in iwuon, nimar in bende itimo mana gik ma jogo timo. 2In iwacho ni, “Wang’eyo ni Nyasaye ni kare kokumo joma timo gik makamago.” 3To in ing’ado bura ni joma timo gigo, to in iwuon bende itimogi, koro ere kaka iparo ni initony e buch Nyasaye? 4Koso itimo kamano nikech ichayo Nyasaye nimar ong’won ahinya kendo ohore mos, mak okumi piyo? Donge ing’eyo ni Nyasaye ng’won kamano mana nikech odwaro ni mondo ilokri? 5To in wiyi tek, kendo itamori yie, omiyo ikano kum ni in iwuon ndalo mar mirima, tiende ni chieng’ ma Nyasaye noelie bura mare makare. 6
Chieng’no nomi ng’ato ka ng’ato pok moromo gi timne. 7Joma osiko katimo maber, kendo dwaro pak gi duong’ moa kuom Nyasaye, kod ngima ma ok rum, nomi ngima ma nyaka chieng’. 8To joma dwaro mana gigi giwegi, kendo ok dwar gima kare, to dwaro mana luwo gima ok kare noyud kum gi mirima mager. 9Masira kod chandruok noyud ng’ato ka ng’ato matimo richo. Jo-Yahudi ema nokwong yudo masichego, eka joma ok jo-Yahudi bende noyudgi. 10To ng’ato ka ng’ato matimo maber noyud duong’ gi pak kod kue. Jo-Yahudi ema nokwong yudogi, eka joma ok jo-Yahudi bende. 11
Nyasaye ok dew wang’ ng’ato!
12Joma timo richo, to ok ong’eyo Chik Musa, nolal nono, kata obedo ni gikia Chik kamano. Joma timo richo, to ong’eyo Chik, nong’adnigi bura kaluwore gi Chik. 13Ng’ato nyalo winjo Chik, to ka ok omako, to ok onyal bet e winjruok gi Nyasaye. 14Ka joma ok jo-Yahudi, ma gin joma onge Chik, timo mana gima Chik dwaro, to mano nyiso ni Chik tiyo e chunygi, kata obedo ni gikia Chik kamano. 15Mano nyiso ni gima Chik dwaro ondiki e chunygi. Parogi bende nyiso ni mano en adier, nikech seche moko parogi nyisogi ni giseketho, to seche moko parogi onyisogi ni gionge bura. 16Kamano e kaka notimre chieng’ ma Nysaye nomi Yesu Kristo yalie weche mopondo duto manie chuny dhano. Mano e kaka Wach Maber ma alando nyiso.
Jo-Yahudi kod Chik Musa
17In to ipakori ni in ja-Yahudi, kendo imor gi bedo kamano. Igeno kuom Chik Musa, kendo isungori ni in achiel kuom jo-Nyasaye. 18Iwacho ni osepuonji Chik, omiyo ing’eyo gima Nyasaye dwaro mondo itim, kendo inyalo pogo gik makare gi gik maricho. 19Bende iwacho ni ing’eyo chutho ni in jatend muofni, kendo ni in e ler ni joma ni e mudho, 20bende ni itayo joma ofuwo, kendo in japuonj nyithindo. Mago duto iwacho nikech Chik oting’oni rieko gi adiera duto. 21To ka ipuonjo jomoko kamano, to in dak ipuonjri iwuon? Ipuonjo ni, “Kik ikwal,” to in donge ikuelo? 22Iwacho ni kik ng’ato terre, to in donge iterori? In isin gi nyiseche manono, to donge ikwuelo e hekalu mag nyiseche manono? 23In ipakori ni in gi Chik Nyasaye, to donge ikelo achaye ni Nyasaye kiketho Chik, 24
mana kaka Muma wacho niya, “Un jo-Yahudi ema umiyo joma ok jo-Yahudi tugo gi nying Nyasaye”?
25Ka imako Chik, to ter moterigo nyangu nigi tiende, to ka ok imako Chik, to ichal gi ng’at ma ok oter nyangu, kata bed ni oteri nyangu kamano. 26Kamano bende, ka ng’at ma ok ja-Yahudi, kendo ma ok oter nyangu, omako Chik, to Nyasaye nokwane ka ka ng’at moter nyangu. 27To in ja-Yahudi moter nyangu kendo man gi Chik mondiki kamano, to pod ikethe aketha, to gin, kata obedo ni ok otergi nyangu, to girito Chik? 28Gik ma oko ok ema mi ng’ato dok ja-Yahudi maradier, bende tero nyangu mar ringruok ok e tero nyangu maradier. 29
Ooyo, ja-Yahudi maradier en ng’at ma ja-Yahudi gie chunye, kendo tero nyangu maradier bende en mar chuny. En tich Roho, to ok en tich rito Chik. Kata obedo ni ng’at ma kamano ok yud pak kuom dhano, to oyudo kuom Nyasaye.

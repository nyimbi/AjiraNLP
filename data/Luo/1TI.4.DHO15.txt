Jopuonj mag miriambo
1Roho wacho malong’o ni e ndalo giko, ji moko nowe yie marwa, ka gichiko itgi ni puonj mag jochiende ma miyo ji lal. 2Jopuonj mag miriambo ma parogi odinore ka gima owang’ gi nyinyo maliet nowuond ji, 3ka gipuonjogi ni kik gikendi, bende ginikwergi mondo kik cham chiemo moko, ma Nyasaye ema ochweyo mondo joma oyie, kendo ong’eyo adiera, ocham gi erokamano. 4To gik moko duto mane Nyasaye ochueyo beyo, kendo onge monego ji kuer, to onego chamgi kigoyo ni Nyasaye erokamano, 5nikech Wach Nyasaye gi lemo miyo gibedo maler.
Jatich maber mar Kristo Yesu
6Ka ipuonjo owete wechego, to inibed jatich maber mar Kristo Yesu, kendo weche mag yie kod puonj maber miseluwo nomi idongi. 7To bed mabor gi sigendini manono mag pinyni maonge tiendgi. Bed kipuonjo timo timbe mowinjore gi luoro Nyasaye. 8Ing’eyo ni ka ng’ato oketo chunye kuom tuke mag rieyo del, to mano konye mana e yore moko kende, to ka ng’ato oketo chunye kuom luoro Nyasaye, to mano konye e yore duto nikech okelone gueth e ngima ma sani, kendo e ngima mabiro bende. 9Wachno en adier, kendo owinjore ji oyiego chuth. 10Mano ema omiyo watiyo matek kendo wakedo, nikech wan gi geno kuom Nyasaye mangima, ma Jawar mar ji duto, to moloyo Jawar mar joma oyie.
11Puonj ji wechego, kendo chikgi, 12ma ok iyie mondo ng’ato ochayi nikech itin. To bed ranyisi ni jo-Kristo, kuom weche miwacho, gi gik mitimo, kod hera miherogi ji, kendo kuom bedo jaadiera, bende kuom bedo ng’ama ler. 13Ket chunyi kuom somo ni ji Wach Nyasaye, kendo kuom lando wach, kendo kuom puonjo ji, nyaka chieng’ moro abi. 14Kik ijwang’ mich mar Roho mane iyudo kane okor wach kuomi e kinde mane jodongo oyieyoe luetgi kuomi. 15Tim gigo gi chunyi duto mondo ji one kaka idongo e yore duto. 16Tim kinda e wechego, kiritori iwuon, kendo kitimo gik mipuonjo. Kitimo kamano to iniresri iwuon, kendo nires joma winjo weche mipuonjo bende.

1  À Dagwi, hwo man nu, hwo wey ɛ yuu wɛt! Hwo ye jaram wɛt! 

2  Di, bemât repugu mō ba ga doyo e ra hwo, vemo de be kyɛ́ ɛn hwo ba ga sogo a hwo nehywɛ. 

3  Bé yong begwông repugu wɔ̀ sɛ̀ mɛ bemat mō, be wuna nu mɛn, na be yong begwong beves e ra vemo de ba sé pyɛ̂ yey mō. 

4  Yɛn e tē, “Tik wot a wey na BeIsrayɛl hywiri, ko bê to wok reza mɛn wɛt!” 

5  Yɛn e ra badu, ko myak a hɛn ba simo be wuna to e ra hwo. 

6  Yɛn vɛ a BeƐdɔm na BeIshmayɛl, BeMowap na BeHagar, 

7  BeGebal, BeAmɔn, BeAmalɛk, BeFilisti, tanga bemât repomô Tur. 

8  BeSirya ba wuna hɛn tanga yɛn, na be gwɛ̌ a BeAmɔn na BeMowap, vɛ a yerê Lɔt, gap. 

9  À Dagwi, rá a yɛn sede hwà ra na BeMidyan, sede hwà ra na Sisera tanga Yabin e Rɔt Kishɔn. 

10  Ba wey na yɛn e Ɛn-Dɔr, ba tik bavin mɛn be bɔsɔ e hey. 

11  Rá a begbɔng bemat mɛn sede bá ra a Ɔrɛp tanga Zeep, sede bá ra a Zeba na Zalmuna. 

12  Yɛn mwa ba ye, “Tik na wot a sey ɛ vwel de ha sé hê Dagwi, na he tɛ́ ɛ mot.” 

13  À Dagwi hom, tɛ̀ ɛn be tɛ́ ɛn sede berang de wula wa rung, sede byɛmɛ de ye raa gul. 

14  Sè a yɛn sede kya de he re rehwot, sede nebelem kya de ne lolo e beraku. 

15  Ró ɛn na gwogul rwak mō, tɛ̀ ɛn, be ye lot na sombabos mō! 

16  À Dagwi Darwey, tik be tē det na rekyen, na be hwo jāma hwo. 

17  Tik ɛn be sɔ rekyen, be sangal e de tē lot neshey gbak-gbak, tik be wey e ji rekyen. 

18  Tik yɛn a tɔk, hwo nju mo zɔng de reza mō à Dagwi Darwey, hwo nju mo à Dagwi De A Dal Na Reto Mwa e jey.

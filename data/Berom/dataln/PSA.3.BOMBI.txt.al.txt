1  À Dagwi Darwey, ma vɔk bemât repugu pyak, gwogyɛ̀ng bemat gwa ga doyo e ra me! 

2  Bé ha e ra me, be tē, “Dagwi ê sɛlɛ hɛ wɛt!” 

3  Ko hwo, à Dagwi Darwey, hwó dul me sede ruga. Hwó nɔngɔs me wɛng, hwó tɛ̀, me kyè na to e gwi. 

4  Kayɛ jeng de me yila hwo, na hwo sɛlɛ me, hwo hanat a me yi e raku de hwà tik jaram yaga hwo. 

5  Me wɔ̂ nevwa, me sene wɛng-wɛng, yaga hwó gyenges me. 

6  Gwogyɛ̀ng bemât repugu hom gwo rɔnɔ me, ko me wok bengyip wɛt. 

7  À Dagwi Darwey, Dagwi hom, vey sey me! Fala a bemât repugu hom nenu, gangas a yɛn yìn! 

8  Hwo wɛ de hwó sey bemat. Tik nerat mō ne se e ra bemat mō!

1  -

2  E jeng de Dagwi a yong mwasi a yong ɛ sede yɛ. A yong ɛn rwas na hwā. A tɛ̀ a yɛn nerat, ko a vo a yɛn rezâ Mwasi. Ngyɛrɛ̂ bazâ yerê mwasi wo mɛsɛng, yɛ̀ Adam, a het ngan: 

3  Jeng de Adam a wul neshey naga na kuru batat (

130  ) a vɔk hwey sede yɛ, a vo a hɛ rezâ Shɛt. 

4  E bama mo, Adam a ros neshey naga rwit (

800  ); a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

5  Mwa neshey Adam na sé neshey naga shabetat na kuru batat (

930  ), a ku. 

6  Jeng de Shɛt a wul neshey naga na nɛ netungun (

105  ), a tɔ Ɛnɔsh. 

7  E bama mo, Shɛt a ros neshey naga rwit na kuru shabatat na nɛ netaama (

807  ), a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

8  Mwa neshey Shɛt na sé naga shabetat na kuru na nɛ neba (

912  ), a ku. 

9  Jeng de Ɛnɔsh a wul neshey kuru shabatat (

90  ), a tɔ Kɛnan. 

10  E bama mo, Ɛnɔsh a ros neshey naga rwit na kuru na nɛ netungun (

815  ), a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

11  Mwâ neshey Ɛnɔsh na sé naga shabetat na nɛ netungun (

905  ), a ku. 

12  Jeng de Kɛnan a wul neshey kuru bataama (

70  ), a tɔ Mahalalɛl. 

13  E bama mo, Kɛnan a ros neshey naga rwit na kuru banaas (

840  ), a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

14  Mwa neshey Kɛnan na sé naga shabetat na kuru (

910  ), a ku. 

15  Jeng de Mahalalɛl a wul neshey kuru batyiimin na nɛ netungun (

65  ), a tɔ Yarɛt. 

16  E bama mo, Mahalalɛl a ros neshey naga rwit na kuru batat (

830  ), a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

17  Mwa neshey Mahalalɛl na sé naga rwit na kuru shabatat na nɛ netungun (

895  ), a ku. 

18  Jeng de Yarɛt a wul neshey naga na kuru batyiimin na nɛ neba (

162  ), a tɔ Ɛnɔk. 

19  E bama mo, Yarɛt a ros neshey naga rwit (

800  ), a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

20  Mwa neshey Yarɛt na sé naga shabetat na kuru batyiimin na nɛ neba (

962  ), a ku. 

21  Jeng de Ɛnɔk a wul neshey kuru batyiimin na nɛ netungun (

65  ), a tɔ Mɛtusɛla. 

22  E bama mo, a sè sangal e ji nesa na Dagwi neshey naga betat (

300  ); a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

23  Mwa neshey Ɛnɔk na sé naga betat na kuru batyiimin na nɛ netungun (

365  ). 

24  Ɛnɔk a sè sangal e ji nesa na Dagwi, ko ba to di ɛ wɛt, yaga Dagwi a ga jut ɛ. 

25  Jeng de Mɛtusɛla a wul neshey naga na kuru rwit na nɛ netaama (

187  ), a tɔ Lamɛk. 

26  E bama mo, Mɛtusɛla a ros neshey naga betaama na kuru rwit na nɛ neba (

782  ), a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

27  Mwa neshey Mɛtusɛla na sé naga shabetat na kuru batyiimin na nɛ shanetat (

969  ), a ku. 

28  Jeng de Lamɛk a wul neshey naga na kuru rwit na nɛ neba (

182  ), a tɔ hwey. 

29  Ko a ye, “Yi e vwel de Dagwi Darwey a kana kyini, hwey hemo hê vey a wot na pɔngɔ yi e reyɔ̂m fwom mot mwa.” Yaga ano, a vo a hɛ rezâ Nowa. 

30  E bama mo, Lamɛk a ros neshey naga betungun na kuru shabatat na nɛ netungun (

595  ), a vɔk nèy nehak bewen na behwong. 

31  Mwa neshey Lamɛk na sé naga betaama na kuru bataama na nɛ netaama (

777  ), a ku. 

32  Jeng de Nowa a wul neshey naga betungun (

500  ), a vɔk nèy netat Shɛm, na Ham tanga Yafɛt.

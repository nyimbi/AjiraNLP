1  À Dagwi Darwey, hwà sé ra shom, hwà sé tabak me. A ga vey ano! Tik me! 

2  Hwà ga mǎ me na ket mō; hwà ga nɔrɔs me vwɔ. 

3  Yaga shom mō, dem hak ha to sé nang wɛt, mwa nedem hom na ga tɛ́ ɛn jwɛɛnɛn yaga banɛn hom. 

4  Neburu hom na ga sɔ a me to, na sé a me pyɛ nedirik de mê kyɛm me jut wɛt. 

5  Má ra netɔ̌n, ma jut o na jɔgɔ, yaga ano, baram hom ba ga bɔsɔ bé ro nesang. 

6  Ma ga ye nguk, ma ga tɛ́ me gbɛrɛk, ko me wosho e de tosho. 

7  Rebek hom ra fes sede kya, dem hak ha to sé nang wɛt. 

8  Ma ga sɔ me, vit a bwâng gap, ko ma ga tɛ́ me buruk, yey hom ye bɔgɔs, me rus yaga nepep. 

9  À Darwey, hwà tɔk pyɛ de ma vɔk cwey yɛ, hwà ga wok rus hom. 

10  Nesong né rasa a me, gap hom ha ga tala hɛ, bayis hom ba ga tɛ́ ɛn hywiinin. 

11  Besa hom tanga bemât vwara hom be vey bagat na me wɛt yaga baram hom, belɔ hom mwa ba ye jaram à me. 

12  Vemo de be simo wey na me be rene bagun, ko vemo de be simo be mǎ me be tɛsɛ a me bengyip wey na me. Mwa wosho be yong pyɛ́ vyes vey sɛ̀ me. 

13  Ko ma sé sede kpey, vit à wok ha, ma sé sede tɔn, vit a tɔk ha. 

14  Ey, ma sé sede mwat de e wok ha wɛt, ko e tɔk toyo na hɛ wɛt. 

15  Me sɛt bayis e ra hwo, à Dagwi Darwey, Dagwi hom, hwo wɛ de hwô hanat e to hom. 

16  Ma ye, “Hwo tik vê repugu hom be feng yaga reyɔm de me sɔ kɛ be dak kaa e ra gaba hom wɛt!” 

17  Ma sé e ji nepep kayɛ jeng, ma sé lɛs e ra gaba. 

18  Ma sé kulu banɛn hom, me lashe yey yaga pyɛ́ de ma ra. 

19  Vê repugu hom de be simo mɔrɔ me bé wok na gap, pyak ba sé de be bɛɛ me ves, vit à tin. 

20  Be tala me na pyɛ́ vyes e tô nerat, be kyɛ́ ɛn me, yaga me tē ngyɛ́k na me ra pyɛ́ ryat. 

21  À Dagwi Darwey, Dagwi hom, hwo ye waa a me kɛ hwo sè yikyik na me wɛt! 

22  À Darwey, mwât sey me, vey sɛlɛ me dey-dey!

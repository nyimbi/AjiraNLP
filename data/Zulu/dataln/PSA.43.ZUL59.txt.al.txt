1   Ngahlulele, Nkulunkulu ungimele endabeni yami esizweni esingenamusa, ngikhulule kumuntu wenkohliso nongenakulunga. 

2   Ngokuba wena Nkulunkulu uyisiphephelo sami; ungilahleleni na? Ngihambelani ngilila, ngicindezelwe yisitha, na? 

3   Thuma ukukhanya kwakho neqiniso lakho, kungihole, kungiyise entabeni yakho engcwele nasezindlini zakho, 

4   ngize ngifike kulo i-altare likaNkulunkulu, kuNkulunkulu wenjabulo yokwethaba kwami, ngikudumise ngehabhu,* Nkulunkulu, Nkulunkulu wami. 

5   Yini ukuba udangale, mphefumulo wami, uyaluze phakathi kwami, na? ngisezakumdumisa yena oyinsindiso yobuso bami noNkulunkulu wami.

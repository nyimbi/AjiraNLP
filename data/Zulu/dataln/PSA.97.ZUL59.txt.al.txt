1   UJehova uyinkosi; umhlaba mawethabe, zijabule iziqhingi eziningi. 

2   Amafu nobumnyama kuyamphahla; ukulunga nokwahlulela kuyisisekelo sesihlalo sakhe sobukhosi. 

3   Umlilo uyahamba phambi kwakhe, udla izitha zakhe nxazonke. 

4  Imibani yakhe ikhanyisa izwe; umhlaba uyakubona, uthuthumele. 

5   Izintaba zincibilika njengengcina phambi kukaJehova, phambi kweNkosi yomhlaba wonke. 

6   Amazulu ashumayela ukulunga kwakhe; izizwe zonke zibona inkazimulo yakhe. 

7   Mabajabhe bonke abakhonza izithombe ezibaziweyo, abazigabisa ngezithombe. Mkhonzeni nina nonke bonkulunkulu. 

8   ISiyoni liyezwa, liyajabula; ngenxa yezahlulelo zakho, Jehova. 

9   Ngokuba wena Jehova uphezu komhlaba wonke, uphakeme kakhulu phezu kwawonkulunkulu bonke. 

10   Nina enithanda uJehova zondani okubi; uyagcina imiphefumulo yabamkhonzayo; uyabasindisa ezandleni zababi. 

11   Ukukhanya kuhlwanyelelwe olungileyo, ukujabula kuhlwanyelelwe abanhliziyo ziqotho. 

12   Jabulani kuJehova nina balungileyo, nibonge igama lakhe elingcwele.

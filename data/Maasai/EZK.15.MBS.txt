Enkitanyaanyukoto naipirta olchani loo sabibu
1Nelotu ororei lo Laitoriani enatii ejo: 2“Enkerai e tung'ani, kaji eiko enchata olchani loo sabibu tenegiroo te nkadori likae shani ake pooki, olng'osila lo sabibu otii atua ilkeek le ntim? 3Keyae enchata te ninye aaitobirie ae toki? Keya iltung'ana enchata te ninye aililiyie ake pooki girra? 4Ng'ura, epiki ninye enkima metaa eilata enye; ore peyie emut enkima ineeiting'ie enyena pokira, ore empolos enye netumute, keitoki aata tipat tiae toki? 5Ing'ura, ore apa eton elulung'a ninye, nemeeta ae naitaasi; kebaa meeta tipat etumuta enkima ninye, keitoki aata tipat tiae toki. 6Neaku inji ejo Olaitoriani Enkai: anaa enatiu ilkeek to olchani loo sabibu tiatua entim, ilo latipika enkima meitaa eilata, neaku neijia alo ake lelo oomanya Yerusalem. 7Naa kaitorioki ninye enkomom ai agilunore ninche; hoo neilanyisho ninche aing'uaa enkima, kenya ina kima ninche amut; niyiolouu aajo ara nanu Olaitoriani tenaitorioki ninche enkomom ai agilunore. 8Naa kaitaa enkop mitil, amu etaa ninche ilemeeta enkirukoto, ejo Olaitoriani Enkai.”

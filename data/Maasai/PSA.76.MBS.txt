Enkai naisulie
1Keyioloi Enkai ti atua Yuda,
eikitok enkarna enye ti atua Israel.
2Neteshetaki emanyisho enye te Salem,
ewueji enye nebikie te Sion.
3Te ine wueji etigila ninye mbaa,
o elong'o, o lalem, o masaa o larrabal.
4Ira iyie kitok, niata enkishiaa,
alang lelo doinyo loo ntarasi.
5Ore lelo lenye oogol tauja,
netooruoki pooki naata,
neirragaitie ninche aaye;
ore lelo tung'ana pooki lo larrabal,
meidim aataasishore nkaik enye.
6Ore te nkisikong'ata ino, Na Ai e Yakobo,
neirrag aaye pokira, olakedoni o embarta.
7Kake, ore iyie, naa intoreisho,
kaing'ae oidim aitasho te dukuya iyie
te nedumunye ngoro ino nabo kata.
8Ore itii keper nidung enkiguana ino,
neureishoyu enkop neitirring'a,
9ena kata edumunye Enkai aitobir enkiguana,
pee eitajeu lelo pooki ooitorogonyaki te nkop.
10Esipa eikisis iyie engoro e tung'ani;
ore ilo pisiai le ngoro nien anaa enkimeita.
11Entobira ilmuma linyi to Laitoriani Enkai inyi,
nintabayaya ninche;
nchoo lelo pooki oomanita ninche
meyau nkishorot aayaki ilo ourei,
12naa kedung ninye iltauja le lelo kituaak,
ilo laa ninye eure ilaiguanak pooki le nkop.

Enkomono te nkaraki eretoto
1Ke nkata nabaa iyie Olaitoriani?
mikidamu iyie nanu?
Kenkata nabaa isudoo iyie enkomom ino te nanu.
2Kanap nanu emion to ltau lai o anu,
naisinanuo to ltau lai endama pooki?
Keitokoos akenya olmang'atinta lai ti alo nanu o anu?
3Ing'uraki nanu, nikiwaliki,
iyie Olaitoriani,
Enkai ai;
ntawang'a nkonyek aainei,
pee mairura ake too njo e keeya;
4pee mejo ake olmang'atinta lai,
“Aisula nanu te ninye”;
pee meng'ida ake ilo mang'atinta lai
tenkaraki embatata ai.
5Kake aisiliga nanu ina nyorrata ino nauno;
naa keng'ida oltau lai te nkaraki enjeunoto ino.
6Karanyaki nanu Olaitoriani,
amu aaitaasa ninye nanu ine supatisho te nkumoi.

Enkisisa o enkishorunoto e nashe
1Kinchiaakino enkisisa te Sion, iyie Enkai;
naa iyie einosieki ilmuma,
2iyie ele oning enkomono!
Enitii elotu pooki toki naishu,
3te nkaraki ing'ok enye.
Te neidimu iyiook nkitarruorot ang,
nipalaki iyie iyiook.
4Emayiana ilo litegelua iyie niyau aitinyiku,
pee eton ti atua indung'orot inono!
Eikindipakino iyiook te supatisho e nkaji ino,
ina aji ino sinyati!
5Ore too nkiaasin naaitureisho
niwaliki iyie iyiook te nkordunoto,
iyie, iyie Enkai Ai e njeunoto ang,
iyie osiligi loo wuejitin pooki neeiting'ie enkop,
o inaaiposha naalakua;
6laa te ngolon ino intasheyie ildoinyo,
irretenita enkidimata ino;
7iyie oitirring'ie enkikurrukur oo naaiposha,
ina kikurrukur oo lopir lenye,
ilo oilepilepi loo ltung'ana;
8neaku etureishote lelo oomanya ineelakua te nkop,
te nkaraki nkitoduaat inono;
nchoo iyie ineelusorie enkakenya o enteipa metabuaku te ng'ida.
9Iyie omutan enkop nibukoki enkare,
iyie oitubulu oleng;
ebore ilo keju le Nkai enkare;
iyie oisho ninche enkurma enye,
amu neijia iyie ntobira aiko.
10Iyie opik ierat lenyena enkare,
nintobir anuk ilbaat pooki,
nintanana enterit te nkirujuruj,
nebulu emayianata.
11Nchopoko iyie olari esupatisho ino,
ore rrekiei le ngarri ino neitong eilata.
12Ore ndaat oo nchoo ti ong'ata neitong,
ore ildoinyo nemanita eng'ida,
13ore nkong'at naaisiaja neiput nkerrai,
neiput enkurma ierat,
nebuaku ninche te nebo aarany te ng'ida.

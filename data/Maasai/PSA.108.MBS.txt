Enkomono te nkaraki ilmang'ati
(Olke 57.7-11, 60.5-12)
1Etuune oltau lai, Iyie Enkai,
etuune oltau lai!
Karanyisho nanu, naosh olkerempe!
Tuputunye lo tau lai!
2Tuputunye na auo oo sinkolioitin o endule!
Kainyototo nanu enasirie!
3Kaaisho nanu iyie enashe Olaitoriani,
ti atua olorere,
kaaranyaki nanu iyie enkisisa ti atua nkuapi.
4Amu ore enyorrata ino nauno naa eikitok te keper,
nebaiki isipat inono keper.
5Nchoo kirrepi iyie, Iyie Enkai, te keper!
Nchoo enkitoo ino meeu shumata enkop pooki!
6Pee etumi aatokordu ilo kirotet lino,
nchoru eretoto te nkaina ino e tatene,
tawalikiaki nanu!
7Eisiligishore Enkai te wueji enye sinyati:
ejo, “Ore te nkisisa kaor nanu Shekem,
naorrior o yerata le Sukot.
8Enaai Gilead; enaai Manase;
enkopiyia ai nagol e lukunya Efraim;
eng'udi ai e nkitoria Yuda.
9Embesen ai naitukuoreki Moab;
shumata Edom nanu aiteleiki nkejek aainei;
te Filistia nanu abuaku te nkisulata.”
10Kaing'ae laariku nanu enetii enkanasa nasheta?
Kaing'ae laarik nanu Edom?
11Keitu iyie iyany iyiook Iyie Enkai?
Milo iyie, Olaitoriani, te nebo ilmang'ati lang.
12Nchoo siaake iyiook eretoto te nkaraki ilmang'ati lang,
amu ene pesho eretoto e tung'ani!
13Ore etii Olaitoriani te nebo iyiook naa eikiaas mbaa sapuki;
Olaitoriani ninye oitunyoki enkop ilmang'ati lang.

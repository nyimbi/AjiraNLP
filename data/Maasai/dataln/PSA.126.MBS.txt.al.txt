1  Ore apa pee errinyu Olaitoriani lelo ootaarayioki le Sion, nikitiu iyiook anaa ilooidetidet. 

2  Neitoki enkuenia aiput nkutukie ang, neiput ilng'ejepa lang mbuakunot e ng'ida; nejo ninche ti atua nkuapi, “Etaasa Olaitoriani mbaa sapuki te nkaraki ninche.” 

3  Etaasa Olaitoriani mbaa sapuki te nkaraki iyiook; kitishipate iyiook. 

4  Torrinyu lelo lang apa ootaarayioki, Olaitoriani, meikununye anaa nchorroi e Negeb! 

5  Nchoo lelo oounisho too lkiyio eisampu too mbuakunot e ng'ida! 

6  Ore ilo olo eishirita, enapita olantererai olo aun, nelotu ang too mbuakunot e ng'ida, enapita enkisampuare enye.

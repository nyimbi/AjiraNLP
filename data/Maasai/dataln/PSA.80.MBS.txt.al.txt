1   Nchoru enkiok Lo Airritani le Israel, iyie ilo orikoo Yosef anaa eluaata oo ntare! Iyie ilo otii olorika le nkitoo te nchumata oo lkerubi, 

2  nintawang'isho te dukuya Efraim o Benchamen o Manase, tuputu enkidimata ino, nilotu aitajeu iyiook! 

3  Torrinyu iyiook, Na Ai; nchoo enkomom ino ewang'u, pee etumi aaitajeu iyiook! 

4  Olaitoriani Enkai oo lororani, kenkata nabaa iyie igorokino enkomono oo ltung'ana linono? 

5  Nintotio iyie ninche te mukate oo lkiyio, nincho ninche metooko ilkiyio te nkumoi. 

6  Nintaa iyiook ilmang'ati too le latia ang; ore ilmang'ati lang nekueni maate. 

7  Torrinyu iyiook, Na Ai oo lororani; nchoo enkomom ino metawang'a, pee etumi aaitajeu iyiook! 

8  Iyie oyaua isabibu aitung'uaa enkop e Misiri; iyie otarayie iloshon niun ilo sabibu. 

9  Iyie oitukut enkop te nkaraki ninye; neibung ntona naapuo atua enkop neiput enkop. 

10  Neitoip oloip lenye ildoinyo, neitoip ilng'osil lenyena iltarakua sapuki; 

11  nereu ilng'osil lenyena aitabaiki enaiposha, neitabaiki ntona enyena olkeju. 

12  Kainyoo naa iyie pee itigila enkikarrata enye, neaku ore pooki oogiroo aaim enkoitoi, neng'osilu ilng'anayio lenyena? 

13  Neinyala ilbitiro le ntim ninye, ore nena pooki naapuo ti ong'ata neinos ninye. 

14  Nyaaki mbelekenyunye, Na Ai oo lororani! Ing'ura kop itii shumata, nidol, tishilai ele sabibu, 

15  ilo shani lituuno iyie te nkaina ino e tatene. 

16  Etapejoitie ninche ninye te nkurma, netudung'o ninche aauroo; meidaa ninche te nkisikong'ata e nkomom ino! 

17  Kake nchoo enkaina ino elotu shumata ilo le nkaina ino e tatene, ina kerai e tung'ani nintagolo iyie te nkaraki kewan ino! 

18  Nemikingil ae kata iyiook aaibelekenya aaing'uaa iyie; nchoo iyiook enkishui, pee kitum aaipoto enkarna ino! 

19  Torrinyu iyiook Olaitoriani Enkai oo lororani! Nchoo enkomom ino metawang'a, pee etumi aaitajeu iyiook!

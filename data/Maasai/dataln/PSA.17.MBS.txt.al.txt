1  Toning'o esipata, iyie Olaitoriani; toning'o enkishirata ai! Nchoo enkiok ino enkomono ai naing'uaa enkutuk nemetii elejare! 

2  Nchoo eaku iyie eing'uaa esipata ai! Nchoo nkonyek inono edol esipata! 

3  Ti nitem iyie oltau lai, ti nikimutan iyie nanu kewarie, ti nikijur iyie nanu, nimitum iyie entorroni ti atua nanu; eitu apong'ori too nkirorot aainei. 

4  Ore ti alo isiaaitin e tung'ana, ore to rorei lino, natapaasha nanu nkoitoi oo larruok. 

5  Ore roruat aainei neibung'a nkoitoi inono, eitu eideketari nkejek aainei. 

6  Aaipoto nanu iyie, amu eikiwaliki iyie, Olaitoriani; irrugokoki enkiok ino, toning'o irorei laainei. 

7  Ntodolu enyorrata ino nauno te mpukunoto e nking'asia, iyie Laaitajeunoni le lelo likisaru, too lmang'ati lenye te nkaina ino e tatene. 

8  Tagolieki nanu anaa enerok e nkong'u; isudoyioki nanu to loip loo naipuko inono, 

9  te nkaraki ilo torrono laainyal nanu, lelo mang'ati laainei laamanita nanu. 

10  Neitagol ninche iltauja lenye; pee meitoki aata olng'ur, ore too nkutukie enye neiro te nkitokoos. 

11  Naasuj ninche nanu; naatamana taata ninche; naaing'or nanu pee aatum aatanang'ie enkop. 

12  Eikununo ninche anaa olng'atuny oibung eng'ues, etiu anaa olkuoo lo lng'atuny erreshito eng'ues. 

13  Tudumunye, Olaitoriani! Intoriokinoyu ninche taarare, tanang'ai ninche! Tokordu enkishui ai ti alo ilo torrono to lalem lino, 

14  tokorduaki too ltung'ana te nkaina ino, iyie Olaitoriani, tialo lelo tung'ana laa ore erubata enye te nkishui naa ena kop. Ntamio ninche teilo sina litushumaka ninche, menoto nkera enye osina olang ilo, metelekakini ilo sina nkera oo nkera enye. 

15  Ore nanu, naa kaing'or enkomom ino te supatisho, te nainyototo, naraposho te nduaata ino.

1  Nedumunye olorora pooki, nerikokini Pilato. 

2  Neiter aaitang'or ninye, ejo, “Kineputua ele tung'ani eiwushie olorere lang nemitiki iyiook kincho Kaisari kodi, nejo kewan Kristo naa olaiguanani.” 

3  Neikilikuan Pilato ninye ajoki, “Ai yie doi Olaiguanani lo Lyahudi?” Newaliki ajoki, “Iyie otejo neijia.” 

4  Nejoki Pilato lapolosak kituaak o lorora, “Eitu atum entiyoto te le tung'ani.” 

5  Kake eitirrishakita ejo, “Keing'oling'ol olorere, eiteng'enita te Yudea pooki, ebaiki Galilaya o meeu ene.” Etorikokoki Yesu Herode 

6  Ore pee ening Pilato ina, neikilikuan te na kole Galilaya. 

7  Ore pee ening ajo ole nkalo naitore Herode, neirriwaki Herode etii ninye Yerusalem ina kata. 

8  Ore pee edol Herode Yesu, neshipa oleng, amu keyieu apa nedol te nkata naado, amu ketoning'o imbaa enyena, nejo sii pee edol neas embae e nking'asia. 

9  Ore te nkaraki ina neikilikuanishore te nkata naado; kake eitu ewaliki. 

10  Neitashoo lapolosak kituaak o lasirak aaitirrishaki aaitang'or ninye. 

11  Nesul Herode o sikarini lenyena, neikordod; neitoki neishopoki olkila nerrinyoki Pilato.

26  Ore erikitoi, neibung'i oltung'ani obo oji Simon le Kirene, oewuo eing'uaa nkurman, neitanapa osalaba, metanapa esujita Yesu. 

27  Nesuj olorora kitok le lelo tung'ana, o nkituaak naaishiraki eoshiosh ilgooitie. 

28  Kake eibelekenye Yesu aing'or nejoki, “Na toyie e Yerusalem, emikinchiraki nanu, kake ate enchiraki o nkera inyi, 

29  amu ng'ura, kepuonu nkolong'i naaku keji, ‘Emayiana nkolupeno, nemayiana nkoishuaa neitu eisho, nemayiana ilki leitu enaki!’ 

30   Neaku kejokini ldoinyo, ‘Entuurokinoto iyiook,’ nejokini ndoinyo, ‘Entoipoi iyiook.’ 

31  Amu, pasa taa eikoni nji olchani onyori, kaji eikoni olotoyio?” 

32  Neriki sii kulikae wushuush aare, metari te nebo ninye. 

33  Ore pee ebaiki ewueji naji Emborbor, neshe ninye te ine, o lelo maisero, atii obo te tatene netii olikae te kedianye. 

34   Nejo Yesu, “Papa, tapalaki ninche, amu ai meyiolo enaasita.” Neosh ninche enkukuo pee etum aatoor nkilani enyena. 

35   Neitashoo ltung'ana te ine, aaing'or; kake kesurdaany ilaitoriak, ejo, “Eitajeuo kulikae, nchoo naa eitajeu kewan te naa ai ninye doi Kristo le Nkai, ilo gelunoti lenye.” 

36   Nesurdaany sii ninche isikarini, epuonu aayaki enaisho naisuijo 

37  ejo, “Te naa ira Olaiguanani loo Lyahudi, intajeu kewan!” 

38  Etisiraki sii olkigeroto te shumata elukunya enye ojo, “Ele Olaiguanani loo Lyahudi.” 

39  Nemor obo le lelo wushuush ootashoki te nebo ninye, ejo, “Ai mira doi Kristo? Intajeu kewan o iyiook!” 

40  Neisikong olikae ajoki, “Ai miure iyie Enkai, nidol ajo ina kiguana kitudung'okoki sii iyie? 

41  Neishiaakino ninche iyiook; amu inikitaasa eitalakitae iyiook; kake meeta hoo ele entorrono nataasa.” 

42  Nejoki, “Yesu, tadamuaki nanu te nilotu te ngolon ino e nkitoria.”

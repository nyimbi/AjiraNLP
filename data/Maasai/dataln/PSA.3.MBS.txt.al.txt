1  Iyie Olaitoriani, meikenayu ilmang'ati laainei! Aatudumakinote nanu ilkumok aagilunore; 

2  ejoito ilkumok ti alo nanu, metii eretoto naing'uaa Enkai te nkaraki ninye. 

3  Kake ore iyie Olaitoriani, ira elong'o naamanita nanu, iyie enkitoo ai, naa iyie likishumu endukuya. 

4  Atabuakua nanu aishiraki Olaitoriani, naawaliki ninye nanu te ilo doinyo lenye sinyati. 

5  Airraga nanu nairura; naitoki ainyototo, amu Olaitoriani laarripito. 

6  Maure nanu iltung'ana nkalifuni tomon, lelo laatudumakinote nanu aarare naamuat. 

7  Inyo, iyie Olaitoriani! Tokorduaki nanu na Ai ai! Amu itoosho iyie ilmang'ati laainei pooki eseder, itigila iyie ilala le lelo arruok. 

8  Eno Laitoriani enkordunoto meetu mayianat inono shumata iltung'ana linono.

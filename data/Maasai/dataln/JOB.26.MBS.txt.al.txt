1  Neitoki Yoab awal ajo: 

2  “Kebaa eretoto nitareto ilo lemeeta engolon. Intajewuo ina aina nemeeta engolon! 

3  Inkoo ilo lemeeta engeno nagut, nincho ninye te lengon engeno sapuk. 

4  Aiteretoto e ng'ae iyie iroro irorei naa ae te nkiyang'et e ng'ae etupukutuo te iyie? 

5  Eikirikira iloipi liabori, o nkariak o lelo oomanya. 

6  Meishopo enemeneng'a te dukuya Enkai, nemeeta Abadon entoki naitoipieki. 

7  Eisalaash moikuape aisalaashaki olngam neik enkop aililioki olngam. 

8  Een inkariak too nkatampo naairrusha, nemepolosa inkatampo tiabori ninche. 

9  Eitoip ninye olapa neisalaashaki ninye enkatampoi enye. 

10  Etuutua olokelel tioriong nkariak, to lpolosie le rishata ewang'an o enaimin. 

11  Eikirikira ing'apeta e keper, neirut aaiputukuny te nkisikong'ata enye. 

12  Eitigir enaiposha te ngolon enye; te ng'eno enye etoosho Rahab. 

13  To lkutati lenye eitisidanaki keper; nerem enkaina enye olasurai oilanyisho. 

14  Ngura, ore kuna naa nkuti ake oo nkoitoi naa kebaa enkitisho enking'oming'om oo mbaa enyena nikitoning'o! Naa amaa endarata engolon enye, aing'ae oidim atoning'u?”

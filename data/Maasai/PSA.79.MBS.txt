Enkomono te nkaraki enkordunoto o losho
1 Na Ai, eetuo lelo limikiyiolo aajing atua enjung'ore ino;
etipika ninche olminong atua enkaji ino sinyati;
eitaa ninche Yerusalem olmuaate.
2Eishoo ninche mpopoki oo seseni loo sinkan linono,
ilmotoni lo loing'ang'e meitaa endaa,
ore lelo sinyat linono neinos ing'uesi e nkop
nkiri oo seseni lenye.
3Eibukoitie isargeta lenye anaa
enkare te manaroto pooki e Yerusalem,
nemeetae hoo obo onukaa ninche.
4Kitaa iyiook entoki nailapi te lelo le latia ang,
nesukenyi neikuyukuyaki lelo oomanita iyiook.
5Kenkata nabaa Olaitoriani?
Igoro iyie ntarasi?
Keyupuyup ina goro o lom lino anaa enkima?
6Tubukoki engoro ino lelo oshon limikiyiolo iyie,
o shumata nena kitoriat nemeipot enkarna ino!
7Amu eidaikie ninche Yakobo,
neitomijoon emanyisho enye.
8Midamu te iyiook entorroni oo loo papa;
nchoo olng'ur lino metasieku anang'are iyiook,
amu eitadouoki iyiook oleng.
9Taretu iyiook, Na Ai e njeunoto ang,
te nkaraki enkitoo e nkarna ino;
tokordu iyiook, nipalaki iyiook ing'ok ang,
te nkaraki enkarna ino!
10Kainyoo pee ejo iloshon,
“Koree Enkai enye?”
Nchoo ina lap,
apa enkibukoroto o sarge loo sinkan linono
metayiolouni too loshon pooki
te dukuya nkonyek ang!
11Nchoo ilo kiyioi le lelo ooena meeu dukuya iyie;
ore te risioroto e ngolon ino kitok
tagolie lelo ootudung'okoki keeya!
12Ntalaa katitin naapishana lelo
le latia ang too lgooitie lenye
nena kilapat apa nikilapa ninche iyie,
Olaitoriani!
13Ore taa iyiook iltung'ana linono,
ina luaata e nkirrita ino,
nikincho iyie enashe ntarasi;
ore ebaiki iishoritin o ishoritin,
nikilimu enkisisa ino.

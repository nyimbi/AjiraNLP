Enkishirata te nkaraki eretoto
1Iyie Olaitoriani, Enkai ai,
iyie nanu aipot taretuoki dama;
naishir nanu kewarie te dukuya iyie.
2Nchoo enkomono ai meeu dukuya iyie,
irrugu enkiok ino toning'o enkishirata ai!
3Amu eiputa oltau lai enyamali;
netataanikia enkishui ai enemeneng'a!
4Aaikenakaki nanu lelo oodoiki engumoto;
ara nanu oltung'ani lemeeta engolon,
5atotiuo anaa oltung'ani oiturrayioki ti atua ilootuata,
anaa oltung'ani otaraki metua neirragieki te ngumoto,
anaa ltung'ana lemeekure edamuni,
amu eiwuang'ieki ninche te nkaina ino.
6Kitipika iyie nanu atua enguton e ngumoto,
iwuejitin neemisimis negut.
7Eirragita engoro ino nairoishi te shumata nanu,
nikindaikie nanu too lopir lo siwuo lino.
8Nchoo iyie lelo le ncholuek ai maaitiaa nanu,
kintaa iyie nanu entoki naitureisho te ninche.
Aaikenoyioki nanu metaa maidim atuluai,
9eiliilaitie nkonyek aainei te nkaraki osina.
Ore nkolong'i pooki naipot nanu iyie Olaitoriani,
Nailepie aitorioo nkaik aainei te nkomono.
10Ias iyie mbaa e nking'asia too lootuata?
Eikinyototo iloipi aaisis iyie?
11Keibalunyeki ina nyorrata ino nauno ti atua enkurare,
anaa esupatisho ino te Abadon?
12Keyioloi mbaa inono e nking'asia ti atua enaimin,
anaa ina retoto ino naitajeusho te nkop nemedamuni?
13Kake ore nanu, Olaitoriani, iyie aishiraka;
ore tadekenya nelotu enkomono ai dukuya iyie.
14Olaitoriani, kainyoo pee kinturrayie nanu,
kainyoo iyie pee kisudorie nanu enkomom ino?
15Aainosa nanu osina neyieu aye apa ebaiki kitisho ai,
aisilisile nanu too nkituriat inono;
ataa nanu pesho.
16Aaisiasha nanu engoro ino;
aaidaikie nanu ioshot inono.
17Aatamana ninche anaa olairrag le nkare endama pooki;
naaman ninche aang'adu te nebo.
18Nchoo iyie ilchoreta laainei laning'ore maaitiaa nanu,
netaa iltung'ana enaimin ake aisiritore.

Enkai naitore enkop pooki
1Entaranyaki Olaitoriani osinkolio ng'ejuk,
amu etaasa ninye mbaa e nking'asia!
Ore ina aina enye e tatene,
o ina aina enye sinyati, netimirishe.
2Eibalunye Olaitoriani enkisulata enye,
eibalunye ninye enkitoo te dukuya iloreren pooki.
3Etadamua ninye ina nyorrata enye nauno o esupatisho enye
te nkaji e Israel.
Ore nkiting'ot e nkop pooki netoduaa enkisulata e Nkai ang.
4Entaranyaki Olaitoriani te ng'ida,
na kop pooki;
entabuaku to sinkolio le nchipai entarany te nkisisa!
5Entaranyaki enkisisa Olaitoriani to lwulul loo usin,
to lwulul loo usin o to ltoilo lo sinkolio!
6Too ntarumpetani o to ltoilo le mouo
entayu oltoilo le nchipai te dukuya Olaiguanani,
laa ninye Olaitoriani!
7Nchoo eikurrukur enaiposha o pooki toki natii atua;
o enkop o pooki oomanya!
8Ilugoyu enaiposha te nchipai;
nchoo eranyisho ildoinyo te ng'ida te nebo,
9te dukuya Olaitoriani, amu kelotu ninye aiguanare enkop.
Keiguanare ninye enkop te supatisho
neiguanare iloreren too sipat.

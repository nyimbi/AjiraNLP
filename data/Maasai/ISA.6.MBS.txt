Eipoto Enkai Isaya aitaa oloiboni lenye
1 Ore te ilo ari apa otuayie olaiguanani oji Usia, atoduaa nanu Olaitoriani etonita to lorika le nkitoo. Neilepieki ninye oleng. Ore olkila lenye neiput enkaji e Nkai. 2Ore te shumata ninye neitashe iserafi. Neeta ake obo inaipuko ile. Ore are neimulumulie enkomom enye, ore are neimulumulie nkejek enyena, ore are nelotie tiaai. 3 Neipoto ake maate ejo: “Sinyati, Sinyati, Sinyati Olaitoriani loo lororani; eiputa enkop pooki enkitoo enye.” 4 Neikirikira olkitamanyunoto le Nkaji e Nkai te nkaraki ina buakunoto, neiput ina aji empuruo. 5Najo nanu: “Woi kewan ai! Amu aimina nanu; amu ara nanu oltung'ani loo nkirorot torrok; naton tiatua iltung'ana loo nkirorot torrok; amu etoduaa nkonyek aainei Olaiguanani, ilo Aitoriani loo lororani!”
6Nelotu obo le lelo serafi enatii, neibung'ita te nkaina enye enkukuo naishu, nayaua to lperere lo lasar. 7Neiseyie enkutuk ai, nejo, “Ng'ura, eiseyie ena enkutuk ino; eiwuang'ieki entorroni ino, nikitapalakaki ing'ok inono.” 8Naning nanu oltoilo lo Laitoriani ejo, “Kaing'ae nanu airriwaa, naa kaing'ae olo te nkaraki iyiook?” Najo nanu, “Nele nanu! Irriwayioki.” 9 Nejo ninye, “Shomo, tiaaki ele orere: ‘Ining'ining'i, kake miyiolouu; Idolidolo kake mimususu.’ 10Iroshiyie iltauja le kulo tung'ana, nintiming'ana nkiyiaa enye, ninkeno nkonyek enye; pee medolisho too nkonyek enye, nemening'isho too nkiyiaa enye, neyiolou too ltauja lenye, neibelekenya pee eishiunyeki.” 11Naitoki nanu ajo, “Oi anu, Olaitoriani?” Nejo ninye: “O metaba anaa nemijoonu nkanas memanya toki, o nkajijik metii iltung'ana, ore enkop neliyioyu tukul, 12neiwuang'ie Olaitoriani iltung'ana aitalamaa oleng, neaku ilmuaateni kumok te mpolos e nkop. 13Ore hoo duoo ninye te neleku ematua nabo e tomon, keitokini aapejoo, ketiu ninye anaa enkobei o lchani onyaanyukie olng'aboli naing'uari eitashe, eidipaki aatuuroi.” Ilo antererai sinyati ninye ina obei.

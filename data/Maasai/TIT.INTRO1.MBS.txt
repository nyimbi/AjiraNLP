ENAITURUK
Ore apa TITO naa oltung'ani loo loreren oiruko Kristo neaku olaretoni le Paulo te siaai enye elikioroto o rorei. Ore empalai e Paulo nairriwaka Tito, netisira ajo olaisiaayiani lai oti otii Krete, otung'uayioki apa teine meitasheiki esiaai teina sirit. Ore ena palai, neutu imbaa uni sapuki naataa Paulo to ltau lenye.
(1) Ore enaituruk, neng'as aitadamu Tito imbaa naaishiaakino neeta ilarikok le sirit e Kristo, eisul ninye tolkuaak torrono loo ltung'ana kumok le Krete. (2) Enyaaka ake Paulo arerioo Tito eneiko teneiteng'en ilturruri opaashipaasha tiatua esirit aa ore ilpayiani kituaak, o ntasati kituaak (neitamok intasati kituaak inkituaak kutiti metonyorra ilpayiani lenye o nkera enye) o ilbarnot o rrindiki. (3) Ore enabayie, eigeroko Paulo Tito eutaroto naipirta olkuaak lo lairukoni, eisul ninye pee eaku oloota eseriani nenyorrisho sii, nepaashare enkiba, o enchankar, o eoro tiatua esirit.

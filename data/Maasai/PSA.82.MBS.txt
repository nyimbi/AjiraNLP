Enkai naata enkitoria pooki
1Etii Enkai ewueji enye ti atua eumata enye;
ore te mpolos oo nkaitin neiguana ninye:
2“Kenkata nabaa iyie inguana too ntorrok,
nintodolu oloshomitore te ilo torrono?
3Nguanaki te sipata ilo omena o ilo okishi;
mbung'a esipata e ilo oata osina o ilo okishi.
4Tokordu ilo oshal o ilo aisinani;
tokordu ninche ntung'uai enkaina e ilo torrono.”
5Meeta ninche eng'eno nemedamisho,
nepuo ninche aamanimanaa te naimin;
eikirikirate ilkitamanyunot pooki le nkop.
6 Najo nanu, “Irara intae nkaitin,
nkera e ilo Otii Shumata Aisul,
intae pooki;
7ore ake eyia,
iyeye intae anaa iltung'ana,
nibatatata anaa pooki tung'ani kitok.”
8Tudumunye, iyie Enkai, nguanare enkop;
amu ilkulinono iloreren pooki!

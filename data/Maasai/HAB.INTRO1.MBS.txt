ENAITURUK
Ore irorei lo loiboni le Nkai oji HABAKUKI neetuo te rishata enkiting'oto o lari le 700 (B.C.) erishata neton etii ilbabilon enkitoria. Eturrumoyioki oleng oltau lenye peyie edol enkikonya o somit oitaasita Ilbabilon olorere. Ore enkaraki ina, neikilikuan ninye Enkai ejoki “Ainyoo peyie igira iyie einyalita ninche iltung'ana supati aalang ninche?” 1.13 Ore ewalata o Laitoriani naa nchere ekeitaas ninye ninche enayieu te nkata enye sidai, neaku ore tena rishata, “Ore lelo loo sipat naa ketoni eishu amu keisiligita ninche Enkai.” 2.4
Ore enkae seshati embuku pooki naa enaibon enkidaata te lelo leme supati, ore te bayie embuku neutu olkerempe oshipakinoi enkitoo e Nkai neibalie sii enkirukoto nauno olasirani lele kerempe.

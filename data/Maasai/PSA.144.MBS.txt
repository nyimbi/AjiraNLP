Eishoo olaiguanani Enkai enashe te nkaraki enkisulata
1meisisi Olaitoriani,
endapidapoi ai,
laa ninye oitaarriyian nkaik aainei to larrabal,
neitayiolo ilkimojik laainei olarrabal;
2endapidapoi ai naa osempewuet lai,
enkisudoroto ai naa olakordunoni lai,
elong'o ai naa ninye ilo lasaru nanu,
laa ninye opik iloreren abori ninye.
3 Olaitoriani, kainyoo tung'ani lishilaa ninye,
anaa enkerai e tung'ani eji nidamu ninye?
4Etiu oltung'ani anaa enkiyang'et,
etiu nkolong'i enyena anaa oloip oloito.
5Irrugu keper ino, Olaitoriani, nilotu adou!
Iseyie ildoinyo pee epuru!
6Imerlelie nkiwang'at nindapashaa ninche,
ning'oru mbaa inono aitanyamal ninche!
7Ntorioi enkaina ino te keper,
tokorduaki nanu nikintayu nanu ti atua nkariak kumok,
ntung'uayioki nkaik oo leeiking'a,
8lelo ooiro elejare too nkutukie enye,
laa ore enkaina enye e tatene,
naa enkaina e tatene e lejare.
9Naa kaaranyaki nanu iyie osinkolio ng'ejuk, Iyie Enkai;
te nkauo oo sinkolioitin naata iusin tomon nanu aaranyaki iyie,
10iyie oisho ilaiguanak meisula,
iyie otokordua Daudi osinka lino.
11Tokorduaki nanu te ilo alem torrono,
nikikordu nanu aitung'uaa enkaina e lelo leeiking'a,
lelo ooiro elejare too nkutukie enye,
laa ore enkaina enye e tatene naa enkaina e tatene e lejare.
12Metotiwuo ilayiok lang te lelero enye
anaa nkaitubulu naatubulutua aabaya,
metotiutu ntoyie ang anaa ing'apeta oo lmairrot,
naatudung'uoki pee eshetieki
enkaji e nkitoo o laiguanani;
13metaboreita nkajijik ang naashumishoreki
meiputa mpukunot pooki oo ntokitin;
metubula nkerrai ang,
aaku nkalifuni o nkalifuni tomon too ndaat;
14menoto nkishu ang oloing'oni,
nemetum olkibiroto anaa ae torroni;
metaa metumi olkiyioi lo sina too nkoitoi ang!
15Emayiana lelo tung'ana ootum ena mayianata!
Emayiana iltung'ana laa Enkai ninye Olaitoriani lenye!

Enkomono te nkaraki esipata
1Kainyoo pee intashe alamari,
iyie Olaitoriani?
Kainyoo iyie pee isudoo kewan
too nkatitin oo ng'olikinot.
2To lwuasa emir lelo torrok ilaisinak;
nchoo meibung'a ninche lelo rresheta ooitobira.
3Amu keitokoos ilo torrono too yieunot o ltau lenye,
ore ilo tung'ani oata olorok loo masaa nemenaa Enkai.
4Ore to lwuasa le nkomom enye nemeing'oru ninye Enkai;
ore too ndamunot enyena pooki naa,
“Meetae Enkai.”
5Nebul too nkoitoi enyena nkatitin pooki;
ore nkiguanat inono netii shumata,
mebaiki ninye;
ore ilmang'ati lenyena pooki neikordod ninche.
6Nedamu ninye to ltau lenye ajo,
“Maaigusugusieki nanu;
ore ti atua iishoritin pooki
matum nanu batisho.”
7 Ebore enkutuk enye ildeketa o elejare o enkitorogonyare;
ore abori olng'ejep lenye netii ntabo o entorroni.
8Neton ninye to lnukata too nkang'itie,
ore ti atua iwuejitin neeisudoriyie nesesekuan lelo supati.
Neikeern lelo ooshal,
9neirrag ninye to lnukata lenye anaa olng'atuny;
neirrag ninye pee etum aibunga ilo aisinani,
neibung ninye ilo aisinani
te nemiraki ninye atua orreshet lenye.
10Nerrarri ilo oshal, neimisieki te nkop,
nebatata te ngolon enye.
11Nejo to ltau lenye,
“Etorikine Enkai, eisudoyie ninye enkomom enye,
medol ae kata ina.”
12Tudumunye, iyie Olaitoriani, iyie Enkai,
tudumu enkaina ino;
mincho kirikino ilo oata osina.
13Kainyoo pee eany ilo torrono Enkai,
nejo to ltau lenye, “Maaitamie ninye nanu.”
14Idol iyie; ee, itoduaa iyie enyamali o sina,
pee itum iyie aiwuang'ie too nkaik inono;
ore ilo oshal naa iyie eigarakino;
iyie otaa olaretoni loo lokishin.
15Tigila iyie enkaina e ilo torrono o eno larruoni;
tusuja entorroni enye o metaba anaa neishunye.
16Olaitoriani ninye olaiguanani ntarasi o ntarasi;
keidak iloshon te nkaina enye.
17Iyie Olaitoriani,
ining iyie eyieunoto eilo obor;
nintagol iyie ninye oshoke
nirrugoki enkiok ino
18pee inguenare te sipata ilo okishi o ilo oibookoki enkop,
paa ore ilo tung'ani le nkop nemeigil ae kata aitureisho.

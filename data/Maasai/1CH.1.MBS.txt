Ebaiki Adam o metabaiki Abraham
(Enk 5.1-32, 10.1-32, 11.10-26)
1Adam, Seti, Enosh, 2Kenan o Mahalael, Yared; 3Enok, o Metusela, o Lamek 4Noa, o Shem, o Ham o Yafet.
5Ore apa ilayiok le Yafet naa Gomer, o Magog, o Madai o Yafan, o Tubal o Meshek o Tiras. 6Ore ilayiok le Gomer naa Askenas, o Difat, o Togarma. 7Ore ilayiok le Yafan naa Elisha, o Tarshish o Kitim o Rodanim.
8Ore ilayiok le Ham naa Kushi o Misiri o Puti o Kanaan. 9Ore ilayiok le Kushi naa Seba, o Hafila, o Sabta o Raama o Sableka. Ore ilayiok le Raama naa Sheba o Dedan. 10Kushi apa menye le Nimrod, naa ninye otang'asa aaku oltung'ani magilani te nkop.
11Misiri apa menye le Ludim, o Anamim, o Lehabim, o Napatuhim, o 12Patusim o Kusluhim (aa ine taa eing'uaa Ilfilisti, o Kaftorim.)
13Kanaan apa menye le Sidon, ilo kikau lenye, o Heti, 14o Lyebusi o Lamori o Lgirgashi, 15o Lhifi ole Arki o Lsini, o 16Lafadi, o Lsamari, o Lhamati.
17Ore ilayiok le Shem naa Elam o Ashur o Arpakashad o Lud, o Aram o Usi o Huli o Feter, o Meshek, 18Apakashad apa menye le Shela; naa Shela menye le Eber. 19Etoikioki apa Eber ilayiok oare, ore enkarna e obo naa Peleg (amu too nkolong'i enyena apa etooroki enkop) ore enkarna o lalashe naa Yoktin. 20Yoktin apa menye le Almodad, o Shelaf o Hasamafet, o Yera. 21o Hadoram, o Usal, o Dikla, 22o Ebal o Abimael o Sheba, 23o Ofir, o Hafila o Yobab; ore kulo pooki naa ilayiok le Yoktan. 24O shem, o Arpakshad o Shela, 25o Eber o Peleg o Reu; 26o Serug o Nahor o Tera; 27o Abram aa taa Abraham.
Eishoi e Ishmael
(Enk 25.12-16)
28Ore ilayiok le Abraham naa Isaak o Ishmael. 29Kuna ninche iiishoritin enye. Ore olkikau le Ishmael naa Nebaiot; o Kedar, o Adbel Mibsan, 30o Mishma, o Duma, o Masa, o Hadad, o Tema, 31o Yetur o Nafish o Kedema. Kulo ninche ilayiok le Ishmael. 32Ore apa ilayiok le Ketura, ina apiani e Abraham, netoiuo Simra, o Yokshan o Medan o Midian o Ishbak o Shua. Ore ilayiok le Yokshan naa Sheba o Dedan. 33Kulo ninche ilayiok le Midian, Efa o Efer, o Hanok o Abida o Eldaa. Kulo pooki ninche eishoi e Ketura.
Eishoi e Esau
(Enk 36.1-19)
34Abraham apa menye le Isaak. Kulo ninche ilayiok le Isaak, Esau o Israel. 35Ore ilayiok le Esau naa Elfas, o Reul, o Yeush, Yalaam o Kora. 36Ore ilayiok le Elfas naa Teman, o Omar o Sefi o Satam o Kenas o Timna o Amaleki. 37Kulo ninche ilayiok le Ruel, Nahat, o Serak, o Shama o Miisa.
Iltung'ana apa ootang'asa aamany Edom
(Enk 36.20-30)
38Kulo ninche ilayiok le Seir: Lotan, o Shobal, o Sibeon, o Anak, o Dishon, o Eser o Dishan. 39Kulo ninche ilayiok le Lotan: Hori o Homan, ore apa enkanashe e Lotan naa Timna. 40Kulo ninche ilayiok le Shobai: Alian o Manahat o Ebai, o Shefi, o Onam. Ore ilayiok le Sibeon naa Aia o Anak. 41Kulo ninche ilayiok le Anak: Dishon ore ilayiok le Dishon naa Hamran o Eshban, o Itran o Keran. 42Kulo ninche ilayiok le Eser, Bilhan, o Saafan o Yaakan. Ore ilayiok le Dishon naa Usi o Aran.
Ilaiguanak le Edom
(Enk 36.31-43)
43Kulo aiguanak apa ooitoreisho te enkop e Edom, eton eitu eitore hoo likae aiguanani Isiraeli: Bela ole Boer, laa ninye eipotieki enkanasa naji Dinhaba. 44Ore pee eye Bela neitoreisho Yohab ole Serak te rishata enye. 45Ore pee eye Yohab, neitoreisho Husam ole nkop oo Ltaman te rishata enye. 46Ore pee eye Husham ore Hadad ole Bedad laa ninye apa otimira Midian te nkop e Moab neitoreisho te rishata enye; ore enkarna enkanasa enye naa Afit. 47Ore pee eye Hadad, neitoreisho Samala le Masreka te rishata enye. 48Ore pee eye Samli, ore Shaul le Rehobot to lkeju le Frati neitoreisho te rishata enye. 49Ore pee eye Shaul neitoreisho Baalhanan ole Akbor te rishata enye. 50Ore pee eye Baalhanan neitoreisho Hadad te rishata enye, ore enkarna e nkanasa naa Pai, ore enkarna e nkitok enye naa Mahetabel, entito e Matred, entito e Mesaba. Neye Hadad.
51Ore ilaiguanak loo loshon le Edom naa; olaiguanani Timna, o Alka, Yetet, 52o Oholibama, o Ela, o Pinon o Kenas, 53o Teman, o Mibsar, o Magdiel, o Iram, 54kulo ninche ilaiguanak le Edom.

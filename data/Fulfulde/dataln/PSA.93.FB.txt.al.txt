1  Jawmiraawo woni laamiiɗo. O ɗon ɓorni maŋngu. Jawmiraawo ɓornake ngu, ɗon moji baawɗe. Duniyaaru tabiti, dimmbataako. 

2  Leeso laamu ma ɗon tabiti diga fuɗɗam, diga ɓooyma, a ɗon. 

3  Maaje mawɗe ɓaŋgti sawtu, yaa Jawmiraawo, ɗe ɓaŋgti sawtu, ɗe kooli. 

4  Ammaa maŋngu Jawmiraawo Ɓaŋiiɗo ɓuri sawtu ndiyamji ɗuuɗɗi e hoolo ƴuuge mbeela ƴaggiiɗe. 

5  Yaa Jawmiraawo, seedamkuuji ma ɗi koolniiɗi masin, senaare fottani suudu ma haa foroy.

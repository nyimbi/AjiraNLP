40  :

14  -

18  ) 

1  Ngam kaliifaajo gime. Yimre Daawuda ngam ciftinorki. 

2  Yaa Allah, hisnam. Yaa Jawmiraawo, yaawɗa wallugo yam. 

3  Yiɗɓe mbarugo yam cemta. Seyortooɓe sarru am, lornu ɓe ɓaawo, toskin ɓe! 

4  Jalanɓe yam, sey ɓe loro ɓaawo daliila toskaare maɓɓe! 

5  Ammaa ɗakkotirɓe bee ma fuu, yaa Jawmiraawo, hokku ɓe seyo e welwelo! Yiɗɓe kisndam maaɗa mbi'a foroy: “Allah woni mawɗo!” 

6  Min ma mi laafuɗo, mi talakaajo, yaa Allah, war haa am law! An woni balloowo yam, kisnoowo yam, taa neeɓu, yaa Allah am!

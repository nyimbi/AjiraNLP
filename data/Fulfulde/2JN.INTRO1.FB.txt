Nastuki
“Ndottiijo” winndani “mayramjo e ɓiyum'en” bataakewol ngo'ol. Foti “mayramjo e ɓiyum'en”, ɗum jama'aare nuɗɗinɓe haa wuro feere. O wasi ɓe ɓe ngiɗindira, ɓe njogo ekkitinol Yeesu Almasiihu, ɓe ndaayo ekkitinooɓe pewe.
Tokkindirki haalaaji:
Fuɗɗoode 1-3
Maŋngu yiide 4-6
Deentinki dow ekkitinol fewre 7-11
Koofli 12-13

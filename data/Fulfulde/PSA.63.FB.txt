Yenwugo Allah
1Yimre Daawuda, yaake o ɗonno nder ladde hamaadaare haa Yahudiya.
2Yaa Allah, an woni Allah am, mi yiɗi ɗakkotirgo bee ma masin,
mi ɗon yenwa gondal bee maaɗa.
Mi ɗon nder lesdi njoorndi laatiindi harde ndi walaa ndiyam,
ɓanndu am fuu ɗon yenwe.
3Mi laari ma nder suudu ma seniindu,
mi yi'i baawɗe ma e tedduŋgal ma.
4Ngam mbooɗeeŋga ma ɓuri ngeendam,
ngam man mi maŋgtete.
5Mi maŋgtete balɗe am fuu,
bee innde ma, mi ɓaŋgtan juuɗe am ngam toraago ma.
6Ɓernde am welweltan bana caftuɗo nyaamduuji ɓurɗi welugo,
hunnduko am yettete bee seyo.
7Mi siftorte to mi waalake dow be'itte am,
mi numtan haala ma jemmaare fuu.
8Ngam an woni balloowo yam,
mi yiman bee seyo les bileeji ma.
9Mi ɗon ɗakkotiri bee ma,
nyaamo ma ɗon jogi yam.
10Ammaa yiɗɓe mbarugo yam,
ɓe nastan joonde maayɓe.
11Kaafahi mbaran ɓe,
ɓe laatoto geɗal cuuli.
12Laamiiɗo seyoto ngam daliila Allah,
hunortooɓe innde maako mantoto,
ngam o sukkan kunnduɗe fewooɓe.

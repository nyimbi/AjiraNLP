Tornde ngam ballal
(Jabuura 40:14-18)
1Ngam kaliifaajo gime. Yimre Daawuda ngam ciftinorki.
2Yaa Allah, hisnam.
Yaa Jawmiraawo, yaawɗa wallugo yam.
3Yiɗɓe mbarugo yam cemta.
Seyortooɓe sarru am, lornu ɓe ɓaawo, toskin ɓe!
4Jalanɓe yam, sey ɓe loro ɓaawo daliila toskaare maɓɓe!
5Ammaa ɗakkotirɓe bee ma fuu, yaa Jawmiraawo,
hokku ɓe seyo e welwelo!
Yiɗɓe kisndam maaɗa mbi'a foroy:
“Allah woni mawɗo!”
6Min ma mi laafuɗo, mi talakaajo,
yaa Allah, war haa am law!
An woni balloowo yam, kisnoowo yam,
taa neeɓu, yaa Allah am!

Nastuki
Bataakewol Piyer ɗiɗaɓol winndaama haa nuɗɗinɓe ɗuuɗɓe wonɓe haa nokkuuje feere feere. Wakkati man ekkitinooɓe pewe ɗonno ɓilla nuɗɗinɓe bee pewe muuɗum'en. Ngam jaalaago pewe ɗe'e, sey nuɗɗinɗo ɓesda anndugo Allah e Jawmiraawo men Yeesu Almasiihu, o tabito boo nder kawtal bee Yeesu Almasiihu mo ɓadake loraago. To o lori, o hiitan yedduɓe mo, o waddan asama kesa e lesdi kesri.
Piyer wolwi boo dow yiide Allah heedi yimɓe fuu. Almasiihu “yiɗaa koo moy halka, o yiɗi yimɓe fuu tuuba.” (3:9) Kanjum waɗi o loraaki tawon.
Tokkindirki haalaaji:
Koofŋgol 1:1-2
Noy ɓesdirgo anndal nder Yeesu Almasiihu? 1:4-15
Ekkitinol gooŋgawol e ekkitinol pewoowol 1:16—2:22
Seedamku Piyer 1:16-21

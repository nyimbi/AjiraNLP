Nastuki
Filemon o nuɗɗinɗo, kuuwdiɗɗiraawo Pol. Kaŋko o mari maccuɗo mo innde mum Onesimus. Maccuɗo oo dogguno jawmum, fotti bee Pol mo ɗonno haa fursina yaake man. Pol waazini mo Linjiila, Onesimus boo nuɗɗini, laati Masiihiŋkeejo. Nder bataakewol ngo'ol, Pol tori Filemon o jaɓɓa maccuɗo maako mo Pol lornanta mo, ngam Onesimus laataaki maccuɗo jaafaaɗo tan, ammaa deerɗiraawo maako nuɗɗinɗo Almasiihu boo.
Tokkindirki haalaaji:
Koofŋgol 1-3
Yiide e nuɗɗinki Filemon 4-7
Tornde Pol heedi Filemon ngam Onesimus 8-22
Koofli ragareeji 23-25

Kuli kembisha mukalamba. Ukwimba ne filimba fya nsale. Ukulingana na Sheminiti.
Ulwimbo lwakwa Davidi.
1We Yawe ala windubulwisha mu cipyu
nelyo kunjalulwila mu bukali.
2Umbelelefye uluse, we Yawe ico ine nshakosa.
Umposhe we Yawe, pantu amafupa yandi yalekalipwa.
3Umutima wandi naushupikwa.
Bushe ndeba ifi ukufika ku nshita nshi?
4We Yawe pilibukila kuli ine no kumpokolola.
Mpususha pa mulandu wa kutemwa kobe ukushipwa.
5Takwaba umuntu uukwibukisha panuma ya mfwa,
Bushe ni nani engakulumbanya ilyo ali ku calo ca bafwa?
6Ine nimfunshika kukuteta ubushiku bonse,
ifilamba filabomfya no musengele wandi,
pamo ne cakusailapo candi.
7Amenso yandi nayanaka ku bulanda ndefilwa no kulolesha,
abalwani bandi ebalenga ifi.
8Ntalukeni imwe bonse mwe bacita ifyabipa,
pantu Yawe naumfwa ukulila kwandi.
9Yawe naumfwa inkuuta yandi,
alapokelela ukupepa kwandi.
10Abalwani bandi bonse bakekatwa insoni,
Bali nokubwelelamo aba nsoni ishingi.

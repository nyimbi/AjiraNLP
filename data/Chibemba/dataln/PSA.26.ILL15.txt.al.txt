1  Ushininkishe umulandu wandi we Yawe, pantu naikala umweo wakaele, natetekela muli Yawe ukwabula ukutelententa. 

2  Unengule no kunjesha we Yawe, ufwailishe mu mutima pamo na mu matontonkanyo yandi. 

3  Icitemwiko icishipwa caba pali ine lyonse, na ine naba uwacishinka kuli iwe. 

4  Nshikala pamo na bantu incenjeshi, kabili nshicita mapange pamo na babumbi munda. 

5  Nshumfwana na babifi, nelyo kwikala pamo na bancitatubi. 

6  Ndesamba iminwe yandi ne wakaele. Ndeshinguluka ubulambo bobe we Yawe, 

7  no kulapunda pakwimba ulwimbo lwakutotela, pakulondolola ificitwa fyobe ifyakupapa. 

8  Nalitemwa i≈ãanda yobe iyakwikalamo we Yawe, incende umwaba ubucindami bobe. 

9  Wilofya umweo wandi pamo na babifi, wilofya ubumi bwandi pamo na bantu abatemwa ukusumya umulopa, 

10  abo mu minwe yabo mwaba imilimo iyibi, abo no kwa kulyo kwabo kwaisulamo amafisakanwa. 

11  Lelo ine njikala umweo wakaele, undubuleko no kumbela inkumbu. 

12  Amakasa yandi yeminina umwatambalala, nakulakupaala we Yawe na matotelo mu lukuta lukalamba.

1  Ine nalisekelele ilyo banjebele bati, “Natuleya mu ŋanda yakwa Yawe!” 

2  Amakasa yesu nayanyanta pa mpongolo shobe we Yerusalemu. 

3  Yerusalemu wakuulwa ngo musumba, uwaba uwatitikana pamofye. 

4  Kanshi eko inko shiya, inko shakwa Yawe, kukwisa lumbanya ishina lyakwa Yawe, nga mulya mwine apeelele icipope ku bena Israeli. 

5  Mulya mwine emwaba ifipuna fyakuputwilamo imilandu, ifipuna fya ba mu ŋanda yakwa Davidi. 

6  Pepeleniko Yerusalemu pakuti mube icibote, mutile, “Leka abatemwa iwe babe abacingililwa! 

7  Namuleba icibote mukati ka malinga yobe, mube ubucingo mu fikuulwa fyobe ifikankala.” 

8  Pa mulandufye wa bamunyinane na bacibusa bandi ine ndeti, “Icibote cibe mukati kobe!” 

9  Pa mulandu wa ŋanda yakwa Yawe Lesa wesu, ine ndekufwailako ishuko lyobe.

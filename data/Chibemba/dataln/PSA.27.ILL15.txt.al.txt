1  Yawe e lubuuto lwandi, kabili Ipusukilo lyandi, bushe nani ningatiina? Yawe ekwakushina umweo wandi, nani ningakwatilako umwenso? 

2  Ilintu ababifi banjimina pakuti bonaule umubili wandi, ilyo abalwani ne fipondo bonse banjiminina, bakatalansuka no kuwa. 

3  Nelyo ifita finganjimina ne fyanso, umutima wandi tekuti utiine. Nangu inkondo ingema, nakulabafye uwatetekela. 

4  Icintu cimofye ico nalomba kuli Yawe, kabili ico nakulatwalilila ukulomba cakutila, ndeikalilila mu ŋanda yakwa Yawe, inshiku shonse isha mweo wandi. Ndefwaya ukulalolesha ubusuma bwakwe, no kupandwa mano ya mwi Tempele. 

5  Pantu mu nshita yakucula eukansunga mu ŋanda yakwe, akamfisa mu cintelelwe ce Tenti lyakwe, no kunsansabalika pa libwe ilyacindama. 

6  Nomba umutwe wandi wasumbuka, ukucila pa balwani bandi abanshinguluka. Nakulaimba ne nsansa pakutuula ilambo mwi Tenti lyakwe, nakulaimba inyimbo ishakulumbanya Yawe. 

7  Umfwa inkuuta ye shiwi lyandi we Yawe, unangulukileko no kunjasuka. 

8  Umutima wandi watila, “Fwaya icinso cakwe.” Icinso cobe we Yawe eco ndefwaya. 

9  Wifisa icinso cobe kuli ine, winkaana ne mubomfi obe ilyo ucili no bukali, we wangafwa pe. Wintamfya nangu ukunsuula, we Lesa, Umupusushi wandi. 

10  Nangu tata na mayo bengandekelesha, we Yawe tekuti undekeleshe. 

11  Umfunde inshila shobe we Yawe, untungulule mu nshila ishalungama pa mulandu wa balwani bandi. 

12  Wilamposa ku balwani bandi kukunjimina, ico baba no bufi pamo no kumpangila. 

13  Icintu cimo nasumina cakuti nkamona ubusuma bwakwa Yawe mu calo ca bamweo. 

14  Lolela pali Yawe, shipa kabili tekanya. Ee ulelolelafye pali Yawe.

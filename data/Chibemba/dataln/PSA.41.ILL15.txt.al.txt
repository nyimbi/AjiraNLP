1  Alishuka umuntu uusakamana abalanda na babusu, ico nao bakamwafwa kuli Yawe mu nshita ya mafya. 

2  Yawe akamucingilila no kumusunga, akamupaala mu calo, kabili takamupose ku balwani bakwe. 

3  Yawe alamukoselesha ninshi ali pa musengele nalwala, no kuposha alamuposha ku malwele yakwe. 

4  Lelo ine natile, “We Yawe umbeleleko uluse, umposhe ico ninkubifya.” 

5  Abalwani bandi balanyamba abati, “Bushe uyu muntu akafwa lilali pakuti tumulabeko?” 

6  Abesa mukumpempula baba ne mitima ibili, umo wakufwailisha apafikile ubulwele, umbi wakuya baleananyafye ubulwele bwandi. 

7  Abampata bekalilafye ukutepeleshanya, no kukabila ukuti ifibi fincitikile. 

8  Bena batontonkanya ukuti balimulowa, takapole nakalya palya pa musengele alele, takemepo iyoo. 

9  Na cibusa wandi uo nacetekele, umuntu uo tulyafye nankwe icipe cimo, nao alinsangukila. 

10  Lelo iwe umbelele uluse we Yawe no kunjimya pakuti ncimfye abalwani. 

11  Na kuli ici eko ndi nokwishibila ifyo wantemwa, pantu umulwani tancimfishe. 

12  Ulangafwa pantu ine ndi wakaele, ulansunga pa cinso cobe pe na pe. 

13  Napaalwe na matotelo Yawe, Lesa wakwa Israeli, ukufuma ku muyayaya no kufika ku muyayaya, Ameni kabili ameni.

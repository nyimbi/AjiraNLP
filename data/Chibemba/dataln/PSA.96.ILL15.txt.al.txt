1   Imilandu 

16  :

23  -

33  ) 

1  Imbileni Yawe ulwimbo ulupya, mweba pano isonde bonse mwimbile Yawe. 

2  Muleimbila Yawe, kabili mulelumbanya ishina lyakwe, mulebila ukupususha kwakwe akasuba na kasuba. 

3  Mulesabankanya ubucindami bwakwe mu mishobo yonse, muleshimikilako abantu bonse ificitwa fyakwe ifyamaka. 

4  Pantu Yawe alipulamisha kabili ewalinga ukulumbanya, ewakukwatila akatiina ukucila imilungu yonse. 

5  Pantu imilungu yonse iya mishobo tulubifye, lelo Yawe ewabumba imyulu. 

6  Ubucindami no bupulami fyaba pa cinso cakwe, ubulamba no lulumbi fyaba mu cifulo cakwe icamushilo. 

7  Mulelondolola ubulumbwa bwa bulamba bwakwa Yawe mwe ndupwa sha mishobo, mulelondolola ubulumbwa bwa bucindami bwakwa Yawe na maka yakwe. 

8  Mulelondolola ubulumbwa ubwalinga ishina lyakwa Yawe, muleleta no mutuulo pakwingila mwisano lyakwe. 

9  Mulepepa Yawe mu bucindami bwa mushilo wakwe, muletutuma pa cinso cakwe bonse mwe ba pano isonde. 

10  Landeni pakati ka mishobo ati, “Yawe euteeka.” Ewashimpa bwino isonde lyonse, talingaseshiwa, elyo kabili akapingula abantu bonse mu bulungami. 

11  Lekeni imyulu isansamuke, ne sonde lisekelele, bemba nao alepoma, pamo pene ne fintu fyonse ifyabamo. 

12  Lekeni mu mpanga mube ukwanga, pamo ne fintu fyonse ifyabamo, pakuti ifimuti fyonse ifya mu mutengo fileimba ne nsansa. 

13  Nabaleimba ku ntanshi yakwa Yawe, pantu naisa, eshile mukupingula aba pano isonde. Ali nokupingula aba pano isonde mu bulungami, no kucita icishinka ku bamishobo bonse.

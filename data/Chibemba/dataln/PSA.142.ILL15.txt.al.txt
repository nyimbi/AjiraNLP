1  Ine ndekuuta ne shiwi ilikalamba kuli Yawe, ndesansula ishiwi lyandi mukupapata kuli Yawe. 

2  Na pa cinso cakwe epo ndeilishanishisha, eo ndelondolwela amacushi yandi yonse. 

3  Ilyo umupashi wandi waba no mwenso, niwe wishiba inshila ndi nokwendamo. Mu nshila umo mpita, abalwani bandi baliteyamo icakunjikatilako. 

4  Lolesha lwa ku kulyo kwandi umone, takuli na wakunsakamanako, nshikwete umwakufisama, nelyofye umo uwakusakamana ubumi bwandi nakalya. 

5  Ndekuuta kuli we Yawe nati, “Niwe cubo candi, icakaniko candi mu calo ca bomi.” 

6  Umfwa ifyo ine ndekuuta, pantu nimpelelwa apakalamba. Mpokololako ku balwani abalenkunkunika pantu ine nabancila amaka. 

7  Undubuleko ukufuma mu cifungo, uleke na ine ndelumbanya ishina lyobe. Elyo abalungami bakanshinguluka, pa mulandu wa busuma bobe kuli ine.

9  Ilyo ifi fyapita nati ndoleshe, namona ibumba lya bantu ilikalamba ilyo umuntu ashingapenda. Aba bantu bafumine ku mitundu yonse, ku mikowa yonse, ku mishobo yonse, na ku ba ndimi shonse, beminine pantanshi ya cipuna ca Mfumu, pa cinso ca Mwana wa Mpanga. Bafwele imingila iyabuuta nabekata no tuncindu mu minwe. 

10  Elyo baikatisha amashiwi abati, “Ipusukilo lyafuma kuli Lesa wesu uwikele pa cipuna ca Mfumu na ku Mwana wa Mpanga.” 

11  Bamalaika bonse ninshi beminine ukushinguluka icipuna ca Mfumu, abakalamba, ne fyamweo fine. Elyo bamalaika abo bafukama, bainamina panshi, balapepa Lesa, 

12  abati, “Ameni! Amalumbo, ubucindami, amano, ukutasha, umucinshi, ubulamba na maka fileba kuli Lesa wesu pe na pe! Ameni!” 

13  Elyo umo pa bakalamba anjipusha ati, “Bushe naubeshiba aba bafwele imingila yabuuta, kabili bushe ni kwi bafumine?” 

14  Na ine nayasuka nati, “Mwe beshibe nimwe mukwai.” Anjeba ati, “Aba ebapita mu macushi ayabipa nganshi. Ebasamba imingila yabo mu mulopa wa Mwana wa Mpanga no kuilenga ukubuutisha. 

15  Kanshi eco beminina pantanshi ya cipuna cakwa Lesa balemutumika ubwaca no bwaila mwitempele lyakwe. Uyu uwikele pa cipuna ca Mfumu apo ali nabo, akulabacingilila. 

16  Tabakatale abomfwa nsala nangu icilaka nakabili. Kabili tabakatale abacula ku kasuba na ku cipuki. 

17  Pantu Umwana wa Mpanga uuli pakati ka cipuna ca Mfumu, eukaba kacema wabo. Akalabatwala na ku tumfukumfuku twa menshi yakupeela umweo. Kabili Lesa akafuuta ifilamba fyonse ku menso yabo.”

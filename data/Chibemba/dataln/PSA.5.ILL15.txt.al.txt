1  Kutika kwipepo lyandi we Yawe, umfwa ukupemashika kwandi. 

2  Umfwa uko ndelila we Mfumu yandi kabili we Lesa wandi, pantu ni kuli iwe ndepepa. 

3  Cila lucelo ulomfwa ishiwi lyandi we Yawe, ilyakulomba elyo no kwikala pakulolela ubwasuko. 

4  Tawaba Lesa uwikala aletamba ifyabipa, ababifi tekuti basangwe mu ceni cobe. 

5  Abamataki tekuti beminine ku cinso cobe, pantu walipata bonse abacita ifyabipa. 

6  Ulonaula bonse ababepa ubufi. Yawe alipata abasumya umulopa ne ncenjeshi. 

7  Lelo pa mulandu wa luse lobe ulukalamba, ine nkengila mu Å‹anda yobe. Nkakontamika icinso candi mwi Tempele lyobe ilyamushilo, pa mulandu wa kukukwatila akatiina. 

8  Ntungulula we Yawe mu kulungama kobe, pantu ine naba na balwani. Ubike inshila yobe iyatambalala pa ntanshi yandi. 

9  Pantu akanwa kabo takalanda fyacishinka, na mu mitima yabo mwaisulamo ubonaushi. Imikolomino yabo ni nindi ishacenama, indimi shabo shilandafye ifyakusembeleka. 

10  Ubapeele umulandu we Lesa, uleke amapange yabo yabawishe. Ubatamfye pantu balikwatisha ulufyengo, kabili bantu abakusangukila. 

11  Lelo leka bonse abafisama muli iwe babe no buseko, kabili leka balekwimbila pe na pe. Fusha ubucingo bobe pali bena, pakuti abatemwa ishina lyobe basekelele. 

12  Pantu we Yawe ulapaala abololoke, no kubacingilila ku luse lobe nge nkwela.

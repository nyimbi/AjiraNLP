1  Umpusushe kwishina lyobe, we Lesa, umpokololeko mu maka yobe. 

2  Umfwa kwipepo lyandi, we Lesa, kutika kufyo ine ndelanda. 

3  Abantu bambi bambifye nabanjimina, abantu abankalwe balefwaya ukunjipaya, abantu abashasakamana Lesa. (Selah) 

4  Ala cine cine Lesa e kafwa wandi, Imfumu eisunga umweo wandi. 

5  Uleke ububi bubwelele abalwani bandi, mu cishinka cobe ubonaule. 

6  Ine nakulakutuula ilambo ilyakuitemenwa, nakulatotela ishina lyobe we Yawe pantu lyaliwama. 

7  Pantu naumpususha ku macushi yandi yonse, kabili nimona na menso yandi uko abalwani bandi balecimba.

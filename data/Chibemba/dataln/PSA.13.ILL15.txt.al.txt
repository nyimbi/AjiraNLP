1  Bushe kufika ku nshita nshi we Yawe uli nokundaba, kabili kufika lilali uli nokufisa icinso cobe kuli ine? 

2  Bushe kufika lilali umutima wandi wakulacula ku matontonkanyo, kabili no kulaikala uwabulanda akasubafye konse? Bushe kufika lilali umulwani wandi akulancimfya? 

3  We Yawe, Lesa wandi, unangulukileko no kunjasuka, mpuputula lintu nshilaponena mu tulo twa mfwa, 

4  Epali na balwani bandi bayamba ukusosa abati “Natumucimfya.” Bakasekela pakumona ukuwa kwandi. 

5  Lelo ine nacetekela mu kutemwa kobe ukushipwa, no mutima wandi ulecankilwa mwipusukilo lyobe. 

6  Nkalaimba kuli Yawe, pa mulandu wa fisuma ifyo ancitila.

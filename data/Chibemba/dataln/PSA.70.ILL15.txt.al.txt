1   We Lesa, wise bwangu umpokololeko, wendeshe ukwisa ngafwa we Yawe! 

2  Uleke bonse abafwaya ukunjipaya basebane na mano yabo yabapele. Na bonse abafwaisha ukuti ngonaike, ubabweseshemo abasebana. 

3  Uleke abatila, “Eya, eya!” babwelelemo ne nsoni nashibekata. 

4  Uleke bonse abakufwaya basekelele no kusansamuka, na bonse abatemwa ipusukilo lyobe balesosa pe ati, “Lesa asumbulwe!” 

5  Lelo ine ndi mulanda kabili umupina, shi wingesa bwangu we Lesa! Niwe kafwa wandi, kabili kapokolola wandi, we Yawe ala wikokola.

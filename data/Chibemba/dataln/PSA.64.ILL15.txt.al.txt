1  Wingomfwa ishiwi lyandi ilyakulilishika, we Lesa, upokolole no mweo wandi ku balwani abalentiinya, 

2  Umfise ukufuma kuli bantalamisoka, aba mapange ya mu bumfisolo, kwibumba lya congo ilya bacita ifyabipa. 

3  Abanona indimi shabo ngo lupanga, na mashiwi yabo ayakali bayafwinta nge mifwi. 

4  Balabelama pakulasa uwakaele, bapumikisha pakumulasa, ababula no mwenso. 

5  Balakoseleshanya ilyo bapanga ukucita icabipa, balomfwana ukwakuya teya ifiteyo fyabo, balanda nokuti, “Takuli uuletumona.” 

6  Ilyo balepangana ukucita ifyabipa batila, “Eya mwandi twatontonkanya ifisuma.” Cine cine munda na mu mutima wa muntu mwaba ubucenjeshi. 

7  Lelo Lesa akalasa umufwi pali bena, no kubalasa ukwabula ukubasoka. 

8  Akabalofya pa mulandu wafyo balanda, na bonse abakamonako bakakunta imitwe. 

9  Elyo na bantu bonse bakatiina, bakashimika ifyo Lesa acita, no kulatontonkanya palwa micitile yakwe. 

10  Lekeni umuntu onse umololoke asekelele muli Yawe, no kubutukila uko ali! Lekeni na bonse abalungama imitima bamulumbanye.

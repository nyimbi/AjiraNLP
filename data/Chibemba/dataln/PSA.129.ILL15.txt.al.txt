1  “Ukufumafye ku bulumendo bwandi aba bantu tepakuncusha nganshi.” Aleni, asuka iwe we Israeli utile, 

2  “Ukufumafye ku bulumendo bwandi aba bantu tepakuncusha, lelo ukuncimfya kwena balifilwa! 

3  Bashibulimi balisantaula inuma yandi, kwati mikolwa iyitali mwibala ililelimwa.” 

4  Lelo Yawe mulungami icine cine, ena alindubulako ku myando ya bubifi bwabo. 

5  Uleke bonse abapata Sioni balengwe insoni, babwelelemo kunuma. 

6  Uleke babe nge cani pa mutenge, iciyuminafye ilyo cishilakula, 

7  icani ico kaseepa teti acibuule mu minwe yakwe, nangu ukukakamo umwanshi nakalya. 

8  Uleke abalepitapo besosa nokuti, “Ipaalo lyakwa Yawe libe nemwe, ifwe twamupaala mwishina lyakwa Yawe.”

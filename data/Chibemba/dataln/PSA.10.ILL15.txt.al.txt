1  We Yawe, bushe mulandu nshi wiminina akatalamukila? Bushe cinshi ufisamina pa nshita ya macushi yesu? 

2  Mu mataki emo umubifi acushisha abalanda, balekatwa mu fiteyo ifyo abateya. 

3  Umubifi alaitakisha pa fya lunkumbwa lwakwe Uwakaso alatuka no kupontela Yawe. 

4  Mu cilumba cakwe, umubifi tafwaya Yawe, mu kutontonkanya kwakwe konse atila, “Takwaba Lesa.” 

5  Enda uwashuka lyonse mu micitile yakwe, tasakamana no bupingushi bobe ubwacindama na bonse abamulwisha alibasuula. 

6  Mu mutima atila, “Takwaba icankutiinya, na mu nshita yonse iyabumi nshakakwatepo ubwafya.” 

7  Mu kanwa kakwe mufumafye intipu, ubufi pamo ne fyakutiinya, ubwafya no bubifi efifuma panshi ya lulimi lwakwe. 

8  Alabelama mu mbali ya nshila sha mu mishi, kukulalilila mukwipaya abantu abakaele, alolela ukwikata abashama. 

9  Abelama pamo nga filya inkalamo ifisama, afisama pakuti ekate umulanda. Nga aikata mulanda, amukulila na mwisumbu lyakwe. 

10  Alateyamina no kubotelela panshi pakufwailisha, no mulanda awila mu minwe yakwe. 

11  Mu mano yakwe, umubifi atila, “Yawe alilaba, nalimo alifimba pa menso yakwe, kabili tekuti amone.” 

12  Ala ima we Yawe, imya ukuboko we Lesa wandi, wilaba abalanda. 

13  Cinshi umubifi akwatila imisuula kuli Lesa, no kulatontonkanya mu mutima ati, “Takandubulwishe?” 

14  Lelo we Yawe, ulamona amafya pamo no kulilishika, ulatontonkanyapo pakufibika mu minwe yobe. Abalanda balilila kuli iwe, niwe kafwa wa bana banshiwa. 

15  Funa ukuboko kwa mubifi no kwa ushumfwa. Kalipa pa bubi bwakwe ubwafisama mpaka bukapwe. 

16  Yawe ni mfumu pe na pe, imishobo ikaloba ukufuma mu calo cakwe. 

17  We Yawe ulomfwa ukupapata kwa bantu abacucutika, ulabakoselesha no kukutika ku kulila kwabo. 

18  Ulacita umulinganya kuli bakashiwa na ku balecululuka pakuti aba pano isonde baleke ukuleta icintiinya.

1  We Yawe, ifyo bafula abalwani bandi, nabafula mu mpendwa abalenjimina. 

2  Nabafula abampingila ukuti, “Lesa takamupokololeko nakalya.” (Selah) 

3  Lelo iwe uli nkwela iyakuncingilila, we Yawe, ulancindamika no kunenga ukwinuna umutwe, 

4  Kuli Yawe eko ndilila mutuule, nao alanjasuka ukufuma pa lupili ulwamushilo. (Selah) 

5  Ndalambalala no kulala mu tulo, kabili ndabuuka pantu Yawe alansunga. 

6  Tekuti ntiine abalwani imyandamishila, abanjimina mu mbali shonse. 

7  Ima we Yawe! Mpokolola we Lesa wandi! Uma pa nsekete sha balwani bandi, no kukula ameno ya babifi. 

8  Ukulubulwa kufuma kuli Yawe. Uleke amapaalo yobe yabe pa bantu bobe. (Selah)

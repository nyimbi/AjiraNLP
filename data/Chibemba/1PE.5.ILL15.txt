Ukusunga abantu bakwa Lesa
1Ine apo ndi mukalamba munenu, ndemupapata imwe mwe bakalamba ba nkuta. Ine ndi kambone pafyo Kristu aculile, kabili nalikumako ku bucindami ubukesa sokoloka. 2Kanshi mulecema umukuni uo Lesa amupeela ukwangalila, teico pantu muli nokubomba lelo nico mulefwaya ukubomba ifyo Lesa alefwaya mubombe. Mwilabomba icofye mulefwaya amalipilo iyoo, lelo mulebomba ico mulefwaisha ukutumika. 3Mwilaumya bufumu ku bantu abo mwapeelwa ukubaka, lelo muleba icilangililo icakuti umukuni wakwa Lesa ulepashanya. 4Ilyo Kacema mukalamba akamoneka, elyo mukapeelwa icilambu icacindama icishakonaike.
5 Eifyo fine na imwe mwe balumendo mulenakila abakalamba. Elyo bonsefye muletumika abanenu mukuicefya. Pantu catila, “Lesa acincintila abacilumba, lelo abaicefya bena alabakwatila icikuuku.” 6Kanshi muleicefya pa cinso cakwa Lesa uwamaka nganshi, umwine akamucindamika pa nshita alefwaya. 7Mulemutuula amasakamiko yenu yonse, pantu eumusunga. 8Muletekanya, kabili mulelola! Umulwani wenu Ciwa, endauka kwati ni nkalamo ilebuluma, ilefwaya uwakuti ikate imulye. 9Mube ne cisumino icakosa, mulemwansha. Kabili mwalishiba ukuti ababwananyina banenu isonde lyonse balepita mu macushi ya musango umo wine. 10Ala mukaculafye akashita akanono. Elyo Lesa wacikuuku conse, uwamwita ico tuli muli Kristu kukukuma ku bucindami bwakwe ubwakubelelela, akamuwamya umupwilapo, akamupeela amaka, akamukosha mukabe abashangila icine cine. 11Lesa e mwine maka pe na pe. Ameni.
Ukusondwelela kalata
12Uyu kalata namulembela, mwipi. Ni Sailasi uo ine mona ukuti ni munyinefwe uwacishinka ewangafwako ukulemba. Ndefwaya ukumukoselesha no kwiminina bukambone ukuti uyu kalata e cikuuku cacine cine icakwa Lesa. Kanshi mushangile mucikatishe. 13Ulukuta lunenu ulwa mu Babiloni ulo Lesa asala nga imwe bene, lwamuposha, elyo no mwana wandi Mako amuposha. 14Muleposhanya mukutomona kwa batemwana.
Umutende ube na imwe bonse imwe mwe baba muli Kristu.

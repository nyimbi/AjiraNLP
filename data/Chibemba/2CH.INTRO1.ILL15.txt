Amashiwi yakulondolola pali ici citabo
Mu citabo cabubili ica Milandu emwaba amashiwi ayakonkanyapo pa milandu yali mu citabo ca Milandu icakutendeka. Mwena imilandu yayambile ilyo Solomoni apyene pa bufumu mpaka napo aishile fwila. Mwalembwa ne milandu yafyo Yeroboamu alengele inko sha ku kapinda ka ku kuso shapondokele ubufumu bwakwa Reoboamu umwana wakwa Solomoni uwamupyene pa bufumu. Imilandu iyingi iyabamo intu yacitikile ku calo ca bena Yuda icabelele ku kapinda ka ku kulyo, iyaishile shinta pa nshita umusumba wa Yerusalemu waonawilwe mu mwaka wa 586 ilyo Yesu talafyalwa.
Imilandu yatantama ifi
Inshita Solomoni ateekele pa bufumu 1:1—9:31
Imyaka yakutendekelapo 1:1-17
Bakuula Itempele 2:1—7:10
Imyaka yakulekelesha 7:11—9:31
Ukupondoka kwa nko sha ku kapinda ka ku kuso 10:1-19
Imfumu shateekele mu Yuda 11:1—36:12
Ukonaulwa kwa musumba wa Yerusalemu 36:13-23

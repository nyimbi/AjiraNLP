1Yawe euteeka, lekeni aba pano isonde basansamuke,
lekeni ne fyalo ifingi ifyakutali ifya ku lulamba lwa bemba fisekelele.
2Amakumbi ne mfifi iyikalamba fyalimushinguluka,
umulinganya ne cishinka e mufula wa bufumu bwakwe.
3Umulilo eutangilila uko aleya,
uya uleoca abalwani bakwe aba mu mbali shonse.
4Ukubyata kwa mfula ekusanika isonde lyonse,
na ba pano isonde nga bamona balatutuma.
5Impili shisunguluka nge pula pa menso yakwa Yawe,
pa cinso ca Mfumu ye sonde lyonse.
6Imyulu ilalangilila ubulungami bwakwe,
na bantu ba mishobo yonse bamona no lulumbi lwakwe.
7Bonse abapepa ifimpashanya fya milungu balalengwa insoni,
pamo na bonse abaitakisha mu tulubi.
Mwe milungu yonse mulemupepa.
8Sioni naumfwako kabili aleanga,
ne mishi ya mu Yuda naisekelela,
pa mulandu wa bupingushi bobe we Yawe.
9Pantu we Yawe, niwe Lesa mukulu uwacila fyonse ifya pano isonde,
walicila apakalamba pa milungu yonse.
10Lekeni bonse abatemwa Yawe bapate ifyabipa,
pantu alasunga imyeo ya bantu bakwe abacishinka,
no kubapokolola ku maboko ya babifi.
11Ulubuuto lulasanikila pa balungama,
na batambalala imitima bakwata insansa.
12Imwe mwe bololoke mulesansamukila muli Yawe,
kabili mulelumbanya ishina lyakwe ilyamushilo.

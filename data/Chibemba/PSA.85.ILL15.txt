Kuli kembisha mukalamba. Ulwimbo lwa bana bakwa Kora.
1Walipaala icalo cobe we Yawe,
wabwesesha Yakobo ishuko lyakwe.
2Abantu bobe naubabelelako uluse kufyo babifya,
naufimba na pa bubifi bwabo bonse.
(Selah)
3Nauleka no kulabakalipila,
ne cipyu cobe icikali nacipwa.
4Utubwekeshe kufyo twali, we Lesa Umupusushi wesu,
unasheko ne cipyu cobe ico watukwatile.
5Bushe wakulatufulilwafye pe?
Bushe wakulalundapofye ukufulwa umwakulakonkanina inkulo?
6Bushe ekuti tawakabwekeshepo ukutupuputula,
pakuti fwe bantu bobe tulesansamukila muli iwe?
7Tulange icitemwiko cobe icishipwa we Yawe,
utupususheko no kutupususha.
8Ine ndekutika kufyo Lesa Yawe alelanda,
palwa kutuletela icibote ifwe fwe bantu bakwe,
nomba cikulu fwe bamushilo bakwe tatubwelele ku fyabuwelewele.
9Icine cine ipusukilo lyakwe lyalipalamina abamutiina,
elyo no bucindami bwakwe bwakulamoneka mu calo.
10Icitemwiko icishipwa ne cishinka nafisuntinkana,
bukaele ne cibote nafiitomona.
11Icishinka nacimena pano isonde,
no bulungami bwa mumulu bulaloleshapo.
12Ifintu ifisuma efyo Yawe aletupeela,
ne calo cesu cakulamesha ifisabo fya busomboshi.
13Ubulungami ebwakulatangilila Yawe pantanshi,
no kupekanya umwakunyanta inkasa shakwe.

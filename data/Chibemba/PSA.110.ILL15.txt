Ulwimbo lwakwa Davidi.
1 Yawe aebele imfumu yandi ati,
“Ikala ku kuboko kwandi ukwa ku kulyo,
mpaka abalwani bobe nkabacite apakunyanta amakasa yobe.”
2Mu Sioni emo Yawe akalundulwila amaka yobe ayabufumu,
epakuti ukaleteeka pakati ka balwani bobe.
3Abashilika bobe bakalaipeelesha mukukubeela pa bushiku bwa nkondo.
Ubupulami bwa bufumu wabupeelwefye ku macaca,
pa bushiku bwine ubo wafyelwemo.
Abalumendo bobe bakesa kuli iwe ngo mume.
4Yawe alilapa umulapo uo takalapuluke atila,
“Iwe uli shimapepo wa pe na pe,
muli bushimapepo bwakwa Melikisedeki.”
5Imfumu ili lwa ku kulyo kobe,
ubushiku bwa kukalipa kwaiko ikashonaula bashamfumu bonse.
6Ikapingula imishobo ya benafyalo,
ikasha ne fitumbi fyabo ntangali ntangali,
ikashonaula ne shamfumu sha pano isonde ponse.
7Imfumu ikanwa amenshi mu kamana akabela mu nshila,
eico ikabwesha amaka no kusansula icinso ne nsansa.

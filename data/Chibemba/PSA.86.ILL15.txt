Ipepo lyakwa Davidi.
1Unkutike we Lesa kabili unjasuke,
pantu ine ndi mulanda kabili ndi mubusu.
2Ubake umweo wandi pantu naliipeelesha kuli iwe,
umpusushe ne mubomfi obe, we Lesa wandi,
pantu natetekela muli iwe.
3Umbeleleko uluse we Mfumu,
pantu nkuuta kuli iwe akasuba konse.
4Nsansamushako ine ne mubomfi obe we Mfumu,
pantu ni kuli iwe eko ndosha umweo wandi.
5Uli wamutembo, kabili ulabelela uluse we Mfumu,
walikwatisha icitemwiko icishipwa kuli bonse abakuuta kuli iwe.
6Kutika kwipepo lyandi we Yawe,
umfwa inkumbu mu kupapata kwandi.
7Pa bushiku nkacula nkalilila kuli iwe,
pantu iwe ukanjasuka.
8We Yawe ala takwaba Lesa umbi uwaba nga iwe,
awe takwaba nangu umo uwingacita ifyo ucita.
9Imishobo yonse iyo wabumba yakulaisa mukupepa pa cinso cobe we Mfumu,
bakaleta ululumbi kwishina lyobe.
10Pantu uli wabulamba, Lesa uucita ifipapwa,
kabili wekafye niwe Lesa.
11We Yawe umfunde inshila yobe
elyo nkalaenda mu cine cobe,
uloshe umutima wandi kuli iwe,
pakuti mbe na katiina kwishina lyobe.
12Ine nakulakulumbanya no mutima wandi onse we Mfumu Lesa wandi,
no kulacindika ishina lyobe pe na pe.
13Pantu ukutemwa kobe kuli ine kukalamba nganshi,
ine walimpususha mu calo ca bafwa umwashika nganshi.
14Nashinjimina inkakashi sha bantu we Lesa,
ibumba lya bantu ababula uluse lilefwaya ubumi bwandi,
bantu abashakucindika.
15Lelo iwe we Mfumu uli Lesa waluse ne cikuku,
uushifulwa bwangu, kabili uwafulamo icitemwiko icishipwa ne cishinka.
16Walukile kuli ine no kumbelela uluse,
unkoshe ne mubomfi obe ku maka yobe,
mpusushako ne mwana wa mubomfi obe umwanakashi.
17Nangilila icakumwenako ca busuma bobe we Yawe,
uleke abalwani bandi bamoneko, ne nsoni shibekate,
pantu niwefye ungafwa no kuntalalika.

Ipepo lyakwa Davidi
1Shi wingomfwa ku kupapata kwandi ukwalungama we Yawe,
ukutike kwipepo lyandi ilyabula ubucenjeshi!
2Niwe ushininkisha umulandu wandi no kuncitila umulinganya,
pantu amenso yobe yamona ifyacishinka.
3Nelyo wingalengula umutima wandi
no kumpempula mu nshita ya bushiku,
nangufye winganjesha lelo tawasangemo nangu cimo.
Nalilaya ukuti akanwa kandi kelalanda ifibi.
4Umwabela ificita abantu bambi,
ndaitalusha ku misango ya bantu abalukakala,
ukulingana na amashiwi yobe.
5Ine nalikonka mu nshila yobe,
na makasa yandi tayashelela.
6Ndakuuta kuli we Lesa wandi,
pantu ula≈ãumfwa no kwasuka,
ukutike no kumfwa kwipepo lyandi.
7Langa ukutemwa kobe ukwapesha amano,
we upususha ku maka ya kuboko kobe ukwa ku kulyo,
wapususha abo abafisama muli iwe ukufuma ku balwani babo.
8Unsunge nge mboni ya linso lyobe,
umfise mu cinshingwa ca mapindo yobe.
9Unsunge ukufuma ku babifi abanjimina,
pamo na balwani bandi abanshinguluka.
10Aba bantu imitima yabo ilafilwa ukukwata uluse
ne milomo yabo ilanda mataki mataki.
11Nabanjimina no kunshinguluka,
ninshi nabatulumuna na menso,
kukufwaya ukumfutula panshi.
12Bali nge nkalamo iyileiteyanya pakwikata icintu,
kwati musepela wa nkalamo uubelamine cimo.
13Ala bemine we Yawe,
ubalwishe no kubafulumuna.
Ubomfye ulupanga lobe kukumpokolola ku babifi.
14We Yawe ku maka ya kuboko kobe mpususha kuli aba bantu,
abantu aba mu calo abasakamanafye ifya pano isonde.
Uleke bekute ku fintu ifyo wababikila,
uleke na bana babo nabo bafikwateko ifingi ukucilapo,
elyo nabo bakashileko na bana babo.
15Lelo ine mu bololoke nkamona icinso cobe,
ne lintu nkashibuka nkasekelela pakumona icata cobe.

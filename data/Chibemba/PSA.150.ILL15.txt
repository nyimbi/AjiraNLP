1Lumbanyeni Yawe!
Lumbanyeni Yawe mu cifulo cakwe icamushilo,
lumbanyeni Lesa mu mulu mwisano lyakwe.
2Mumulumbanye pa mulandu wa fintu acita ifyabulamba,
mumulumbanye pantu alipulamisha.
3Mumulumbanye no kulisha amapenga,
mumulumbanye no kulisha amaseese ne nsango!
4Mumulumbanye no kulisha insombo no kucinda,
mumulumbanye no kulisha ifilimba na bamutolilo!
5Mumulumbanye no kulisha imiwaya,
mumulumbanye ne miwaya iyalilisha!
6Ee, cine cine ifibumbwa fyonse ifipema nafilumbanye Yawe!
Akulumbanyeni Yawe!

Ulwimbo lwakwa Davidi. Ulwakupaala Itempele.
1Nakutasha we Yawe pantu naumpususha,
taulekele abalwani bandi ukuitutubula pali ine.
2Ni kuli iwe nalepapata pakuti ungafwe we Yawe, we Lesa wandi,
mpaka waisa wamposha.
3We Yawe wabwesha umweo wandi ukufuma ku calo ca bafwa,
wapususha umweo wandi ukufuma mu bakuya mu nindi.
4Akwimbileni Yawe inyimbo shakutasha, mwe bamushilo bakwe,
no kulatotela ishina lyakwe ilyamushilo.
5Pantu ubukali bwakwe tabuya patali,
lelo uluse lwakwe lwamuyayaya.
Mu nshita ya bushiku kuti walila ifilamba
lelo insansa shisa ulucelo.
6Lelo ine, ilintu naikele umutende natile,
“Nshakatelentenshiwe iyoo.”
7Nomba we Yawe ilintu wansungile bwino,
walengele fyonse ukungwamina,
lelo ilintu wamfutatile,
nalilengelwe ukusakamana.
8Na ine efyo nakubilikishe we Yawe,
no kulomba uluse lobe.
9Bushe busuma nshi bwingaba mu kufwa kwandi,
nga naingila mu nindi?
Bushe kuti iloba lyakulumbanya,
nangu ukulumbula ifya bucishinka bobe?
10Umfwa no kumbelelako uluse we Yawe,
we Yawe ube kafwa wandi.
11Ukuloosha kwandi naukwalula insansa,
naumfula insalu shabulanda,
wamfwika insalu shakucindilamo,
12pakuti umutima wandi utwalilile ukukulumbanya ukwabula ukwikala tondolo,
we Yawe, we Lesa wandi, niwefye nakulatotela pe na pe.

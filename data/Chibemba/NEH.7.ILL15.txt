1Nomba ilyo ilinga lyapwile ukukuulwa, elyo nabikamo ne fiibi bwino bwino mu ncende shafiko, nasonta na bakalinda wa mpongolo, bakemba, elyo abena Levi nabo basontwa ku mulimo wabo. 2Elyo ine nasontele munyinane Hanani ukuba kangalila wa Yerusalemu, pamo na Hananiya uwali kangalila wa bashilika ba kwisano, pantu ali uwacishinka no kuba uwakutiina Lesa ukucila abantu bambi. 3Elyo nalandile kuli bena nati, “Mwilaisula impongolo sha mu Yerusalemu, kano ilyo akasuba katampa ukukaba. Kabili pa nshita bakalinda wa mpongolo bacili nabeminina pakulonda, bafwile ukulaisalako ifiibi no kupilikishako. Elyo musontemo na bekashi bamo aba mu Yerusalemu ukuba bakalinda, bamo pa ncende ishasontwa elyo bambi nabo pa ntanshi ya mayanda yabo.”
Impendwa ya bantu abafumine muli bunkole
4Nomba umusumba wali uwapabuka kabili uukalamba nganshi. Lelo abantu balimo abanono, kabili na mayanda yene yali tayalakuulwa. 5Kanshi Lesa wandi acibika mu mutima wandi ukuti nonganye abasambashi, bacilolo elyo na bekalacalo mukubalembesha ukulingana ne ndupwa shabo. Awe nasanga ne citabo ico balembelemo ababalilepo ukubwela ukulingana ne ndupwa shabo. Ifi efyo nasangile ifyalembelwemo.
6Aba, e bantu abaikele mu citungu ca calo abaishile ukufuma muli bunkole, abantu abo imfumu Nebukadinesa iya mu Babiloni asendele. Babwelele ku Yerusalemu pamo pene na ku Yuda, cila muntu abwelele ku mushi kumwakwe. 7Bonse baishile pamo na Serubabeli na Yeshua, Nehemia, Asariya, Ramiya, Nahamani, Modekai, Bilishani, Misipereti, Bigifai, Nehumu elyo na Baanaya.

63Nomba kuli bashimapepo ni aba: Abana baume bakwa Hobaya, abana baume bakwa Hakosi elyo na bana baume bakwa Basilai uwabuulile umukashi wakwe ukufuma ku lupwa lwakwa Basilai umwina Gileadi no kwinikwako ishina limo line. 64Aba ebo bafwailishe amashina ya fikolwe fyabo mu fitabo lelo tabafimwene, kanshi epakubafumya pa mulongo wa bashimapepo no kubapendela pa bashasangululwa. 65Elyo umulashi wa ici calo nao apeele icebo icakuti takuli kulya icakulya camushilo mpakafye bakakwate shimapepo uukapendula ukubomfya Urimu na Tumimu.
66Kanshi ulukuta lonse ulwabwelele ukufuma kuli bunkole lwali pamo amakana amakumi yane na yabili ne myanda itatu na makumi mutanda (42,360). 67Kabili ababomfi babo abaume na banakashi bambi bali amakana cine lubali ne myanda itatu na makumi yatatu na cine lubali (7,337). Balikwete na bakwimba inyimbo, abaume elyo na banakashi, abali imyanda ibili na makumi yane na yasano (245). 68Imfalashi shabo shali imyanda cine lubali na makumi yatatu na mutanda (736). Elyo bakabalwe babo bali imyanda ibili na makumi yane na basano (245). 69Ingamila nasho shali imyanda ine na makumi yatatu na shisano (435). Bapunda nabo amakana mutanda ne myanda cine lubali na makumi yabili (6,720).
70Elyo abakalamba bamo aba ndupwa batuulile ne fyabupe ifyakwafwilisha muli uyu mulimo. Umulashi wa calo ici, apeele ku mulimo wa cipao, indalama drakimasi ikana limo (1,000) isha golide, inweno nasho amakumi yasano (50) ne fyakufwala bashimapepo nafyo imyanda isano na makumi yatatu (530). 71Bamo abakalamba ba mu ndupwa balepeela indalama drakimasi amakana amakumi yabili (20,000) isha golide, kabili indalama minasi amakana yabili ne myanda ibili (2,200) isha silifa. 72Elyo bambi abantu abashala baletele indalama drakimasi amakana amakumi yabili (20,000) isha golide pamo ne ndalama minasi amakana yabili (2,000) isha silifa. Kabili pali ne fyakufwala ifya bashimapepo nafyo amakumi mutanda na cine lubali (67).
73Kanshi nomba bashimapepo, abena Levi, bakalinda wa mpongolo she Tempele, bakemba, ababomfi be Tempele, bambi bamo bamo elyo na bena Israeli bonse baikele mu misumba yabo ine.

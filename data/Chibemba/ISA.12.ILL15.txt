Ulwimbo lwakutotela ulwa bantu abakalubuka
1Muli ubo ubushiku ukemba no kutila,
“Ine nakutasha we Yawe.
Iwe walitalile wamfulilwa,
pali nomba nauntalalika umutima,
nauleka no kumfulilwa kabili wankoselesha.
2 Icine cine Lesa e pusukilo lyandi,
eo nakulatetekela ukwabula ukutiina nelyo cimo.
Pantu ni Yawe, cine cinefye ni Yawe eunkosha kabili e lumbo lyandi, kabili ewaisa ba ipusukilo lyandi.
3Mukusekelela ukukalamba mukalatapa amenshi mu fishima fye pusukilo.”
4Kabili muli ubo bushiku mukemba amuti,
“Akutoteleni Yawe. Mulekuuta peshina lyakwe.
Muleshimikilako na bantu ba mishobo yonse ifyo acita.
Mulebebako ukuti ishina lyakwe lyalipulamisha.
5Muleimbila Yawe, pantu ifyo acita fyakupapa.
Lekeni abantu bonse aba pano isonde bomfweko uyu mulandu.
6Lekeni bonsefye abaikala mu Sioni bange, no kwimba baleimba,
pantu alipulama Uwamushilo uwakwa Israeli, uwaikala pakati kenu.”

Ekosio loka Adokanar.
1
Naebelokinio bobo EJAKAIT aŋatario naka Sion,
Kipou kasubata bala ŋul luirujaete.
2
Kilelebuni do akiena aitukewok,
Ayoore da anaiepawok:
Kotemasi do atekerin ebe,
“Eswamaki EJAKAIT kes nuepolok.”
3Kiswamak oni EJAKAIT nuepolok;
Ŋes kiyalamar.
4Wu EJAKAIT, kibelok bobo akosiŋatario,
Kwape kalelesia nuka ari naka Enalaklmak.
5
Ŋul lueyeete komonyete akiyo,
Esakete kailel.
6Karaida elosi korotoke komonyi akiyo,
Kitiŋite ikinyom luyean;
Eboŋuni kailel, eyaŋauni da iisutanake.

1   Paul, ka Siluano, ka Timoteo, Mama ejai ekanisa loka Isesalonikan lokotoma Edeke Papawok ka Ejakait Yesu Kristo: 

2   Asianut kojai mama ijaas ka ainapakin nuepote kama ejai Edeke Papa ka Ejakait Yesu Kristo. 

3   Iinacan, kiburao sio aimalaenen Edeke apaarasia kere kanukus, kwape kibusakinitor, naarai iyata noi akusiiyuun, idila da akusiminanar ŋinituŋanan kayesi kere mama kayenice. 

4   Kiton sio da kiwolobobot kanukus kotoma okanisan luka Edeke kanuka atitiŋikinikus, ka aiyuun kotoma areokus kere, ka aiticanio naiŋirikinito. 

5   Ŋes aanyunet cut naka etube loedolit loka Edeke, kosodete kitubusikin yes ajakanut naka Edeke, nakiticanere yes kanuke. 

6   Naarai edolit kakonyen nuka Edeke atacakin aiticanio lukiticanete yes, 

7   yes da luiticanio kisiyeŋunio nepepe kasio, kotoma apukorio naka Ejakait Yesu naebunio kotoma akuj nepepe ka imalaikan luka akepedor, kotoma akim naesarani, 

8   inyaŋi kama kaŋul lumam ejenete Edeke, ka ŋul da lumam erimarito Akiro Nukajokak nuka Ejakaitiwok Yesu. 

9   Egelitikec ŋes amudiario naka ikar kere, enyounete kakonyen nuka Ejakait, ka aibuses naka agogoŋuke da, 

10   naebunio ŋesi kapaaran kaŋin, tetere itopoloorio kotoma kalukalaunakake, ka aumokinio da kotoma kaluiyuunito kere, naarai iyuunitos yesi akosiitejenun. 

11   Tape da kilipanakini yes apaarasia kere, tetere kitubusik yes Ewokodeke kanyariteke, kitodolik da kapedor ŋiniwomisioit kere nakajokis, ka aswam da naka aiyuun, 

12   tetere itopoloorio ekiror loka Ejakaitiwok Yesu kotomakus, yes da kotomakeŋ, kwape ejaar asianut naka Edekewok ka Ejakait Yesu Kristo.

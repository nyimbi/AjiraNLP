Loka Ekapolokiton lueruos; aruo naka Gitit. Esabuli loka Daudi.
1Wu EJAKAIT, EkosiJAKAIT,
Ejok ca ma noi ekonikiror kakwapisinei kere!
Lokibwaikini aibusesukon kuju kakuj.
2
Ibu kiticat agogoŋ kaituke nuka imukerun ka idwe luenakete, Kanuka lukasurupokon,
Tetere itapalaari yenikasurup ka ikenyanan.
3
Naasesenia eoŋ akonikuj, aswam naka ikonibokoro,
Elap ka aacer nukibwaikini ijo;
4
Inyo bo ituŋanan, tape jo ipodo ŋes?
Okoku loka ituŋanan da, tape jo ianyanari ŋes?
5Naarai ibu kosub ŋes nepetai arianakin ka Edeke,
Itanapit ŋes aboko naka aibuses ka ayoŋio.
6
Isurumit ŋes aswamisio nuka akaninikon;
Ibwaikinit iboro kere kwap kakejeneke:
7Amerekekin kere ka imoŋin,
Eebo, ka itiaŋ da luka ais;
8Akweny nuka apak, iboro da luka ecor,
Kere luitoritos orotin luka aŋololin.
9
Wu EJAKAIT, EkosiJAKAIT,
Ejok ca ma noi ekonikiror kakwapisinei kere!

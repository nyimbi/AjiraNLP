Ooindap Kamuktaindet
1Amegoonech, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, amegoonech telunateet, ago igoochi kaineng'ung',
amu rireeneng'ung' ak imandang'ung'.
2Amu nee si komwa pororyosyek kole,
“Mi ano Kamuktaindennywa nguno?”
3Miitei Kamuktaindennyo kipsengwet;
yaei ineendet kiy age tugul ne machei.
4 Po fedha ak thahabu kanamonikwak,
poisyetap eunegap piik.
5Tinyei kutusweek, ago ma twegu;
tinyei koonyek, ago ma keertoi.
6Tinyei itiik, ago ma kastoi;
tinyei seruunek, ago ma ing'utoi.
7Tinyei eunek, ago ma namei kiy;
tinyei keelyek, ago ma pendoti;
ama ng'alaale mookwekwak.
8Kergeitu icheeget che kiyaei ak tuguuchooto;
uu nooto age tugul ne imang'u chooto.
9Ee Israel, imang'u inyee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor;
ineendet toretindennywa ak long'ennywa.
10Ee kootap Aron, omang'u okweek Kamuktaindet ne Toroor;
ineendet toretindennywa ak long'ennywa.
11Okweek che oiywei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, omang'u Kamuktaindet ne Toroor,
ineendet toretindennywa ak long'ennywa.
12Kigobwaatech Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; iberuurech;
iberuuri kootap Israel,
iberuuri kootap Aron.
13 Iberuuri icheek che iywei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor,
che mengech ak che eech.
14Ingoyabak Kamuktaindet ne Toroor obaraitu,
okweek ak lagokwok.
15Oberuurotin eng' Kamuktaindet ne Toroor,
ne ki yai kipsengwet ak ng'wony.
16kipsengwet ko kipsengwet ne po Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.
ago ki igoochi ng'wony weeriigap piik.
17Ma ilasu Kamuktaindet ne Toroor che ki kobek,
anan age tugul ne choorchingei ye mi siisek.
18Ago kilasu acheek Kamuktaindet ne Toroor
kobooch nguno agoi koigeny.
Olasu Kamuktaindet ne Toroor!

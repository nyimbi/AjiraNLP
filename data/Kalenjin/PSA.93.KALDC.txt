Telunatetap Kamuktaindet
1Laitorani Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; ilaagat eng' telunateet;
Ilaagat Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, kigermenegei kimnateet:
Ki kigimit ng'wony, si ma ng'eigei.
2Ki kigimit ng'echereeng'ung' ne po laitornatet kobooch keny:
Ki imi kobooch keny agoi.
3Ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, tuldos ainoosyek,
Kigong'eet ainoosyek tuleennywa;
Ing'eeti maranosyek mildaennywa pogei.
4Kiim Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' parak,
Kosiir tuleetap peek che chaang', Kosiir mildaetapkei ne kiim ne po nyanjet.
5Lumdootin paornatosyeguk:
Nyoljin koong'ung' tiliilindo,
Ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, agoi koigeny.

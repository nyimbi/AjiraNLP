Mwaetap kongoi agobo katyagetap Israel
1Nda ki ma ko Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ne ki mi komostanyo,
ingomwa Israel nguno kole
2nda ki ma ko Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ne ki mi komostanyo,
ye king'eetyinech piik,
3ta ki koluguech kesaptosi,
ye kilaldos nereegekwak agobo acheek;
4kita imaluuchech peek,
kita roote aineet saboonwekyok;
5kita roote saboonwekyok peek che wasegei.
6Iberuurot Kamuktaindet ne Toroor,
ne ki ma igoitech kibutyi kelekwak.
7Chilildos saboonwekyok ko uu ye chilili toriityon eng' mesteetap kiplogotinik che parei toriitik;
kagong'et mesteet, ak kagechililse.
8Miitei toretennyo kainetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor,
ne ki yai kipsengwet ak ng'wony.

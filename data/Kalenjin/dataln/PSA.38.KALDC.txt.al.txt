1  Ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, amegera kong' eng' nereegeguk; ameneeta eng' nereegeguk che koroomen. 

2  Amu ki komwogo kipchaboniguk, ak irurunyo eung'ung' miising'. 

3  Ma kosabei poortanyu amu nereegeguk; ama komi kamuung'et kawekyuk amu tengektonyu. 

4  Amu kagomaluucha tengekwogikyuk; kagotemena ko uu mileet ne nyigiis. 

5  Kagosamisitu mookyuk ak kogonunio, amu abusnatennyu. 

6  Kaarigage ak ang'uruuksei miising'; ariiroti peet komugul. 

7  Amu nyiitaat ng'wanindo suennyu; ama kosabei poortanyu. 

8  Kanyaratitu ak kagonyanya poortanyu; atigeni amu nyalildap muguleldanyu. 

9  Ee Kiptaiyat, mi taing'ung' magennyu tugul; ama ung'aat tigenennyu eng' inyee. 

10  Rukrugeni muguleldanyu, kogobetwa kimnateennyu; kogomanda agine lapkeiyet eng' konyekyuk. 

11  Tonondos eng' ole loo che chama ak choronokyuk si ma riikyi nyalildanyu; ak tonondos eng' ole loo tilyonutikyuk ak icheek. 

12  Chobei mestonok kole konama icheeget che cheeng'ei saboondanyu; mwaei yaitwogik icheeget che cheeng'ei koyaiywo ne ya, ak kobwaatoti ng'aalsosyek peet komugul. 

13  Ago aa uu chii ne miing'at, ne ma kastoi, aa uu ne namaat kuut, ne ma twegu. 

14  Ee, aa uu chiito ne ma kastoi, ne ma tinyei ng'olyoo ne saruunegei. 

15  Amu amang'un inyee, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; inyee ne iwolu, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindennyu. 

16  Asaae ale, si ma poiboitunena; ye kagochabaita keldanyu, kolasunenogei. 

17  Amu neegit aterteryot, ak mi aragenennyu tainnyu kotugul. 

18  Ang'aanye tengektonyu; aarageni amu chalwoktanyu. 

19  Saptos komyee puunikyuk, ak ko kiimeen; ak kagotesagis icheek che wecho puch. 

20  Kaek puunikyuk che yaaktoi yaityet eng' ne myee, amu asubi kiit ne myee. 

21  Mebagaakta, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; ee Kamuktaindennyu, amelooitena. 

22  Chogu toreto, ee Kiptaiyat, yetunennyu.

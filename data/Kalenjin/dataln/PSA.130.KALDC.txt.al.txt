1  Ki ariirchin eng' rirosyek, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 

2  Kiptaiyat, kas kutinnyu! Ingoepchi iit iting'ung' kutitap soomutikyuk. 

3  Ngot iit inyee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, tengekwogik, ko ng'oo ne imuuchi kotonon, ee Kiptaiyat? 

4  Ago miitei nyoetap kaat eng' inyee, si kobiit kiywein. 

5  Ageni Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, igenisyei saboondanyu, ak amang'u ng'olyoondennyi. 

6  Igeni saboondanyu Kiptaiyat, kosiir riibiik che igeni egonet; ee, kosiir riibiik che igeni egonet. 

7  Ee Israel, imang'u Kamuktaindet ne Toroor; amu mi rireenet eng' Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ak mi katyaget ne siirei eng' ineendet. 

8   Ak kityaachi ineendet Israel eng' tengekwogikyik tugul.

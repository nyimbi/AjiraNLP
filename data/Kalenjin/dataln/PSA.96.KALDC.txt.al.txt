1  A.Pet 

16  :

23  -

33  ) 

1  Otyenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor tyendo ne leel: Otyenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ng'wony komugul. 

2  Otyenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, oberuur kainennyi; Obarte yetunennyi eng' peetuut agoi peetuut. 

3  Omwaite toroornatennyi eng' pororyosyek, Ak poisyonikyik che kikwong'ee eng' piik tugul. 

4  Amu oo Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, nyolu kilas ineendet miising': Kanyool kiywei ineendet kosiir kamuktaik tugul. 

5  Amu kamuktaik tugul che po piik koma tilei kiy: Ago kiyaei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kipsengwet. 

6  Mi tainnyi teegisto ak etunateet: Mi koonnyi ne tiliil kimnateet ak korooronindo. 

7   Ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, okweek tilyonutigap piik, Ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor toroornatet ak kimnateet. 

8  Ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor toroornatet ne nyoljin kainennyi: Oibu kagoochinet, ak obwa seremusyekyik. 

9  Ogutung'ji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' karaaranindap tiliilindo: Potan eng' tainnyi, ng'wony komugul. 

10  Omwa eng' pororyosyek ole, Laitorani Kamuktaindet ne Toroor: Ki kigimit ng'wony si ma ng'eigei: Irwookyin piik eng' imanda. 

11  Ingogaskei kipsengwet, ak ingobaibait ng'wony; Ingomoror nyanjet, ak tuguuk che nyiitaat olooto; 

12  Ingogaskei mbareenik, kobooto tugul che mi olooto; Si kotyenso anyun eng' poiboiyet keetiik tugul che mi timiin; 

13  Eng' taitap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, amu nyoonei: Amu nyoonei korwookyin ng'wony, Irwookyin ng'wony eng' imanda, Ak korwookyin piik eng' imandanyi.

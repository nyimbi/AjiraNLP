Tyendap kamang'unet eng' siisinetapkei
1Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ma meenei muguleldanyu;
ama mi katorooretapkei eng' konyekyuk;
ama yae tuguuk che eecheen.
Ma alosyige tuguuk che eechen
anan che kikwong'ee miising'.
2Ki abirge iman ak agoochi saboondanyu kosiis;
ko uu lakweet ne kaluge kamet,
uu saboondanyu lakweet ne kagiluge.
3Ee Israel, imang'u Kamuktaindet ne Toroor
kobooch nguno agoi koigeny.

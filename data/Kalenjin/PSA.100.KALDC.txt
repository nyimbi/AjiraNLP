Otyenji Kamuktaindet
1Otyenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' kutitap poiboiyet, okweek emotinwek tugul.
2Oboisyechi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' kagasetapkei;
obwa tainnyi otyenonu.
3Onai ole Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ko Kamuktaindet.
Ineendet ne kiyaech, ak ki chechiik;
ki piikyik, ak ki keechiiregap akweennyi.
4Oba kurgotikyik kobooto mwaetap kongoi,
ak obe seremusyekyik olasunoti.
Oweekyi kongoi ineendet, ak oberuur kainennyi.
5 Amu myee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor;
tebyei rireenennyi koigeny,
ak imandanyi eng' ibinwek tugul.

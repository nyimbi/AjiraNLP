Kalasunet eng' kemoi
1Obwa, olasu Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, okweek kiboitinigap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor,
che tonondos kemoi eng' kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.
2Otokyi eunekwok ye mi tiliilindo,
ak olasu Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.
3Ingoberuurin Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' Zion;
ineendet ne ki yai kipsengwet ak ng'wony.

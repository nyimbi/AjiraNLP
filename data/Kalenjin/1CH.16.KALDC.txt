1Ki ip anyun moingetap Kamuktaindet ak konde kwenutap emait ne kiga teekyi Daudi ak ki igoito kagoochinoik che kibeelei ak kagoochinoigap kaalyeet eng' taitap Kamuktaindet. 2Kingo kagogees Daudi kogoitoetap kagoochinet ne kibeelei ak kagoochinoigap kaalyeet, koberuur piik eng' kainetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 3Yeityo kogoochi chii age tugul ne po Israel, ne muren ak kwony, magatyat, pendo kitigin ak magatyatap sabibuk che yamyotiin.
4Ki lewen alak eng' Lawiik kobois eng' taitap moingetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, koyai kabwaatet, komwachin kongoi, ak kolasu Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindetap Israel; 5Chooto ko ki chu: ki ne oo Asaph, korubei Zekariah, ak Jeiel, Semiramot, Jehiel, Matitia, Eliap, Benaiah, Obed-edom ak Jeiel. Kibutei icheek kibugandinik ak ketubosyek; kibirei Asaph sanisyek che po sugunonik kocheiyo miising'; 6ak kiguutei kotugul iryagook tisiik Benaiah ak Jahasyel eng' taitap moingetap arorutyetap Kamuktaindet.
7Ki ng'at ne po tai anyun Daudi eng' peetuunooto Asaph ak tupchosyekyik kole kewekyi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kongoi.
Tyendap Daudi ne po kalasunet
(T.Kal 105:1-15; 96:1-13; 106:1,47-48)
8Oweekyi kongoi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, oguur kainennyi;
ongonaak poisyonikyik eng' piik tugul.
9Otyenji, otyenji kalasunoik;
ong'alaale poisyonikyik che kikwong'ee!
10Olasunegei kainennyi ne tiliil;
ingoboiboitu mugulelwegap icheek che cheeng'ei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.
11Ocheeng' Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ak kimnateennyi;
ocheeng' togeennyi kotugul.
12Obwaat poisyonikyik che kikwong'ee che ki koyai;
kwang'utikyik ak kirwoogutigap kutinnyi,
13ee okweek kesweegap Israel kiboityondennyi,
okweek lagogap Jakobo, chechiik che kigelewen.
14Ineendet ko Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindennyo;
miitei kirwoogutikyik ng'wony komugul.
15Ibwaate kayomisennyi koigeny,
ng'olyoot ne king'atyi ibinwek eliphuut.
16 Arorutyet ne ki yai kobooto Abraham,
ak kayosyennyi ne ki iyasyechi Isak.
17 Ki igimityi Jakobo nooto koek ng'atuutyet,
ki igimityi Israel koek kayomiset ne po koigeny,
18koleenji, “Agoonin emetap Kanaan,
koek kebebertap poroteng'ung'.”
19Ye kita ko oo piik che ng'ering',
ee ki oo ng'ering' miising', ak ki oo toek eng' olooto.
20Kibendat icheek kobunoot pororyet age agoi pororyet age,
kobooch laitornatet age agoi age.
21 Ki ma chamji chii koyachi icheek ne ya;
ee amu icheek, ko ki kergong' laitorinik:
22Kole, “Amat otwaa chechuuk che iilaatin,
amoyachi ne ya maotikyuk.”
23Otyenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ng'wony komugul;
omwaite yetunennyi eng' peetuut age tugul.
24Omwaite toroornatennyi eng' pororyosyek,
poisyonikyik che kikwong'ee eng' piik tugul.
25Amu oo Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, nyolu kilas ineendet miising';
kanyool kiywei ineendet kosiir kamuktaik tugul.
26Amu kamuktaik tugul che po piik koma tilei kiy;
ago kiyaei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kipsengwet.
27Mi tainnyi teegisto ak etunateet;
mi oldanyi kimnateet ak kagasetapkei.
28Ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, okweek tilyonutigap pororyosyek,
ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor toroornatet ak kimnateet.
29Ogoochi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor toroornatet ne nyoljin kainennyi;
Oibu kagoochinet, ak obwa tainnyi;
ogutung'ji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' karaaranindap tiliilindo.
30Potan eng' tainnyi, ng'wony komugul!
Ki kigimit ng'wony si ma ng'eigei.
31Ingogaskei kipsengwet, ingobaibait ng'wony!
Ingomwa eng' pororyosyek, kole “Laitorani Kamuktaindet ne Toroor!”
32Ingomoror nyanjet, ak tuguuk che nyiitaat olooto!
Ingogaskei mbareenik, kobooto tugul che mi olooto!
33Si kotyenso anyun eng' poiboiyet keetiik che mi timiin eng' taitap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor,
amu nyoonei ineendet korwookyin ng'wony.
34 Oweekyi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kongoi, amu myee ineendet;
amu tebyei rireenennyi koigeny.
35Omwa ole, “Sarwech, ee Kiptaiyat Kamuktaindetap yetunennyo,
iyumech ak isarwech kemong'u eng' pororyosyek,
si kobiit keeweekyi kongoi kaineng'ung' ne tiliil,
ak kigaaskei eng' kalasuneng'ung'.”
36Iberuurot Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindetap Israel,
kobooch koigeny agoi koigeny!
Ki mwa piik tugul kole, “Amen, ilasunoot Kamuktaindet ne Toroor!”
Saaet eng' Jerusalem
37Ki pagaach anyun Asaph ak tupchosyekyik eng' yooto, eng' taitap moingetap arorutyetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, si kobiit kobois eng' yooto peetuusyek tugul ko uu ye yamegei ak poisyetap peetuut age tugul. 38Ki pagaach kora Obed-edom kobooto tupchosyekwak, piik tamanwogik lo ak sisiit kobois eng' olooto; Obed-edom weeriitap Jeduthun ak Hosah ko ki riibiigap kurgeet. 39Ki pagaach Daudi Zadok tisiindet ak tupchosyekyik tisiik, eng' taitap kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' komosta ne toroor ne ki mi Gibeon, 40si kogoochin Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kagoochinoik che kibeelei eng' kaptisisyetap kagoochinet ne kibeelei kotugul, karon ak koskoleny, ko uu tugul che ki kisirchi Ng'atuutigap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, che king'atyi Israel. 41Ki mi kobooto icheek Herman ak Jeduthun ak che kiga ng'et tugul che ki kilewen, che ki kimwa eng' kainaikwak, koweekyi kongoi Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, “Amu tebyei rireenennyi koigeny.” 42Ki mi kobooto icheek Herman ak Jeduthun che ki tinyei iryagook ak sanisyek che po sugunonik che kibirei, che po icheek che pirei kocheiyo miising', ak tuguuk che kibutei che po tyenwogigap Kamuktaindet. Ki tebee weeriigap Jeduthun ormariich. 43 Ki pa piik tugul, ki wo chii tugul koonnyi, ak ki week Daudi agine kwo koberuur koonnyi.

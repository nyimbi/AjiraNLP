Laitornatetap Hezekiah
(2Lai 18:1-3)
1Ki tau Hezekiah kolaitorani ko po kenyiisyek tiptem ak muut; ak ki laitoran kenyiisyek tiptem ak sogool eng' Jerusalem. Ki kiguure kamennyi Abijah cheptap Zekariah. 2Ak ki yai ne myee eng' taitap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ko uu tugul che ki yai Daudi kwandanyi.
3Ki yaat kurgotigap kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, eng' kenyiit ne po tai ne po laitornatennyi, eng' arawet ne po tai, ak koteech kogeny ole kiga kobutok. 4Ki imut Tisiik ak Lawiik kobwa ko, ak koyumji icheek komosta ne paraa eng' kong'asiis, 5ak koleenji, “Ogas, okweek Lawiik; oanyinyge, ak oanyiny kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindetap kwanisyekyok, ak omaande tuguugap simdo eng' ole-tiliil. 6Amu ki kochiliil kwanisyekyok ak koyai ne ya eng' taitap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor Kamuktaindennyo, ak ki kometo ineendet ak kowelelgei kometo ole menyei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ak kotuktoochi patai. 7Kigoger kurgotigap keterut, ak komis taisyek, mata kobeel nyamdutik anan kogoito tiseiywek che kibeelei eng' ole-tiliil, kogoochi Kamuktaindetap Israel. 8Ki nyoonji anyun nereegegap `Kamuktaindet ne Toroor Judah ak Jerusalem, ak ki igoito icheek ketoortaat koba komosi ak komosi, koek kwang'utyet, ak koek che kireenyei ko uu ye ogeere eng' konyekwok. 9Kigebar kwanisyekyok eng' rotoogap chok, ak kigeluul weeriikyok ak tiibiikyok ak osotikyok amu niito. 10Ara anyun, mi muguleldanyu kiyomiske ak Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindetap Israel, si keistoenech nereegekyik che koroomen. 11Weeriikyuk, amata oegu che ma igiilegei, amu ki lewenok Kamuktaindet ne Toroor otonone tainnyi, oboisyechi ineendet ak oegu kiboitiinikyik, ak obeel nyamdutik.”
12Ki ng'eetyo anyun Lawiik, Mahath weeriitap Amashai, ak Joel weeriitap Azariah, eng' weeriigap Kohatik; ak eng' weeriigap Merari, Kish weeriitap Abdi, ak Azariah weeriitap Jehallelel; ak eng' Gershonik, Joah weeriitap Zimmah, ak Eden weeriitap Joah; 13ak eng' weeriigap Elizaphan, Shimri ak Jeuel; ak eng' weeriigap Asaph, Zekariah ak Mattaniah; 14ak eng' weeriigap Herman, Jehuel ak Shimei; ak eng' weeriigap Jeduthun, Semaiah ak Uzziel.
15Ki iyum tupchosyekwak ak koanyinygei, ak koba ko kotiliil kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ko uu ng'atuutyetap laitoryat, kosuubi ng'aleegap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 16Ki pa tisiik komosta ne po oriit miising' eng' kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, koba kotiliil, ak komaanda tugul che po simdo che kiganyoorchi Hekaluutap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, ak koip koba seremutap kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. Ki nam Lawiik ak koip koba timiin ak kowiirchi kererutap Kidron.
17Ki toi anyun koanyinyis eng' peetuut ne po tai ne po arawet ne po tai, ak ki pwa eng' peetuut ne po sisiit ne po arawet, keterutap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. Ki ianyiny kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor peetuusyek sisiit, ak ki iges eng' peetuutap taman ak lo eng' arawanooto po tai.
Kitabaani hekaluut
18Ki pa anyun ole mi Hezekiah laitoryat eng' ko ak koleenji, “Kagitiliil koot tugul ne po Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, kaptisisyet ne po kagoochinoik che kibeelei kobooto tereenikyik tugul, ak meesetap magatinigap kabartaet kobooto tuguukyik tugul. 19Kogechop ak ki anyiny tuguuk tugul che kiga wiirta Laitoryat Ahaz eng' laitornatennyi ye kiga kobistoe; keero, mi taitap kaptisisyetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.”
20Ki ng'eet anyun Hezekiah laitoryat karon inne ak koyum pounigap nganaseet ak kotogosta kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 21Kiget kiruugik tisap, mengichik tisap, aaregap keechiir tisap ak kwesik tisap koek kagoochinetap tengekto ne po laitornatet ak ne po ole-tiliil ak ne po Judah. Ki ng'at `ineendet tisiichooto ko weeriigap Aron kogoito chooto eng' kaptisisyetap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 22Ki eeny anyun kiruugik, ak kotaach tisiik korotiik, ak koisee kaptisisyet; kieny mengichik ak koisee korotiik kaptisisyet; ak kieny aaregap keechiir ak koisee korotiik kaptisisyet. 23Kiget kwesigap kagoochinetap tengekto koriik taitap laitoryat ak tuiyet; ak ki igonoor eunekwak paragunnywa. 24Ki eeny anyun tisiik ak ki igoito korotiikwak eng' kaptisisyet koek kagoochinetap tengekto, si koyai kagaigaet agobo Israel tugul; ko uu ye ki ng'at laitoryat kole kigoito kagoochinet ne kibeelei ak kagoochinetap tengekto agobo Israel tugul.
25Ki inde Lawiik kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, kotinyei sanisyegap sugunonik che kibirei, kibugandinik ak ketubosyek, ko uu ng'atuutyetap Daudi, ak ne po Gad keerindetap laitoryat, ak maotyot Nathan; amu ki punu ng'atuutyonooto Kamuktaindet ne Toroor eng' maotikyik. 26Ara kitononso Lawiik kotinyei kibugandinigap Daudi, kotinyei tisiik iryagook. 27Ki ng'at Hezekiah kigoito kagoochinet ne kibeelei eng' kaptisisyet. Ak ki ngetoi kigoito kagoochinet ne kibeelei, ketoi tyendap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor agine, ak iryagook kobooto kibugandinigap Daudi laitoryatap Israel. 28Ki ing'uruukso tuiyet tugul, ak kotyenso tyeniik, ak koguut iryagook piik che kuutei; ki yai ko uu nooto agoi kogeskei kagoochinet ne ki kibeelei. 29Kingo kagogees kagoitoet tugul, kong'uruuk laitoryat kobooto tugul che ki mi ak ineendet ak kolas. 30Ki ng'at kogeny `Laitoryat Hezekiah ak `pounik, Lawiik kotyenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor kalasunoik eng' ng'aleegap Daudi ak che po Asaph keerindet. Ara kityen kalasunoik eng' kagasetapkei, ak ki ing'uruukso ak kolas.
31Yeityo kowol Hezekiah kole, “Amu kaotabaanjigei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, oriigu oibu tiseiywek ak kagoochinoigap kongoi kobwa kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.” Ki ip tuiyet tiseiywek ak kagoochinoigap kongoi; ak tugul che ki chamei eng' mugulelwekwak ko ki ip kagoochinoik che kibeelei. 32Ak ki ek koitetap kagoochinoik che kibeelei che kiget tuiyet, kiruugik tamanwogik tisap, mengichik pogol ak aaregap keechiir pogol aeng'; ki po chooto tugul kagoochinoik che kibeeljin Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 33Ki ek tuguuk che ki kitabaan eiik pogol lo ak keechiirek eliphuusyek somok. 34Ago king'ering' tisiik kot koma imuuch koeny kagoochinoik tugul che kibeelei; kotoret anyun Lawiik tupchosyekwak, kot kogeskei poisyonooto, ak agoi koanyinygei tisiik; amu ki lititen mugulelwegap Lawiik che ki chamei koanyinygei kosiir tisiik. 35Ak ki chaang' kogeny kagoochinoik che kibeelei, kobooto mwanigap kagoochinoigap kaalyeet, kobooto kagoochinoigap tuguuk che kieei che po kagoochinet age tugul ne kibeelei. Ki kitet anyun otisyet eng' kootap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor. 36Ak ki paibait Hezekiah, kobooto piik tugul, amu otisyonooto kiga chopchi `Kamuktaindet piik; amu kiyaak ng'olyoondonooto eng' yooto.

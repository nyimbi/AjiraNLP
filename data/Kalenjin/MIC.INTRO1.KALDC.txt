Ng'aleek che indou
Kitabuutap Mikah ko ng'alalee agobo maotyo ne ki salu Moresheth eng' emetap Judah. Ki imang'u Mikah kole neegit koityi aseenet Judah ne kergei ak ne ki maotenji `maotyot Amos Israel. Ngeweech kainet Mikah koleen “Ng'oo ne kergei ak Kamuktaindet ne Toroor.”
Ki maoten maotyot Mikah agobo katerteeret ne nyoonjin pororyetap Israel kobun pororyetap Assyria, ak ne ityin pororyetap Judah kobun pororyetap Babilon.
Ki iteechi `maotyot Mikah piik koweekyigei Kamuktaindet ne Toroor amu ngo ma yai ko uu nooto, konyooru kirwooget ak ngo riip kayomisutigap Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, konyooru kaberuret. Ki maotenji icheek kole tuun ko ng'etu ng'etuunenik che kileliitee Israel kogeny.
Teteetap Kaneetutik
Kirwooget ne ityin Israel ak Judah 1:1—3:12
Kaweegunet ak kaalyeet 4:1—5:15
Ng'olyoo agobo kateunet ak kamang'unet 6:1—7:20

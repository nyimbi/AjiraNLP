Soomet agobo rireenet
1Anyalin,
ee inyee ne tebee kipsengwet.
2Ogeero, ko uu ye keerei otwogik eutap kiptaiyannywa,
ko uu ye keerei chepto eutap chebyoset ne pou ineendet;
ko uu nooto keerei konyekyok Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, Kamuktaindennyo,
agoi korirenech.
3Rirenech, ee Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, rirenech;
amu kigonyiiech sasisyet miising'.
4Kigonyi saboonwekyok miising'
chotisyetap icheeget che imuunyi,
ak eng' sasisyetap menotik.

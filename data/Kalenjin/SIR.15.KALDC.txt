1Age tugul ne iywei Kiptaiyat ko yaei niito,
ak age tugul ne name ng'atuutyet ko nyooru utaatyet.
2Nyoonei ng'omnateet ko taach ineendet ko uu kamet,
ak ko uu murereet ne ming'in kotaachei `inee ineendet.
3Tuun paei `inee ineendet kobooto magatyatap kaneetisyet,
ak kogoochi ineendet peegap utaatyet koe.
4Tuun tiegei inee eng' ineendet ak ma ibuti,
ak ko moong' `inee ineendet ak ma koi kigoochi kolilaan.
5Ieetu ineendet kosiir che po kokwennyi,
ak yaatei kutinnyi eng' kwenutap tuiyet.
6Tuun nyooru poiboiyet ak kuutweetap poiboiyet,
ak koondi kainet ne po koigeny.
7Ma nyooru ineendet `abusanik,
ak ma keerei ineendet `kiptengeginik.
8Loo inee eng' menoteet,
ak ma koi ibwaat kiplembechonik agobo ineendet.
9Uui komong' kalasunet eng' kutitap kiptengekyon,
amu tomo kiyook nooto kobun Kiptaiyat.
10Kanyool ketyen tyenwogigap kalasunet eng' utaatyet,
ak yaei Kiptaiyat nooto koberurok.
11 Memwa ile, “Kiabut amu Kiptaiyat;”
amu ma imuuchi inee koyai nooto wechei inee.
12Memwa ile, “Inee ne ki koono amasta;”
amu ma makyingei inee toretetap kiptengeginik.
13Wechei Kiptaiyat tugul che kiwechei,
agot che iywei ineendet ko wechei tuguuchooto.
14Inee ne ki yai chiito eng' taunet,
ak ko metechi icheek kamugetap katyaknatetap lewenennywa.
15Ngot imache, imuuche iriip ng'atuutik,
ak iboisyee imanit ko ng'olyoo ne po leweneng'ung'.
16Ki konde taing'ung' maat ak peek,
chuul eung'ung' itokyi nooto ileweni.
17Eng' taitap chii age tugul komi saboondo ak meet;
ak ne ka lewen chii, kigoochin.
18Utaatyetap Kiptaiyat ko oo;
kiim inee miising' ak keere tuguuk tugul.
19Riiboti konyekyik tugul che iywei ineendet,
ak kongen poisyet age tugul ne po chii.
20Ma ng'at `inee chii koyai yaityo,
ama chamji chii koyai tengek.

Saaet agobo riipseet
Tyendap Kalasunet ne po Daudi.
1Riibo, ee Kamuktaindet;
amu amang'un.
2Kaaleenji Kamuktaindet ne Toroor, “Ii Kiptaiyandennyu;
ma tinye myeein age kobatenen inyee.”
3Ago eng' che tiliileen che mi ng'wony, chooto ko che tagunoot,
che mi poiboiyennyu tugul eng' icheek.
4Icheeget che leweni kamuktain age ko tesoksei nyalilwogikwak;
ma tumdoi anee kagoochinoikwak che kieei che po korotiik,
anan amwa kainaikwak eng' kutinnyu.
5Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ko kebebertap porotennyu ak ne po kigombennyu;
iname parbaryandennyu.
6Kagokwaiywa komoswek che myachen;
ee, atinye poroteet ne myee.
7Alasu Kamuktaindet ne Toroor ne ki ing'omita;
ee, ineeta muguleldanyu agot kemoi.
8 Kande Kamuktaindet ne Toroor tainnyu peetuusyek tugul;
amu mi ineendet eunnyu ne po tai, ma ki istaa.
9Paibai muguleldanyu amu nooto, ak kogasegei saboondanyu;
tebyei poortanyu agine eng' chametapkei.
10 Memetoi saboondanyu eng' ye mi meet;
amechamjini kogeer neng'ung' ne tiliil nunet.
11Iboorwo ooreetap saboondo;
mi kagasetapkei taing'ung' ne nyiitaat,
mi eung'ung' ne po tai che kechamei agoi koigeny.

Ng'aleek che Indou
Kitabuuni, ko atindyot agobo kabelisyet ne kinyoor Yahudik kobun kwony ne kiguree Judith. Ki mi pororyeetap Yahudik ole ng'oi amu kimnateet ne po lugeetap Holofernes ak mageet ne ki tinyei konde emet komugul laitornatetap Nebukanezzar. Ki machei kora koisto kayanosyegap kamuktaik alak ip kong'et kayanetap Nebukanezzar. Ki kagigikyi ole mariich pororyeetap Yahudik ne ki neegit kogoongei. Ki ichergei kasarata kwony agenge mosok ne ki kiguuree Judith ne ki tinyei itoon ne karaaran. Ki ng'oom ak ko pekyinoot. Ki cheer pororyetap Yahudik konyiganitu eng' taitap lugeetap Assyria.

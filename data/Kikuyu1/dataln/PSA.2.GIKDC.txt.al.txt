1   Bata wa mabũrũri kũũngana hithoinĩ, na ndũũrĩrĩ gũthugunda mĩbango ya tũhũ nĩ ũrĩkũ? 

2  Athamaki aao-rĩ, nĩmaremeete, nao aathani aao nĩmacookanĩĩtie ndundu maremere MWATHANI o na mũthamaki ũrĩa aitĩrĩirie maguta akamũthuura. Marerana atĩrĩ, 

3  “Nĩtwĩyohoreei kuuma wathaniinĩ wao, namo matamu maao tũmateanie matweherere.” 

4  MWATHANI arĩ o igũrũ, gĩtĩinĩ gĩake kĩa ũnene, no gũtheka aratheka, na akanyũrũria mĩbango ĩyo yao mĩhinyaru. 

5  Agacooka akamakaania araakarĩĩte, o na akameekĩra guoya na ang'ũrĩkĩĩte akameera atĩrĩ, 

6  “Nĩhandĩĩte mũthamaki wakwa igũrũ wa kĩrĩma gĩakwa gĩtheru gĩa Zayuni.” 

7   Naake mũthamaki akoiga atĩrĩ, “Nĩngwanĩrĩra itua rĩa MWATHANI. MWATHANI aanjĩĩrire atĩrĩ, ‘Wee nĩ we mũũriũ wakwa, ũũmũũthĩ nĩndatuĩka thooguo. 

8  Hooya, na nĩngũkũhe mabũrũri moothe matuĩke ũgai waku; thĩ yothe ĩtuĩke yaku kĩũmbe. 

9   Wee nĩũkaamoinanga na njũgũma ya cuuma; o na ũmamemende makuange ta nyũngũ.’ ” 

10  Tondũ ũcio kĩũhĩgeei, inyuĩ athamaki aya; inyuĩ aathani a thĩ iguai ndũmĩrĩri ĩno. 

11  Tungatĩraai MWATHANI mũkĩmũtĩĩaga; 

12  mũinamĩrĩreei mũkĩinainaga; kana ahiũkĩrwo nĩ maraakara, na inyuĩ mũgĩkue o narua. Kũraathimwo-rĩ, nĩ arĩa oothe mamenyagĩrĩrwo nĩwe.

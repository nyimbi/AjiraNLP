1  Rĩrĩa MWATHANI aatũcookirie Jerusalemu, tuonaga ta arĩ kũroota twarootaga! 

2  Hĩndĩ ĩyo tũgĩtheka na tũkĩina nyĩmbo tũkeneete, nacio ndũũrĩrĩ ikĩĩrana atĩrĩ, “MWATHANI nĩekĩĩte andũ aake maũndũ manene!” 

3  MWATHANI nĩatwĩkĩire maũndũ manene; kaĩ nĩtũkeneete-ĩ! 

4  Tũma tũgaacĩre o rĩngĩ, MWATHANI, o ta ũrĩa mbura ĩtũmaga njũũĩ iria ihũĩte ciyũre rĩngĩ. 

5  Arĩa mahandaga makĩrĩraga, marogetha makĩrũũhagia nĩgũkena. 

6  Arĩa moimagaraga makĩrĩraga makuuĩĩte mbegũ cia kũhanda, makaahũndũka makĩrũũhagia nĩgũkena, makuuĩĩte magetha maao.

Kũgaathĩrĩria Jerusalemu
1Nĩndĩrakenire manjĩĩra atĩrĩ,
“Nĩtũthiĩi nyũmbainĩ ya MWATHANI.”
2Atĩrĩĩrĩ, wee Jerusalemu,
rĩu tũrũngiĩ na thĩinĩ wa ihingo ciaku!
3Ĩ wee, Jerusalemu,
ndũrĩ itũũra rĩakĩĩtwo rĩnyiitithanĩĩtio wega!
4Gwaku nĩkuo mĩhĩrĩga yũkaga,
o yo mĩhĩrĩga yothe ya Isiraeli,
gũcookeria MWATHANI ngaatho o ta ũrĩa aamĩathire.
5Thĩinĩ waku wee Jerusalemu,
nĩkuo athamaki a Isiraeli maikaragĩra itĩ ciao cia ũthamaki
nĩguo matuithanie andũ aao.
6Hoeraai Jerusalemu thaayũ; ugaai atĩrĩ,
“Arĩa meendeete Jerusalemu marogaacĩra.
7Kũrĩa thingo ciaku irigicĩirie kũrogĩa na thaayũ,
na thĩinĩ wa nyũmba ciaku cia ũthamaki kũroikara kũhooreire.”
8Tondũ wa arĩa twĩtainwo nao o na araata,
wee Jerusalemu, ndakũhoera thaayũ.
9Tondũ wa nyũmba ya MWATHANI Ngai witũ,
ndakũhoera ũgaacĩru, wee Jerusalemu.

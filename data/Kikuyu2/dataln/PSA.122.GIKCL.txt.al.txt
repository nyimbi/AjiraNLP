1  Nĩndĩrakenire manjĩĩra atĩrĩ, “Nĩtũthiĩi nyũmbainĩ ya MWATHANI.” 

2  Atĩrĩĩrĩ, wee Jerusalemu, rĩu tũrũngiĩ na thĩinĩ wa ihingo ciaku! 

3  Ĩ wee, Jerusalemu, ndũrĩ itũũra rĩakĩĩtwo rĩnyiitithanĩĩtio wega! 

4  Gwaku nĩkuo mĩhĩrĩga yũkaga, o yo mĩhĩrĩga yothe ya Isiraeli, gũcookeria MWATHANI ngaatho o ta ũrĩa aamĩathire. 

5  Thĩinĩ waku wee Jerusalemu, nĩkuo athamaki a Isiraeli maikaragĩra itĩ ciao cia ũthamaki nĩguo matuithanie andũ aao. 

6  Hoeraai Jerusalemu thaayũ; ugaai atĩrĩ, “Arĩa meendeete Jerusalemu marogaacĩra. 

7  Kũrĩa thingo ciaku irigicĩirie kũrogĩa na thaayũ, na thĩinĩ wa nyũmba ciaku cia ũthamaki kũroikara kũhooreire.” 

8  Tondũ wa arĩa twĩtainwo nao o na araata, wee Jerusalemu, ndakũhoera thaayũ. 

9  Tondũ wa nyũmba ya MWATHANI Ngai witũ, ndakũhoera ũgaacĩru, wee Jerusalemu.
